DIRECTIONS : Adults and children 2 years of age and older : Apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
Children under 2 years of age : consult a physician .
A passing burning sensation may occur upon application , but generally disappears in several days .
Application schedules of less than 3 to 4 times a day may not provide optimum pain relief ant the burning sensation may persist .
WASH HANDS WITH SOAP AND WATER AFTER APPLYING UNLESS TREATING HANDS .
WARNING : For external use only .
Avoid contact with eyes and mucus membranes .

Do not apply to wounds , damage or broken ( open ) , irritated skin or excessive irritation develops .
Do not bandage tightly .
Do not use with heating pad .
As with any drug , if you are pregnant or nursing a baby , seek the advice of a health professional before using this product .
Keep this and all drugs out of the reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek professional assistance or contact a poison control center immediately .
