Warnings For external use only .
Use only as directed .
Ask doctor before use • for prolonged periods • if condition covers a large area of the body .
• around the rectum or in the genital area or groin • with other forms of psoriasis therapy such as ultraviolet radiation or prescription drugs • do not use for prolonged periods without consulting a physician When using this product • avoid contact with the eyes .
