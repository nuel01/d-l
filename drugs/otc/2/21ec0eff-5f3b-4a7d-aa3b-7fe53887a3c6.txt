Drug Facts Active Ingredients Triclosan 0 . 3 % Purpose Health - care personnel handwash Uses For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin Warnings For external use only .
When using this product • Keep out of reach of children • Avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush with water .
• If irritation or redness develops , discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor .
• If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • Wet hands with water .
• Add soap and vigorously rub together all surfaces of lathered hands for at least 10 seconds .
• Thoroughly rinse under a stream of water .
• Dry thoroughly .
Other information Store at room temperature .
Keep from freezing .
