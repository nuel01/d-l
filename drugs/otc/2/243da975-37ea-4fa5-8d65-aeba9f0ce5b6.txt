Drug Facts Active Ingredient : Chloroxylenol ( 1 . 49 % w / w ) Purpose : Anti - Microbial Hand Cleanser Uses • Helps reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease • Helps prevent cross contamination by hand contact Warnings • For external use only .
• Keep out of reach of children .
• In case of eye contact flush with water for 15 minutes .
• If irritation persists , get medical attention .
• In case of accidental ingestion , seek medical attention or contact a poison control center immediately .
• This product must be dispensed from an adequate dispenser located a sufficient distance from processing line to prevent accidental product contamination .
• Do not contaminate water , food or feed by storage and disposal .
Directions • Apply cleaner to palm of hand • Lather and scrub hands ( including fingers , wrist , and cuticles ) vigorously for 60 seconds • Rinse with water until all cleaner is removed from hands • Dry completely ( incomplete drying may lead to chapped skin ) .
