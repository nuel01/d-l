Active Ingredients Methyl Salicylate 30 % Menthol 10 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Uses Temporarily relieves minor aches and pains associated with : • arthritis • simple backache • bursitis • tendonitis • muscle strains • muscle sprains • bruises • cramps Warnings For external use only .
If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use .
Allergy Alert : Do not use if allergic to salicylates ( including aspirin ) without consulting a physician .
When using this product • use only as directed • do not bandage tightly or use with a heating pad • avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes • do not apply to wounds or damaged skin Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days • redness is present • irritation develops Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
