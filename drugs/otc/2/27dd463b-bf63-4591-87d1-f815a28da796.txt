Active Ingredients Benzalkonium chloride 0 . 13 % .
Purpose Antiseptic Warnings For external use only .
When using the product • Keep out of eyes , in case of contact with eyes , flush thoroughly with water • Avoid contact with broken skin • Do not inhale or ingest Stop use and ask a doctor if skin irritation develops .
Uses • to decrease bacteria on the skin that could cause disease .
Directions • Wipe down surface thoroughly and allow to air - dry .
• For children under 6 , use only under adult supervision .
• Not recommended for infants .
Keep Out of Reach of Children .
• If swallowed , get medical help or contact a poison control center right away .
Other Information • do not store above 105 F .
