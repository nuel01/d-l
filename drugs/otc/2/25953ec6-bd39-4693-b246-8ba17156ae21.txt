When using this product • avoid contact with eyes .
Rinse with water if contact occurs .
Stop use and ask doctor if • if symptoms persist for more than 7 days .
Do not use in large quantities , particularly over raw surfaces or blistered areas .
Keep out of the reach of children Directions • Adults and children 2 years and older : apply to affected area not more than 3 - 4 times a day .
