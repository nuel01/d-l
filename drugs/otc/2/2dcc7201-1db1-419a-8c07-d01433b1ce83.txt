Active Ingredient Zinc Oxide ( CI 77947 ) 25 % Purpose Skin Protectant Uses : Treats diaper rash and skin irritations Warnings For external use only .
Do not use in the eyes or if you allergic to any of the ingredients .
Stop use and ask a doctor if you need to use longer than 1 week or condition worsens .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed get medical help or contact Poison Control Center right away .
Directions : Apply a thick coat after diaper change , especially at bedtime .
If baby cries during application it is because of their sensitive skin , this product does not sting .
Use soft cloth or paper towel to clean bottom .
Other Information : Do not store in conditions of excess humidity or heat beyond 42 ° C ( 107 ° F ) .
Do not use if seal under cap is missing or broken .
Inactive Ingredients : Bismuth Subgallate , Gossypium Herbaceum ( Cotton ) Seed Oil , Lanolin , Zea Mays ( Corn ) Starch .
