To help protect against infection in minor oral irritation .
Warnings Allergy Alert do not use this product if you have a history of allergy to local anesthetics such as procaine , butacaine , benzcaine or other " caine " anesthetics Do not use more than is directed for more than 7 days unless directed by a dentist or doctor Stop use and ask a doctor if sore mouth symptoms do not improve in 7 days swelling , rash or fever develops irritation , pain or redness persists or worsens Keep out of reach of children .
In case of overdose or allergic reaction , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
