Active Ingredient Triclosan 0 . 15 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses for handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product Avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor If irritation and redness develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • pump into hands • lather vigorously for at least 15 seconds • rinse and dry thoroughly .
