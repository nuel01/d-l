Drug Facts Active ingredient Ethyl alcohol 70 % Purpose First aid antiseptic Use First aid to help decrease the risk of infection in : • minor cuts • scrapes • burns Warnings For external use only Flammable Keep away from heat and flame .
Ask a doctor before use if you have • deep or puncture wounds , animal bites , or serious burns When using this product • do not get into eyes • do not apply over large areas of the body • do not use longer than 1 week unless directed by a doctor Stop use and ask a doctor if condition persists , worsens , or infection occurs .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • clean the affected area • apply a small amount of this product on the area 1 to 3 times daily • may be covered with a sterile bandage if desired • if bandaging , let dry first Other Information Do not store above 105 ° F Inactive ingredients Aloe barbadensis leaf juice , glycerin , isopropanol , propanediol , tocopheryl acetate , water Questions or Comments ?
