Active ingredient Benzalkonium chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antiseptic Handwash Uses • Hand sanitizing to decrease bacteria on skin • For use in food processing facilities Warnings For external use only .
Can cause eye irritation .
Avoid contact with eyes .
When using this product • Do not use in or around eyes .
If eye contact occurs , rinse well with water for at least 15 minutes .
Consult a physician .
• Do no swallow this product .
If swallowed , do not induce vomiting .
If individual is conscious , give large quantities of water to drink and consult a physician immediately .
Discontinue use if irritation and redness develops .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours , consult a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
