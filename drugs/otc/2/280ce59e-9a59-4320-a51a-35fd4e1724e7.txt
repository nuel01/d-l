Directions Clean the affected area .
Apply a small amount of this product ( an amount equal to the surface area of the tip of a finger ) on the area and rub gently using a cotton swab .
If not completely rubbed in after a 20 - second rub - in , too much product was used .
Repeat the process three times daily .
Keep this product refrigerated to preserve effectiveness and color .
Other information This product is an OTC antibiotic drug product for human use .
Blended for typical skin color .
