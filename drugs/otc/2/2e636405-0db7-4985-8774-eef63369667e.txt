Active ingredient Purpose Menthol 5 % ................................................................ Topical analgesic Uses Temporarily relieves minor pain associated with : • arthritis • simple backache • bursitis • tendonitis • muscle strains • muscle sprains • bruises • cramps Warnings For external use only When using this product • use only as directed • do not bandage tightly or use with a heating pad • avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes • do not apply to wounds or damaged skin Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days • redness is present • skin irritation develops Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use .
