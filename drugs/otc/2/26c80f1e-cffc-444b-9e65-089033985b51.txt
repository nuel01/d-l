Active ingredient Triclosan 0 . 5 % Purpose Antiseptic Handwash Uses For handwashing to decrease bacteria on skin .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product Do not use in the eyes .
In case of contact , immediately flush with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if Discontinue use if irritation and redness develop .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours , consult a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions FOR INSTITUTIONAL AND CANADIAN FOOD PLANT USE .
To decrease bacteria on skin , wet hands and apply a dose of FOAM SELECT soap .
Rub hands together to lather for 20 seconds , rinse with potable water and dry thoroughly .
Avoid contamination of food during use and storage .
