Drug Facts Active ingredient Povidone - iodine , 5 % ( 0 . 5 % available iodine ) .
Purpose Antiseptic Uses Patient pre - operative skin preparation • for preparation of the skin prior to surgery • helps reduce bacteria that potentially can cause skin infection Warnings For external use only Do not use • in the eyes • If you are allergic to povidone - iodine or any other ingredients in this preparation When using this product • prolonged exposure to wet solution may cause irritation or , rarely , severe skin reactions • in pre - operative prepping , avoid “ pooling ” beneath the patient Stop use and ask a doctor if • irritation , sensitization , or allergic reaction occurs and lasts for 72 hours .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
