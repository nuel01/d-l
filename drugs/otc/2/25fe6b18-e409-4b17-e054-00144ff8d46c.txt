Boxed Warning fetal toxicity When pregnancy is detected , discontinue losartan potassium tablets as soon as possible .
Drugs that act directly on the rennin - angiotensin system can cause injury and death to the developing fetus .
See WARNINGS , Fetal Toxicity .
