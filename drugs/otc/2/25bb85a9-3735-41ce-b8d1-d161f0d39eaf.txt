Active Ingredient Medicinal Ingredient : Benzocaine6 % , NON MEDICINAL : SD Alcohol 60 % .
Use For the temporary relief of pain and itching associated with minor burns , scrapes and insect bites Directions Apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
For adults and children 2 years of age and older .
Children under 2 years : consult a physician .
DO NOT USE IN EYES , broken skin , deep puncture wounds , or if unusual redness , swelling , irritation or other symptoms occur , consult a physician immediately Cautions FLAMMABLE . KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN .
FOR EXTERNAL USE ONLY .
AVOID CONTACT WITH EYES : if this happens , rinse thoroughly with water .
