Active Ingredient Triclosan Purpose Antiseptic Uses For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only When using this product Do not get into eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes throughly with water .
Keep out of reach of children .

Apply palmful to hands .
Scrub thoroughly for 30 seconds .
