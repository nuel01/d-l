Calcium Carbonate 750 mg Purpose Antacid Uses for the relief of : • acid indigestion • heartburn • sour stomach • upset stomach associated with these symptoms Warnings Do not use if you have ever had an allergic reaction to this product or any of its ingredients Ask a doctor or pharmacist before use if you are presently taking a prescription drug .
Antacids may interact with certian prescription drugs .
When using this product • do not take more than 5 chewable tablets in a 24 - hour period • do not use the maximum dosage of this product for more htan 2 weeks except under the advice and supervision of a physician .
• constipation may occur If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
