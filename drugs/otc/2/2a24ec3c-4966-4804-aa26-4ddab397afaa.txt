VAGISIL Anti - Itch Medicated Wipes Drug Facts Active ingredient Pramoxine hydrochloride 1 % ( w / w ) Purpose External analgesic Use temporarily relieves itching Warnings For external use only Avoid contact with eyes Stop use and ask doctor if condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days , or clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions adults and children 12 years and older Unfold towelette and gently pat or wipe external vaginal area from front to back .
Use each towelette only once and then throw away .
Apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
Do not flush .
