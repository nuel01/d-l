Drug Facts Active Ingredients Salicylic Acid ( 2 % ) Purpose Acne Treatment Use Treats Acne Warnings For external use only .
When using this product , skin irritation and dryness is more likely to occur if you use another topical acne medication at the same time .

Dispense product onto fingertips and massage over face .
