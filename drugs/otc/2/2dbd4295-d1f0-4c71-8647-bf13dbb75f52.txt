Active Ingredients and Purpose Colloidal Oatmeal 1 % ( Skin Protectant ) Uses To relieve symptoms associated with eczema , psoriasis , and severe dry , itchy and flaky skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use on wounds or damaged skin .
Avoid contact with eyes .
If symptoms persist for more than seven days , discontinue use and consult a physician .
If pregnant or breast feeding , contact a physician prior to use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , consult a physician .
Directions : Adults and children 12 years of age and older : Massage into affected skin .
Reapply up to 4 times daily .
Store at room temperature .
Children under 12 years of age : ask a doctor .
Inactive ingredients Beeswax ( organic ) , chamomile oil , cotton seed oil , eucalyptus oil , hemp extract ( THC - free ) , jojoba seed oil , peppermint oil , silver oxide , sorbic acid , stearic acid ( vegetable derived ) , zinc oxide .
