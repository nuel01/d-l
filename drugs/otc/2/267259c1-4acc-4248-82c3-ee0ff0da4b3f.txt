CAN CAUSE ANESTHETIC EFFECTS .
MAY CAUSE FROSTBITE .
Avoid breathing gas .
Store and use with adequate ventilation .
Keep oil and grease away .
Use only with equipment cleaned for oxygen and rated for cylinder pressure .
Do not get liquid in eyes , on skin or clothing .
Cylinder temperature should not exceed 52 degrees C ( 125 degrees F ) .
Open valve slowly .
Close valve after each use and when empty .
Use a back flow preventive device in the piping .
Use in accordance with the Material Safety Data Sheet ( MSDS ) .
FIRST AID : IF INHALED , remove to fresh air .
If not breathing , give artificial respiration .
If breathing is difficult , give oxygen .
Call a physician .
