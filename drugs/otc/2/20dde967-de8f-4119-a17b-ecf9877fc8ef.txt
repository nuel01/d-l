Active ingredient and purpose : Menthol 8 % ( Topical Analgesic ) Uses Temporarily relieves minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with simple backaches , arthritis , strains and bruises .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use on wounds or damaged skin and avoid contact with the eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , consult a physician .
Directions Adults & children 12 years of age and older .
Apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
Children under 12 years of age – consult a physician .
