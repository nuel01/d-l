Drug Facts Active ingredient Ethyl alcohol 70 % v / v Purpose Antimicrobial Uses • Hand Sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially cause disease .
• For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings Flammable • Keep away from fire or flame .
• For external use only .
When using this product • Do not use it in or near the eyes .
• In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if skin irritation occurs .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help in a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • Put enough product in your palm to cover hands and rub hands together briskly untill dry .
• Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
Other information • Store below 110 ° F ( 43 ° C ) • May discolor certain fabrics Inactive Ingredients Triethanolamine , Carbomer , Polysorbate , Water , Glycerin .
