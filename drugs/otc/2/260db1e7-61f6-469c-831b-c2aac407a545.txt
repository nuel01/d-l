Active Ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 1 % Purpose Antimicrobial Uses • For hand sanitizing to decrease bacteria on the skin • Recommended for repeated use Warnings For external use only .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush eyes with water .
Stop use an ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops , or if condition persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Pump a small amount of foam into palm of hand .
• Rub thoroughly over all surfaces of both hands .
• Rub hands together briskly until dry .
