Camphor 4 % , Menthol 4 % , Methyl Salicylate 11 % Uses Temporarily relieves minor muscle pain , joint pain .
Temporarily relieves pain associated with : arthritis pain , backache , strains , sprains , and bruises .
Warnings For external use only .
Use only as directed .
Do not use on wounds or damaged skin .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes , do not bandage tightly .
Stop use and consult doctor if condition worsens or symptoms persist for more than 7 days or symptoms clear up and occur again within a few days .
Directions Adults and children 12 years of age and older : use no more than 3 to 4 times daily .
Apply cream to affected areas and thoroughly massage until penetrated .
Wash hands with soap and water after use .
Inactive ingredients Acrylates C10 - 30 alkyl acrylate crosspolymer , Water , Caprylic / Capric Triglyceride , Cetearyl Alcohol , Ceteth - 10 Phosphate , Cyclomethicone , Dicetyl Phosphate , Glycerin , Helianthus Annuus ( Sunflower ) Seed Oil , Isopropyl Palmitate , Methylsulfonylmethane ( MSM ) , Phenoxyethanol , Triethanolamine .
Purpose Topical Analgesic .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
