Active ingredient ( in each chewable tablet ) Calcium carbonate 400 mg Purpose Antacid Uses relieves : • heartburn • acid indigestion • sour stomach • upset stomach associated with these symptoms Warnings Ask a doctor or pharmacist before use if the child is presently taking a prescription drug .
Antacids may interact with certain prescription drugs .
Stop use and ask a doctor if symptoms last more than two weeks .
Keep this and all drugs out of the reach of children .
