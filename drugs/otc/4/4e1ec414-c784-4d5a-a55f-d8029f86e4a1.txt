Drug Facts Ethyl Alcohol .............. 70 % Purpose Sanitizer Use • To decreases bacteria on the skin that could cause disease • Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings • For external use only - hands .
Use only as directed .
• Flammable , keep away fro heat and flame .
Excessive use or prolonged exposure may cause irritation to skin .
Discontinue use if rash , redness , or itching occurs .
When using this product • Keep out of eyes .
In case of contact with eyes , immediately flush with water and call a doctor • avoid contact with broken skin .
Other Information : • do not store in temperatures over 118 ℉ • children under 6 years of age should be supervised while using this product .
• may discolor certain fabrics .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek professional assistance or contact poison control Center immediately .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions put a thumb size amount into your palm and rub hands together until dry .
