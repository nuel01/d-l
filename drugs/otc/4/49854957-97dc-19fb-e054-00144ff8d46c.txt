Ilex Paraguariensis leaf ( Yerba Mate ) Extract , Mentha Piperita ( Peppermint ) Oil , Methylisothiazolinone ( MSM ) , Polysorbate - 20 , SD - Alcohol 40 B , Triethanolarmine Directions : Apply directly to affected area .
Do use more than four times per day .
Uses : For temporary relief of minor aches and pains of the muscles and joints associated with simple backache , arthritis , bruises , strains , and / or sprains .
