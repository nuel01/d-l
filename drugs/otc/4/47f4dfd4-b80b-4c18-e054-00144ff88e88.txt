Desert Harvest RELEVEUM Active Ingredients : Lidocaine , USP 4 % Purpose : External analgesic Uses : For the temporary relief of pain associated with minor burns .
Warnings : For external use only .
Do not use on open wounds .
Avoid contact with eyes .
Keep out of reach of children .
Stop use and ask a doctor if : • condition worsens • a rash or irritation develops Directions : Adults and children 2 years of age and older : Apply a thin , even later up to 4 times daily or as directed by a qualified healthcare practitioner .
Wash hands after use .
Children under 2 years of age and pregnant / breastfeeding women , consult a doctor .
