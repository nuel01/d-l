KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN Directions [ Directions ] 1 ) Clean and dry face .
2 ) Remove mask from package and peel off one side of the protective paper .
3 ) Place mask directly on skin , contouring it to fit the curves around the eyes , nose and mouth .
4 ) Remove the protective film from the outside of the mask .
5 ) Leave on for 10 - 20 minutes .
6 ) Remove mask and gently pat the face with fingertips until the remaining serum is fully absorbed .
* Suggested use is 2 - 3 times a week .
