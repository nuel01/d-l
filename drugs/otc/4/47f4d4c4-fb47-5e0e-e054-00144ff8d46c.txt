Drug Facts Active ingredients Methyl Salicylate 18 . 50 % Menthol 9 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Indications : For the temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with arthritis , simple backache , strains , bruises and sprains Warnings : • Always consult a physician before use .
• For external use only .
• Avoid contact with eyes .
• If symptoms persist for more than seven days , discontinue use and consult physician .
Keep out of reach of children .
• If swallowed , consult physician .
• Do not apply to wounds or damaged skin .
• Do not bandage tightly .
If pregnant or breast feeding , ask a health professional before use .
Allergy Alert : If you have had a prior allergic reaction to aspirin or salicylates , please consult physician prior to use .
Directions : • Adults and children two - years of age and older : Apply to affected area not more than three to four times daily .
• Children under two - years of age : consult a physician .
Other information : Store at room temperature .
