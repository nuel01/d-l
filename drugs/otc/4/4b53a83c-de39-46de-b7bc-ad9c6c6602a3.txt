Topical Analgesic Uses Temporarily relieves minor pain associated with : • arthritis • simple backache • strains • sprains • bruises Warnings • For external use only • Discontinue use at least 1 hour before a bath or shower and do not use immediately after a bath or shower .
• Allergy Alert : If you are allergic to any ingredients of this product or chili peppers contact a doctor before use Do not use • on wounds or damaged skin • with , or at the same time as other external analgesic products When using this product • use only as directed • do not bandage tightly or use with a heating pad • avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days • rash , itching or excessive skin irritation develops If pregnant or breastfeeding consult a healthcare professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swollen , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
