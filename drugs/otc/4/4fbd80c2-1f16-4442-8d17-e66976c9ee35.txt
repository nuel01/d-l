Drug Facts Active Ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 10 % Purpose Antibacterial ( helps reduce germs ) Uses Helps kill germs that can potentially cause infection .
Helps eliminate odor .
Reduces cross contamination .
Warnings For external use only .
Avoid contact with eyes .
Do not use on deep or punctured wounds , serious burns or animal bites .
If irritation or redness develops , and persists for more than 72 hours after use , stop use and seek medical help .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact poison control center immediately .
