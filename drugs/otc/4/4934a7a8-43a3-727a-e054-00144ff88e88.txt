Drug Facts Active ingredients : Tolnaftate 1 . 0 % Purpose Antifungal Uses • Proven clinically effective in the treatment of most athlete ' s foot ( Tinea pedis ) and ringworm ( Tinea corporis ) • Helps prevent most athlete ' s foot with daily use • For effective relief of itching , burning and cracking .
Warnings • For external use only Do not use on children under 2 years of age except under the advice and supervision of a doctor When using this product avoid contact with the eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if : • irritation occurs • There is no improvement within 4 weeks Keep out of reach of children .
• If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
