Topical analgesic Uses For temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with over - exertion , simple backache , arthritis , strains and sprains .
Warnings For external use only When using this product • Avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes • Do not apply to open wounds or damaged skin • Do not bandage or use heating pad Stop use and ask a doctor if • Condition worsens • Severe skin irritation occurs • Pain persists for more than seven days • Pain clears up and then recurs few days later If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a healthcare professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • Rub well on the affected area .
Repeat 3 to 4 times daily .
• For children 2 - 12 years , consult a doctor before use .
Other information This product may cause allergic reaction in some individuals .
Test on small area before use .
