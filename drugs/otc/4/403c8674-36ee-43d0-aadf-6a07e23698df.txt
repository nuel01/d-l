Active Ingredient Clotrimazole 1 % Purpose Antifungal Uses Cures most ringworm ( tinea cruris ) .
For effective relief of itching , redness , irritation and discomfort associated with ringworm .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use on children under 2 years of age unless directed by a physician .
Avoid contact with the eyes .
If irritation occurs or if there is no improvement within 4 weeks , discontinue use and consult a physician .
Directions Wash and dry affected skin .
Apply a thin layer of ointment over the affected area twice daily ( morning and night ) or as directed by a doctor .
Supervise children in the use of this product .
Store at room temperature .
May stain fabrics .
