Dietary Fiber Supplement Dietary Fiber Supplement ( continued ) Uses Psyllium Fiber is low in saturated fat and cholesterol that include 7 grams of soluble fiber which reduces the risk of Coronary Heart Diseases ( CHD ) by lowering cholesterol .
Directions Adults 12 years & older : Put one rounded tablespoon into an empty glass .
Mix this product with at least 8 ounces of liquid .
Taking Naturlax Fiber without enough liquid may cause choking .
See choking warning .
Stir quickly and drink promptly .
If mixture gets too thick add more liquid and stir briskly .
Fiber can be taken two times daily .
Children 6 years to under 12 years : 1 / 2 adult dose in 8 ounces of liquid .
Children under 6 years : Consult a doctor before using .
