Drug Facts Active ingredient Isopropyl alcohol , 95 % Purpose Ear drying aid Uses Dries water in the ears and relieves water - clogged ears after : • • swimming • • showering • • bathing • • washing the hair .
Warnings Flammable : Keep away from fire or flame .
Do not use in the eyes .
Ask a doctor before use if you have • • ear drainage or discharge • • pain , irritation or rash in ear • • had ear surgery • • dizziness • Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation ( too much burning ) or pain occurs .
• Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Apply 4 to 5 drops in each affected ear .
Inactive ingredient Anhydrous glycerin 5 % base .
