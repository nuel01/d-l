Active Ingredient Dimethicone 20 % Purpose Skin Protectant Uses • Helps treat and prevent diaper rash associated with incontinence - associated dermatitis • Protects minor skin irritation due to excess moisture , urine or stool • Helps seal out wetness .
Warnings Do not use on deep puncture wounds , serious burns or animal bites .
For external use only .
When using this product do not get into eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if condition worsens or symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a poison control center immediately .
Directions • Cleanse skin area and allow to dry .
• Shake bottle well before use .
• Spray 4 - 6 inches from skin .
No rub - in is required .
• Apply liberally as often as necessary .
