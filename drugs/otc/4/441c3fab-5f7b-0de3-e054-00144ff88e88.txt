Drug Facts Active ingredient : Tolnaftate 1 % Purpose Antifungal Uses • Proven clinically effective in the treatment of most athlete ' s foot ( tinea pedis ) , jock itch ( tinea cruris ) and ringworm ( tinea corporis ) • Helps prevent most athlete ' s foot with daily use • For effective relief of itching , burning and cracking Warnings • For external use only Do not use on children under 2 years of age except under the supervision of a doctor When using this product avoid contact with the eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if : • Irritation occurs • There is no improvement within 4 weeks Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away Directions • Wash affected area well , and let product sit for 1 - 2 minutes before rinsing .
• Make sure to dry affected area thoroughly .
• Supervise children in the use of this product • For athlete ' s foot : pay special attention to the spaces between the toes ; wear well - fitting , ventilated shoes and change shoes and socks at least once daily • Use 1 - 2 times daily .
• Use daily for 4 weeks ; if condition persists longer , ask a doctor • This product is not effective on the scalp or nails Inactive ingredients : Butylhydroxytoluene ( BHT ) , Carthamus Tinctorius ( Safflower ) Seed Oil , Oryza Sativa ( Rice ) Bran Oil , Prunus Armenicaca ( Apricot ) Kernel Oil , Sesamum Indicum ( Sesame ) Seed Oil , Simmondsia Chinensis ( Jojoba ) Seed Oil , Tocopheryl Acetate ( Vitamin E ) .
