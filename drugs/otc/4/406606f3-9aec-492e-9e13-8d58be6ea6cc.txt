Eye Lubricant Uses • For the temporary relief of burning , irritation , and discomfort due to dryness of the eye or exposure to wind or sun .
• May be used as a protectant against further irritation Warnings • For external use only .
• To avoid contamination , do not touch tip of container to any surface .
Replace cap after using .
Stop use and ask a doctor if you experience eye pain , changes in vision , continued redness or irritation of the eye , or if the condition worsens or persists more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
