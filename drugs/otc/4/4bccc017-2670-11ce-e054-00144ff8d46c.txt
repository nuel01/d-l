Sun protection measures .
Spending time in the sun increase your risk of skin cancer and early skin aging .
To decrease this risk , regularly use sunscreen with broad spectrum protection and an SPF rating of 15 or higher , along with other sun protection measures including : - Limit time in the sun , especially between 10 am and 2 pm .
- Wear long sleeve shirts , pants , hats and sunglasses .
- For childre under 6 - months , consult with a physician .
