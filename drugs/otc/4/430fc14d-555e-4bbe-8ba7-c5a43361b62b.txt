Active Ingredients Octinoxate 7 . 0 % Purpose sunscreen Uses • Helps prevent sunburn .
• Higher SPF gives more sunburn protection .
• Provides minimal protection against sunburn .
Warnings • For external use only .
• When using this product keep out of eyes .
Rinse with water to remove .
• Stop use and ask a doctor if rash or irritation develops and lasts .
• Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Apply liberally before sun exposure and as needed .
Other information • Sun alert : Limiting sun exposure , wearing protective clothing , and using sunscreen may reduce the risk of skin aging , skin cancer , and other harmful effects of the sun .
• Apply to the forehead , nose , chin and both cheeks .
Blend with circular motion and spread softly without stretching the skin .
