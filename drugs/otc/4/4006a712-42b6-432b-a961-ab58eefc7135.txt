Active ingredient Selenium Sulfide 1 % Purpose Anti - Dandruff / Anti - Seborrheic Dermatitis Uses For the relief of flaking and itching associated with dandruff and seborrheic dermatitis and to help prevent the chance of recurrence .
Warnings For external use only .
Ask a doctor if you have seborrheic dermatitis in areas other than the scalp .
When using this product • Avoid contact with eyes • if contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water • may stain color treated or permed hair , rinse thoroughly Stop use and ask a doctor if condition worsens or does not improve after regular use of this product as directed Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
