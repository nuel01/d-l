Active Ingredient Menthol 2 . 5 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Uses Temporarily relives minor pain associated with : • arthritis • simple backache • muscle strains • muscle sprains • bruises • cramps For external use only When using this product • Use only as directed • wash hand thoroughly with soap and water after use • do not bandage tightly or use with a heating pad • avoid contact with eyes or mucous membranes • do not apply to wounds or damaged skin Stop use and ask doctor if • Condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days • redness is present • irritation develops If pregnant or breast - feeding ask a health care professional before use .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a poison control center immediately .
