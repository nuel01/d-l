Acne Treatment Uses • for the treatment of acne • reduces the number of acne blemishes • clears up most acne blemishes Warnings For external use only When using this product • Skin irritation and dryness is more likely tooccur if you use other topical acne medications at the same time .
If irritation occurs , only use one topical medication at a time .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Sunburn Alert : Use only as directed .
This product contains an alpha hydroxy acid that may increase your skin ’ s sensitivity to the sun and particularly the possibility of sunburn .
Please limit sun exposure and apply a sunburn protectant while using this product and for a week afterwards .
Directions Wet face .
Squeeze product onto fingertips and then gently rub and smooth over face to activate warming sensation .
Leave on 5 - 10 minutes .
Rinse well with warm water .
Use 3 times per week .
