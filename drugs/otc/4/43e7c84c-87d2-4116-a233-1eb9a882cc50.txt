Drug Facts Active Ingredient Salicylic acid 2 % Purpose Acne Treatment Uses • For the treatment of acne • Clears acne blemishes and allows the skin to heal • Penetrates the pores to reduce acne blemishes , blackheads and whiteheads • Helps keep skin clear of new acne blemishes , blackheads and whiteheads • Helps prevent new acne blemishes from forming Warnings For external use only When using this product skin irritation and dryness is more likely to occur if you use another topical acne medication at the same time .
If irritation occurs , only use one topical acne medication at a time .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation occurs Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Centre right away .
Directions • Clean the skin thoroughly before applying this product • Cover the entire affected area with a thin layer one to three times daily • Because excessive drying of the skin may occur , start with one application daily , then gradually increase to two or three times daily if needed or as directed by a doctor • If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to once a day or every other day Inactive Ingredients Water ( Aqua ) , Alcohol Denat .
