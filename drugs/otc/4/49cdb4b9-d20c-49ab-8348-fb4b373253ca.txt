• this product generally produces a bowel movement within 12 to 72 hours .
Warnings - Do not use • if you are taking mineral oil , unless directed by a doctor • when abdominal pain , nausea , or vomiting are present • for more than 1 week , unless directed by a doctor Ask a doctor before use if you notice a sudden change in bowel habits that persists over a period of 2 weeks Stop use and ask a doctor if • you have rectal bleeding • you fail to have a bowel movement after use If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use .
