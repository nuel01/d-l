Active Ingredient Triclocarban 0 . 30 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses • Helps reduce bacteria on the skin that could cause disease .
• Recommended for repeated use Warnings For external use only When using this product : do not use in or near the eyes .
In case of eye contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if : irritation and redness develops and lasts more than 72 hours .
Keeps out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
