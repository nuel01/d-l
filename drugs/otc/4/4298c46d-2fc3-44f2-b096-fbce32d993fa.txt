Drug Facts Active Ingredients Purified water 99 % Purpose Eyewash Indication For flushing the eyes to help relieve irritation , discomfort , burning , stinging or itching by removing loose foreign material and air pollutants ( smog or pollen ) .
Warnings For external use only .
• Obtain immediate medical treatment for all open wounds in or near the eye • To avoid contamination , do not touch tip of container to any surface • Do not reuse • Discard vial after use Do not use if : • Solution changes color or becomes cloudy • With contact lenses • If vial is open or seal is broken .
Stop use and consult a doctor if you experience : • Eye pain • Changes in vision • Continued redness or irritation of the eye or if the condition worsens or persists .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
