Lidocaine HCl 0 . 50 % Uses Temporarily relief of pain and itching due to : sunburn , minor burns , insect bites , cuts , and scrapes .
Warnings For external use only .
Avoid contact with eyes .
If symptoms persist for more than 7 days , discontinue use and consult a physician .
Directions Adults and children 2 years of age and older : apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
Children under 2 years of age : consult a physician .
Inactive ingredients Aloe Vera Gel , Water , Propylene Glycol , Glycerin , Triethanolamine , Isopropyl Alcohol , Polysorbate , Carbomer , Diazolidinyl Urea , Menthol , Disodium EDTA , Yellow 5 , Blue 1 .
Purpose Pain Reliever .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
