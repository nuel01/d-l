Drug Facts Angelica sinensis 12 . 5 mg Use For treatment of loose skin , such as breast , face , and lower body .
Dosage Suggestion Adults 3 ml maximum per day Other Ingredients Vitex agnus , Astragalus chinensis , Cedarwood , Geranium , Sweet Cherry Oil , Walnut Oil , Jojoba Oil Directions Apply on skin and gentle massage around affected area .

Stop use and ask doctor if rash occurs .
Keep out reach of children .
