See your doctor .
● if you have kidney disease , except under the advice and supervision of a doctor ● with other acid reducers ASK A DOCTOR BEFORE USE IF YOU HAVE ● had heartburn over 3 months .

OTC - KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN In case of overdose , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
