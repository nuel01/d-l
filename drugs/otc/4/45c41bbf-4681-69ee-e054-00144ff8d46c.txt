DRUG FACTS Active Ingredients Menthol 4 . 00 % Capsaicin 0 . 035 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Indications : For the temporary relief of minor aches and pains of the muscles and joints associated with arthritis , simple backache , sprains , bruises and strains .
Warnings : • For external use only .
• Avoid contact with eyes .
• If symptoms persist for more than seven days , discontinue use and consult physician .
Keep out of reach of children .
• If swallowed , consult physician .
• Do not apply to wounds or damaged skin .
• Do not bandage tightly .
