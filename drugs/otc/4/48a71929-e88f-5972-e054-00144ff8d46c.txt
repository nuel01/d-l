Do not use for the treatment of diaper rash .
Consult a doctor .
When using this product , • avoid contact with the eyes • do not exceed the recommended daily dosage unless directed by a doctor • do not put into the rectum by using fingers or any mechanical device or applicator Stop use and ask a doctor if • bleeding occurs • condition worsen • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up occur again within a few days .
Do not begin use of any other hydrocortisone product unless you have consulted a doctor Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Centre right away Directions : • Adults : cleanse the affected area by patting or blotting with an appropriate cleansing wipe .
