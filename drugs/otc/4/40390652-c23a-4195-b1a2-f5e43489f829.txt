Warning • General Advice : show this safety data sheet to the doctor in the attendance .
• Eye contact : Hold eye open and rinse slowly and gently with water for 15 - 20 minutes .

• Skin Contact : Rinse skin with plenty of water .
If irritation persists , call a doctor .
• Inhalation : Move a fresh air .
If breathing problems develop , call a doctor .
• Ingestion : Drink a glassful of water .
Call a doctor or poison control center .
● If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Contrl Center right away .
Directions for use ; Instruction , Storage and Disposal Directions for use : It is a violation of Federal law to use this product in a manner inconsistent with labeling .
Not for cleaning or sanitizing skin .
Do not use as a diaper wipe or for personal cleansing .
Instructions : Before using , open the tab , then take the wipe and pass it through the hole of the bag .
wipe the surface and allow one minute contact time .
