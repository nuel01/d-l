Eye Lubricant Uses • For the temporary relief of burning , irritation , and discomfort due to dryness or the eye or exposure to wind or sun .
• May be used as a protectant against further irritation Warnings • For external use only .
• To avoid contamination , do not touch tip of container to any surface .
• Keep tube tightly closed .
Stop use and ask a doctor if : • You experience eye pain , changes in vision , continued redness or irritation of the eye .
• The condition worsens or persists for more than 72 hours .
.
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away at 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 .
Directions • Wash your hands .
• Pull down the lower lid of the affected eye and gently apply a small amount ( 1 / 4 ” ) of ointment to the inside of the eyelid .
