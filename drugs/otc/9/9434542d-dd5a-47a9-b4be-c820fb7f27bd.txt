Drug Facts Menthol - 6 % , Methyl Salicylate - 14 % Purpose : External analgesic Uses For the temporary relief of minor aches and pain of muscles and joints .
Directions Adults and children 2 years of age and older : Apply generously to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
Children under 2 years of age : Consult a doctor .
