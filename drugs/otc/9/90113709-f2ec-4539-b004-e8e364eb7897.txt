Active Ingredient Alcohol 75 % by volume Purpose Antiseptic Uses • For hand sanitizing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
• Apply topically to the skin to help prevent cross contamination .
• Not recommended for repeated use .

Warnings Flammable , Keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only .
Do not use in or contact the eyes .
Stop use and ask a doctor if too much skin irritation or sensitivity develops or increases .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
