In case of accidental overdose , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1221 ) .
Prompt medical attention is critical for adults as well as for children even if you do not notice any signs or symptoms .
WARNINGS Allergy alert : Acetaminophen may cause severe skin reactions .
Symptoms may include : • skin reddening • blisters • rash If a skin reaction occurs , stop use and seek medical help right away .
Liver warning : This product contains acetaminophen .
Severe liver damage may occur if you take • more than 6 caplets in 24 hours , which is the maximum daily amount • with other drugs containing acetaminophen • 3 or more alcoholic drinks every day while using this product Caffeine warning : The recommended dose of this product contains about as much caffeine as a cup of coffee .
Limit the use of caffeine - containing medications , foods , or beverages while taking this product because too much caffeine may cause nervousness , irritability , sleeplessness , and occasionally , rapid heartbeat .
