Drug Facts Active ingredients ( s ) Ethyl Alcohol 70 % v / v Purpose Antiseptic Use ( s ) Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
Do not use • on children less than 2 months of age • on open skin wounds When using this product keep out of eyes , ears and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask for a doctor if irritation occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Centre right away .
Directions • place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
