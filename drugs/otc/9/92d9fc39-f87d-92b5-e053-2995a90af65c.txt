ALLERGY ALERT Aspirin may cause a severe allergic reaction which may include : hives facial swelling asthma ( sneezing ) shock LIVER WARNING : This product contains acetaminophen .

The chance is higher if you : are age 60 or older ; have had stomach ulcers or bleeding problems take a blood - thinning ( anticoagulant ) or steroid drug take other drugs containing prescription or nonprescription NSAIDs ( aspirin , ibuprofen , naproxen , or others ) take more or for a longer time than directed CAFFEINE WARNING : The recommended dose of this product contains about as much caffeine as a cup of coffee .
