When using this product do not use more than directed Stop use and ask a doctor if • you get nervous , dizzy or sleepless • symptoms get worse or last more than 5 days ( children ) or 7 days ( adults ) • fever gets worse or lasts more than 3 days • redness or swelling is present • new symptoms occur • cough comes back , or occurs with rash or headache that lasts .

Keep out of reach of children .
