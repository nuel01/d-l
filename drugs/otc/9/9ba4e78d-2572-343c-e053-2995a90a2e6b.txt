Active Ingredient Zinc Oxide , 13 . 0 % Purpose Skin protectant Uses • helps treat and prevent diaper rash • protects chafed skin due to diaper rash and helps seal out wetness Warnings For external use only .
When using this product - do not get into eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if - condition worsens - symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
