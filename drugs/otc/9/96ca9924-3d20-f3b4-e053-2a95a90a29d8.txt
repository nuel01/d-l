Active Ingredients Sodium Fluoride USP 0 . 243 % w / w Purpose Anticavity toothpaste Uses Aids in the prevention of dental cavities .
Warnings Keep out of reach of children under 6 years of age .
If you accidentally swallow more than used for brushing , seek professional assistance or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Adults and children two years of age and older : brush teeth thoroughly , preferably after every meal or at least twice a day , or as directed by a dentist or physician .
• Instruct children under six years of age on good brushing and rinsing habits ( to minimize swallowing ) .
• Supervise children as necessary until capable of using without supervision .
• Children under two years of age : Consult a dentist or physician .
