In case of contact with eye in undiluted form , flush with clean water Do not use • in full strength When using this product • do not change dilution or use with other chemicals • do not reuse Stop use and ask a doctor ifyou have any of the following • changes in vision • eye pain • condition worsens or persists • continued redness or irritations of the eye Keep out of reach of children .
