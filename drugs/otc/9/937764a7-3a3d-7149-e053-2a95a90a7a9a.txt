Warnings For external use only Do not use • as a first aid antiseptic for more than 1 week • in the eyes • over large parts of the body Stop use and ask a doctor if Irritation or redness develop and persist for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .

Discard after use .
