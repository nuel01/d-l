Active ingredient ( in each tablet ) Calcium Carbonate 750 mg Purpose Antacid Uses relieves • heartburn • acid indigestion • sour stomach • upset stomach due to these symptoms Warnings Ask a doctor or pharmacist before use if you are taking a prescription drug .
Antacids may interact with certain prescription drugs .
When using this product • do not take more than 10 tablets for adults and 5 tablets for children in a 24 hour period .
Keep out of reach of children .
Directions • Adults : chew 2 tablets daily .
• Children : chew 1 tablet daily .
If symptoms persist , ask a doctor .
