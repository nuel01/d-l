Ask a doctor before use if you have : sensitive skin When using this product • Avoid contact with the eyes or mucous membranes • Do not apply to wounds or damaged skin • Do not use with other ointments , creams , sprays or liniments • Do not apply to irritated skin or if excessive irritation develops • Do not bandage • Wash hands after use with cool water • Do not use with heating pad or device Stop use and ask a doctor if : Condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days , or clear up and reoccur If pregnant or breastfeeding : Ask a health professional before use Keep out of the reach of children : If accidentally ingested , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
