Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol , 70 % Purpose Antiseptic hand wash Uses • for hand washing to decrease bacteria on the skin Warnings • For external use only • Flammable , keep away from fire or flame , heat , sparks and sources of static discharge .
Do not use • in eyes When using this product • if in eyes , rinse promptly and thoroughly with water • discontinue use if irritation and redness develop Stop use and ask a doctor if • Skin irritation or redness occurs for more than 72 hours Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • apply product onto hands , spread thoroughly and rub until dry .
