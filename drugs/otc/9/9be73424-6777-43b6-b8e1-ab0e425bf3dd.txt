Drug Facts Active ingredient Salicylic Acid ( 1 . 0 % ) Indications • For the treatment of acne .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • Do not use around eye area .
• If contact occurs , flush thoroughly with water .
• If irritation develops , discontinue use .
Consult a doctor if irritation persists .
When using this product • If dryness or irritation occurs when using other topical acne products at the same time , limit usage to one acne product .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Apply to cleansed face and throat twice daily .
• Avoid eye area .
