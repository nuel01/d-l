Drug Facts Active ingredients : Capsaicin 0 . 027 % Purpose : Topical Analgesic Indications : For the temporary relief of aches and pains of the muscles and joints associated with arthritis , backache , sprains , and strains .
Warnings : • For external use only .
• Avoid contact with eyes .
• If symptoms persist for more than seven days , consult physician .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , consult physician .
• Do not apply to wounds or damaged skin .
• Do not bandage tightly .
If pregnant or breast feeding , contact physician prior to use .
Directions : • For adults and children ten ( 10 ) - years of age or older : Massage into affected area not more than three to four times daily .
Wash hands thoroughly after each application .
Additional Information : Store at room temperature .
