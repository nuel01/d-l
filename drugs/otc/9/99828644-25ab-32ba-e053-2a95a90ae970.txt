Drug Facts Active ingredient Salicylic Acid 2 % Purpose Acne Treatment Use • for the treatment of acne • clears acne blemishes • helps prevent new acne blemishes Warnings For external use only .
When using this product Skin irritation and dryness is more likely to occur if you use another topical acne medication at the same time .
If irritation occurs , only use one topical acne medication at a time Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
