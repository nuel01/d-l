Warnings For external use only .
When using this product • • Avoid contact with the eyes • • Do not use in large quantities , particularly over raw surfaces or blistered areas • • Do not apply to wounds or damaged skin • • Do not bandage Stop use and ask a doctor if • • allergic reaction occurs • • condition worsens or does not improve within 7 days • • symptoms clear up and return within a few days • • redness , irritation , swelling , pain or other symptoms begin or increase Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
