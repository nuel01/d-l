ACTIVE INGREDIENT Salicylic acid 2 . 0 % w / w PURPOSE Acne medication USES • for the treatment and management of acne • reduces the number of acne pimples and blackheads and allows skin to heal • helps to prevent new acne pimples from forming WARNINGS For external use only .
Keep away from eyes , lips and other mucous membranes .
Allergy alert do not use this product if you have a known allergy to salicylic acid .
When using this product use of other topical acne medications at the same time or immediately following use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one medication should be used unless directed by a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
