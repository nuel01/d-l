Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 65 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses • hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin .
• recommended for repeated use Warnings • Flammable • Keep away from fire or flame • For external use only When using this product • avoid contact with eyes • in case of eye contact immediately flush eyes with water , call a doctor • avoid contact with broken skin Discontinue use if irritation or redness develops .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours , consult a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
Children should only use this product under adult supervision .
Do not drink .
Not edible .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek professional assistance or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Other Information • do not store above 105 F .
May discolor some fabrics • Harmful to wood finishes and plastics Directions • place enough product in your palm to thoroughly spread on both hands and rub into the skin until dry • recommended for repeated use .
