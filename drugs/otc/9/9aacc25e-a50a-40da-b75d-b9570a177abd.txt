Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antiseptic Use Antiseptic cleanser , helps decrease bacteria on skin .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product Avoid contact with the eyes ; in case of contact , flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops and persists Keep out of reach of children .
If injested , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away Directions • Lift tab and pull out towel to wipe hands thoroughly for 30 seconds .
Allow to air dry .
• Reseal after use to prevent moisture from evaporating .
• Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
Other information Store at room temperature .
