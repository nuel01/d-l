Active ingredient Water 99 . 8 % Purpose Emergency eyewash Uses • for flushing or irrigating the eye to reduce chances of severe injury caused by acid , alkali , or particulate contamination .
Warnings For external use only Do not use • if solution changes color or gets cloudy • with contact lenses • if twist - off top is broken or missing • in open wounds in or near the eyes When using this product • avoid contamination , do not touch tip of container to any surface When using this product • avoid contamination , do not touch tip of container to any surface Stop use and consult a doctor if you have • changes in vision • eye pain • continued redness or irritation of the eye , or if the condition worsens or persists Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
