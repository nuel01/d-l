Directions Directions ■ Start with a dry , cleansed face .
■ Remove from sachet , find the fold ( 1 / 3 the size of the rest of the mask ) and gently unfold it upward .
■ Hold the upper right side of the mask , and unfold it from right to left .
■ Unfold the right edge first and then the left edge .
■ Carefully place mask over the entire face , smoothing to fit the curves of the face and around eyes , nose , and mouth .
■ Leave on for 10 to 20 minutes , as desired .
■ Gently remove mask and lightly massage any remaining liquid into your skin , or wipe off .
Do not rinse .
■ Discard the mask after a single use .
