DRUG FACTS : Active Ingredient : Menthol 2 . 00 % Topical Analgesic Indications : For the temporary relief of minor aches and pains of the muscles and joints associated with arthritis , simple backache , sprains , bruises and strains .
Warnings : For external use only .
Avoid contact with eyes .
If symptoms persist for more than seven days , discontinue use and consult physician .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , consult physician .

Do not bandage tightly .
