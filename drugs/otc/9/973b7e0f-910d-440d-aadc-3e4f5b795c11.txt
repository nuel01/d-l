Drug Facts Active ingredient ( in each drop ) Pectin 2 . 8 mg Purpose Oral demulcent Uses For temporary relief of minor discomfort and protection of irritated areas in sore mouth and sore throat .
Warnings Sore throat warning : if sore throat is severe , persists for more than 2 days , is accompanied or followed by fever , headache , rash , swelling , nausea , or vomiting , consult a doctor promptly .
These may be serious .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • sore mouth does not improve in 7 days • irritation , pain or redness persists or worsens Keep out of reach of children .
Directions • adults and children 3 years of age and older : allow one drop to dissolve slowly in mouth .
