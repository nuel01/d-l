Active ingredient Hydrocortisone acetate 1 % Purpose Anti - itch Uses For the temporary relief of itching associated with minor skin irritations , inflammation and rashes due to eczema , insect bites , soaps , detergents and cosmetics .
Warnings For external use only Do not use • in or near the eyes • for the treatment of diaper rash : ask a doctor Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
