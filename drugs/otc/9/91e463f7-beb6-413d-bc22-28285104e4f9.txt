Skin Protectant Uses ● temporarily protects minor : ● cuts ● scrapes ● burns ● temporary protects and helps relieve chafed , chapped or cracked skin ● helps prevent and protect from the drying effects of wind and cold weather ● helps treat and prevent diaper rash ● protects chafed skin associated with diaper rash and helps protect from wetness Warnings For external use only .
When using this product ● do not get into eyes .
Stop use and ask a doctor if ● condition worsens ● symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days .
Do not use on ● deep or puncture wounds ● animal bites ● serious burns Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions ● apply as needed ● for diaper rash , change wet and soiled diapers promptly , cleanse the diaper area , and allow to dry .
Apply ointment liberally as often as necessary , with each diaper change , especially at bedtime or anytime when exposure to wet diapers may be prolonged .
