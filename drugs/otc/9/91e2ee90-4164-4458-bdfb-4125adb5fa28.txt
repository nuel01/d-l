Drug Facts Active ingredient Salicylic acid 3 % Purpose Psoriasis and seborrheic dermatitis treatment Uses controls and fights the recurrence of skin itching , redness , flaking and irritation associated with psoriasis and seborrheic dermatitis Warnings For external use only .
When using this product avoid contact with the eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if condition worsens or does not improve after regular use of this product .
Ask a doctor if the condition covers a large area of the body .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact Poison Control Center right away .
