Drug Facts ACTIVE INGREDIENT : Pyrithione Zinc 1 . 00 % PURPOSE Dandruff Treatment USES : For relief of flaking and itching associated with dandruff and seborrheic dermatitis and to help prevent chance of recurrence .
WARNINGS For external use only .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
If this happens , rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop and ask a doctor if condition worsens or does not improve after using this product as directed .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
DIRECTIONS : After shampooing , apply conditioner by gently massaging into hair and scalp .
Rinse thoroughly until water runs clear .
For best results , let conditioner sit for 3 - 5 minutes before rinsing thoroughly .
Use a least twice per week , or as directed by physician .
