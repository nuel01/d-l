Directions After shaving with a razor or clippers , apply SBR on the entire shaved area , exfoliate with finger tips for 5 minutes , then rinse well or remove with a white wet cloth .
In case of severe out - break of folliculitis , apply SBR , exfoliate and leave on overnight and remove with white wet cloth in the morning .
