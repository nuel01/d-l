Topical analgesic Uses Temporarily relieves minor pain associated with : • arthritis • simple backache • bursitis • tendonitis • muscle strains • bruises • cramps Warnings For external use only When using this product • use only as directed • do not bandage tightly or use with a heating pad • avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes • do not apply to wounds or damaged skin Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • redness is present • irritation develops • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of reach of children .
