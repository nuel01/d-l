Drug Facts Active ingredient : Sodium Fluoride - 0 . 22 % ( 0 . 1 % w / fluoride ion ) Purpose : Anticavity toothpaste Use : Helps protect against cavities .
Warnings : Keep out of the reach of children under 6 years of age .
If more than used for brushing is accidentally swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions : Adults & Children 6 years of age & older : Brush teeth thoroughly , preferably after each meal or at least twice a day , or as directed by a dentist or doctor .
Children 2 to 6 years : Use only a pea sized amount and supervise child ' s brushing and rinsing ( to minimize swallowing ) .
Children under 2 years : Ask a dentist or physician .
