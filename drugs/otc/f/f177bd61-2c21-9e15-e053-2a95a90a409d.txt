Lidocaine 0 . 5 % External Analgesic For the temporary relief of pain and itching associated with sunburn , minor burns , minor cuts , scrapes , insect bites , and minor skin irritations .
For external use only .
Flammable -- Keep away from fire or flame .
Do not use in large quantities , particularly over raw surfaces or blistered areas .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
In case of contact with eyes , flush thoroughly with water , do not apply to wounds or damaged skin , and do not bandage tightly , do not use with a heating pad .
Contents under pressure .
Do not puncture or incinerate .
Do not store at temperature above 120 F .
.
Stop use and ask a doctor if condition worsens , if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Shake well before use .
Adults and children 12 years of age and older , apply to the affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
Children under 12 years of age : ask a doctor .
