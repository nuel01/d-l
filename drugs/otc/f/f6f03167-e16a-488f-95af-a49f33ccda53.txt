Warnings For external use only .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Do not use on deep or puncture wounds , animal bites or serious burns Use and Directions First aid to help prevent infection in minor cuts , scrapes and burns .
Use on adults and children 2 years and older .
Use to clean minor cuts , scrapes and burns by thoroughly flushing the affected area .
Let air dry .
Cover with an adhesive bandage or sterile gauze .
Use no more than 2 times daily .
