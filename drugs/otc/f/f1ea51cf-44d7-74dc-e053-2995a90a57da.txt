Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame Do not use • in children less than 2 months of age • on open skin wounds When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .

Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash develops and continues for more than 72 hours .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
