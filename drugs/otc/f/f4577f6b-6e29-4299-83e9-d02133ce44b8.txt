NITROGEN , REFRIGERATED LIQUID NF UN1977 7727 - 37 - 9 REFRIGERATED LIQUID ALWAYS KEEP CONTAINER IN UPRIGHT POSITION .
DO NOT CHANGE OR FORCE FIT CONNECTIONS .
Rx Only WARNING : Administration of Nitrogen may be hazardous or contraindicated .
For use only by or under the supervision of a licensed practitioner who is experienced in the use and administration of Nitrogen and is familiar with the indications , effects , dosages , methods , and frequency and duration of administration , and with the hazards , contraindications and side effects and the precautions to be taken .
WARNING : EXTREMELY COLD LIQUID AND GAS UNDER PRESSURE .
CAN CAUSE RAPID SUFFOCATION .
CAN CAUSE SEVERE FROSTBITE .
Store and use with adequate ventilation .
Do not get liquid in eyes , on skin or clothing .
For liquid withdrawal , wear face shield and gloves .
Do not drop .
Use suitable hand truck for container movement .
Cylinder temperature should not exceed 52 ° C ( 125 ° F ) .
Close valve after each use and when empty .
Use a back flow preventive device in the piping .
Use in accordance with the Material Safety Data Sheet ( MSDS ) .
FIRST AID : IF INHALED , remove to fresh air .
If not breathing , give artificial respiration .
If breathing is difficult , give oxygen .
Call a physician .
IN CASE OF FROSTBITE , obtain medical treatment immediately .
