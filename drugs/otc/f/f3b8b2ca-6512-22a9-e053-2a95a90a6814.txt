Active ingredient Sodium Fluoride 0 . 02 % Purpose • Anticavity Toothpaste Use • Aids in the prevention of dental cavities Directions • Apply appropriate amount on a toothbrush and brush teeth .
Warnings • Be careful not to swallow .
Rinse mouth thoroughly after use .
• If pain occurs when using this product , stop using and consult your dentist .
• For children under 6 years of age , use a pea size amount of toothpaste as recommended by the KFDA .
Use under the guidance of a guardian .
• If a child under 6 years old swallows a large amount , consult a physician or dentist immediately .
• Keep out of the reach of children under 6 years old .
