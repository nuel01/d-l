Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 62 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses • A hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin Warnings Warnings For external use only Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame or sparks When using this product do not use in or near eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash appears on the skin .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Place enough to cover both hands in the palm , and rub hands together until dry .
• Children under 6 years of age should be supervised by adult when applying this product .
