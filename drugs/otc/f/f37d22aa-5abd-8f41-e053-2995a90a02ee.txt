Active Ingredient Chloroxylenol 1 % w / w Purpose Skin antimicrobial Use Reduces amount of bacteria on hands .
Warnings • For external use only .
Do not use in eyes .
• Discontinue use if irritation and redness develop .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours consult a doctor .
• Not for use on children under six months of age .
• For institutional and professional use only .
Directions • Wet hands and forearms .
• Apply a small amount ( 5 mL ) or palmful to hands and forearms .
• Scrub thoroughly for at least 15 seconds .
• Rinse completely and dry .
