ACTIVE INGREDIENT Benzalkonium Chloride - 0 . 26 % ( w / w ) Purpose Antiseptic USE For hand washing to decrease bacteria on skin .
Recommended for repeated use .
WARNINGS For external use only When using this product do not use in or near the eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash appears and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a poison control center right away .
