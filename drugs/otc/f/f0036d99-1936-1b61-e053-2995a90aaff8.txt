Active Ingredient Cetylpyridinium chloride 0 . 07 % Purpose Antigingivitis / Antiplaque Use helps control plaque that leads to gingivitis and bleeding gums WARNINGS Stop use and ask a dentist if Gingivitis , bleeding , or redness persists for more than 2 weeks You have painful or swollen gums , pus from the gum line , loose teeth , or increasing spacing between the teeth .
These may be signs or symptoms of periodontitis , a serious form of gum disease .
