Active Ingredient Lidocaine HCl 5 % Purpose Topical analgesic / Anesthetic Uses For the temporary relief of minor pain , irritation and itching Warnings For external use only .
When using this product Avoid contact with eyes .
Do not apply to wounds or damaged , broken or irritated skin Stop use and ask a doctor If • Condition worsens .
• Irritation develops .
Redness appears .
• Symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occurs again within a few days .
Keep out of reach of children If product is swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
