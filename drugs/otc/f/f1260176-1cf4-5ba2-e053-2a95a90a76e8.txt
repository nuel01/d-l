Directions : Adults and children 6 years of age and older : Brush teeth thoroughly , preferably after each meal or at least twice a day , or as directed by a dentist or doctor .
Instruct children under 6 years of age in good brushing and rinsing habits ( to minimize swallowing ) .
Supervise children as necessary until capable of using without supervision .
Children under 2 years of age : Consult a dentist or doctor .
