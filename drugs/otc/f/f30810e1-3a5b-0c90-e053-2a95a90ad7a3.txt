Drug Facts Active ingredients Undecylenic acid 10 % Purpose Antifungal Uses For treatment of Athlete ' s Foot Relieves • itching • scaling • cracking • burning Warnings For external use only Avoid contact with the eyes Do not use on children under 2 years of age unless directed by a doctor If irritation occurs or if there is no improvement within 4 weeks , discontinue use and consult a doctor .
Keep out of the reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek professional assistance or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions Wash the affected area and dry thoroughly .
Apply a few drops of the product over affected area twice daily ( morning and night ) or as directed by a doctor .
Supervise children in the use of this product .
Pay special attention to spaces between the toes ; wear well - fitting , ventilated shoes , and change shoes and socks at least once daily .
Use daily for 4 weeks .
If condition persists longer , consult a doctor .
This product is not effective on the scalp and nails .
