Active ingredient ( each gram contains ) Bacitracin 500 units Purpose First aid antibiotic Uses first aid to help prevent infection in : • minor cuts • scrapes • burns Warnings For external use only Do not use • in the eyes • over large areas of the body • if you are allergic to any of the ingredients Ask a doctor before use if you have • deep or puncture wounds • animal bites • serious burns Stop use and ask a doctor if • you need to use longer than 1 week • condition persists or gets worse • rash or other allergic reaction develops Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • clean the affected area • apply a small amount of this product ( an amount equal to the surface area of the tip of a finger ) on the area 1 to 3 times daily • may be covered with a sterile bandage Other information • store at 20 ° - 25 ° C ( 68 ° - 77 ° F ) Inactive ingredients light mineral oil , white petrolatum Questions or comments ?
