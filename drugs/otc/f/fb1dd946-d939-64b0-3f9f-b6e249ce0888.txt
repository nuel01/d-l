Drug Facts Active ingredient Trolamine salicylate 10 % Purpose Pain relieving cream Use • temporarily relieves minor aches and pains of muscles and joints due to arthritis , strains and sprains , and simple backache Warnings For external use only Do not use • in or near eyes • on wounds or damaged skin Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition lasts or gets worse • symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days If you are pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health care professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a poison control center right away .
