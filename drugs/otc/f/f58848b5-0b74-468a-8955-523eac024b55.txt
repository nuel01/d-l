Drug Facts Active Ingredients Benzalkonium Chloride Purpose Antimicrobial Uses Sanitizes hands to help reduce bacteria that potentially cause diseases .
Warnings For External use only .
Use on hands only .
Do not use over large areas of the body .
Do not use in the eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop Use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develops , or persist for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Place enough product in your palm to thoroughly cover your hands .
Rub hands together briskly until hands are entirely dry .
Other information Store at room temperature .
