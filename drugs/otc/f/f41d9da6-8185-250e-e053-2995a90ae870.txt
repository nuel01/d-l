Active ingredients Benzalkonium Cl 0 . 13 % Lidocaine HCl 4 % Purpose Benzalkonium Cl – First Aid Antiseptic Lidocaine HCl – Pain Relieving Spray Uses first aid to help prevent bacterial contamination or skin infection , and for temporary relief of pain and itching associated with minor : • cuts • scrapes • burns • sunburn • skin irritations Warnings For external use only Ask a doctor before use if you have • deep or puncture wounds • animal bites • serious burns When using this product • do not use in or near the eyes • do not apply over large areas of the body or in large quantities • do not apply over raw surfaces or blistered areas Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days , or clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
