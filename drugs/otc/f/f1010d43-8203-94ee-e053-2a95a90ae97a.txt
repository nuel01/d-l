Drug Facts Active ingredient Salicylic Acid 1 . 50 % Purpose Acne treatment Uses • For the treatment of acne Warnings For external use only .
Avoid eye area .
Using other topical acne drugs at the same time or right after use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one medication should be used unless directed by a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , call a doctor or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Cleanse skin .
• Apply a thin layer 1 to 3 times daily or as needed .
• If dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to every other day .
