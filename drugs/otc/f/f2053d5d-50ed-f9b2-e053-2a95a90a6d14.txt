MAXIMUM STRENGTH FEMININE ANTI - ITCH WIPES Drug Facts Active ingredient Pramoxine Hydrochloride 1 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Uses For the temporary relief of personal discomfort Warnings For external use only Do not apply on wounds or damaged skin .
When using this product avoid contact with the eyes .
Do not use other than as directed .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • conditions worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • remove cloth from container , close container • unfold cloth and gently wipe area from front to back • use only once , then throw used cloth away • do not flush , discard in trash container • apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
• adults and children 12 years of age and older : apply to affected area daily • children under 12 years of age : consult a doctor before using Other information • store at controlled room temperature 20 ° - 25 ° C ( 68 ° - 77 ° F ) Inactive ingredients Aloe Barbadensis Leaf juice , Aqua ( Purified Water ) , Benzoic Acid , Citric Acid , Dehydroacetic Acid , Disodium Cocoamphodiacetate , Fragrance , Glycerin , Phenoxyethanol , Polyaminopropyl Biguanide , Polysorbate 20 , Sodium Hydroxide , Tocopheryl Acetate ( Vitamin E ) .
