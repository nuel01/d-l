Active Ingredient ( in each lozenge ) Pectin 2 . 8 mg Purpose Oral Demulcent Uses • For temporary relief of minor discomfort and protection of irritated areas in sore mouth and sore throat .
Warnings Sore throat warning : If sore throat is severe , persists for more than 2 days , is accompanied or followed by fever , headache , rash , nausea , or vomiting , consult a doctor promptly .
If sore mouth symptoms do not improve in 7 days , see your dentist or doctor promptly .
Keep out of the reach of children .
Directions Use as needed , up to 8 lozenges per day , or as directed by a dentist or doctor .
• Adults and children 2 years of age and older : Allow lozenge to dissolve slowly in the mouth .
For optimal effect , intermittently position lozenge under the tongue .
Do not swallow whole .
Do not chew .
• Children under 12 years of age : Should be supervised in the use of this product .
• Children under 2 years of age : Consult a dentist or doctor .
Other Information • Store at room temperature .
• You may experience a slight , temporary tingle sensation upon initial use ; this is normal and causes no harm .
