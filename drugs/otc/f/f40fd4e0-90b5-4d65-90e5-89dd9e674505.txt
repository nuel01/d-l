Active ingredient Lidocaine 5 % Purpose Topical Anaesthetic Uses • For the temporary relief of local discomfort associated with anorectal disorders • For the temporary relief of pain , soreness , and burning Warnings For external use only .
When using this product • avoid contact with eyes • do not exceed the recommended daily dosage unless directed by a physician • do not put this product into the rectum by using fingers or any mechanical device or applicator • certain persons can develop allergic reactions to ingredients in this product .
