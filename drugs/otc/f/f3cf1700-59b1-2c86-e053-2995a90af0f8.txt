Active Ingredient PCMX 0 . 1 % Purpose Antibacterial Handwashing Uses • Handwash to help decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use in the eyes .
In case of contact , immediately flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash appears and lasts .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Apply a small amount , covering hands with product for 30 seconds .
Lather and rinse .
• Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
Inactive Ingredients Water , Lauramine Oxide , Sodium Lauryl Ether Sulfate , 4 - chloro - 3 , 5 - dimethylphenol .
