Menthol 5 % Topical Analgesic For temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with deep muscle therapy treatments .
For external use only Do not bandage tightly • avoid contact with eyes • do not apply to wounds or damaged skin • do not use with heating pads or other heating devices .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • Condition worsens or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • adults and children 12 years of age and older , apply to affected area no more than 10 times a day or as directed by your physical or occupational therapist • children under 12 years of age , do not use , consult a doctor .
