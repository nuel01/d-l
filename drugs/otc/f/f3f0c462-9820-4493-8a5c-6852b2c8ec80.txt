Active Ingredient Purified water 99 . 1 % Purpose Emergency eyewash and skin flush Uses For flushing the eye and skin to reduce chances of severe injury caused by acid , alkali , or particulate contamination .
Warnings For external use only Do not use ● for injection ● in intraocular surgery ● internally ● If solution changes color or becomes cloudy When using this product ● avoid contamination ● do not reuse Once opened , discard .
Ask doctor if you have ● eye pain ● changes in vision ● redness or irritation of the eye after use ● an injury caused by alkali Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
