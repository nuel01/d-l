Warnings Flammable , keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only When using this product keep out of eyes , ears and mouth .
In case of contact rinse thoroughly with water .
Avoid contact with broken skin .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash appears and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Place enough product in your palm to thoroughly cover your hands .
Rub hands together until dry .
For children under 6 , use only under adult supervision .
Not recommended for infants .
Other information • Store below 105 ° F ( 40 ° C ) • May discolor certain fabrics or surfaces ; harmful to wood finishes and plastics .
