Drug Facts Active Ingredients ( In Each Gram ) Bacitracin Zinc 500 unit Uses : First aid to help prevent infection in : • minor cuts • scrapes • burns Do not use : • in the eyes • if you are allergic to any of the ingredients , due to the possibility of anaphylactic shock over large areas of the body • longer than 1 week unless directed by a doctor Warnings : For external use only Stop use and ask a doctor before use in case of • deep puncture wounds , animal bites or serious burns • the condition persists or gets worse • if a rash or other allergic reaction develops KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions : • Clean the affected area .
• Apply a small amount of this product ( an amount equal to the surface area of the tip of a finger ) on the area 1 to 3 times daily .
• May be covered with a sterile bandage .
Other Information : • Store at controlled room temperature 59 * to 86 * F ( 15 * to 30 * C ) • Avoid excessive heat and humidity • Tamper Evident .
