Actve Ingredients : In each gram - Neomycin Sulfate - 5 mg ( equivalent to 3 . 5 mg Neomycin ) , Polymyxin B Sulfate - 5000 I . U . , Bacitracin Zinc - 400 I . U . PURPOSE : First aid antibiotic First aid to help prevent infection in minor cuts , burns , scrapes For external use only .
Do not use on eyes .
Do not use if you are allergic to any of the ingredients due to the possibility of anaphylactic shock .
Stop use and ask a doctor before use in cases of deep puncture wounds , animal bites , serious burns ; if the condition persists or gets worse ; if a rash or other allergic reaction developes If swallowed get medical help or contact a poison control center right away KEEP OUT OF THE REACH OF CHILDREN Clean the affected area .
apply a small amount of product ( an amount equal to the surface area of the tip of a finger ) on the area 1 - 3 times daily .
May be covered with a sterile bandage .
