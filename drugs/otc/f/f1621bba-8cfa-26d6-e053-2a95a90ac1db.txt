Description Minute - Foam ® is a flavored aqueous foaming solution of acidulated phosphate sodium fluoride with an optimized low pH and a fluoride ion concentration of 1 . 23 % w / w .
Inactives • Purified water • Poloxamer 407 • Cocamidoprophyl betaine • Xylitol , sucralose • Sodiumphosphate monobasic • Flavor Indication and Usage Warnings • DO NOT SWALLOW .
• Keep out of reach of children .
In the event more than the treatment dose is swallowed , administer calcium ( e . g . milk ) and get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Avoid spraying towards open flame .
• Contents under pressure .
Do not puncture or incinerate .
Do not expose to heat .
• Store at temperatures between 59 - 86 ° F • ( 15 - 30 ° C ) .
Keep from freezing .
