Purpose : Antiseptic Purpose Antiseptic , Antibacterial Wet Wipes Use Decrease bacteria on skin .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • in children less than 2 months of age • on open skin wounds When using this product do not get into eyes , ears , and mouth .
If contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash develops and continues for morethan 72 hours .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away at 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 .
