Drug Facts Active ingredient Chloroxylenol 0 . 25 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses • for handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin • recommended for repeated use Warnings For external use only Do not use in the eyes .
In case of contact , flush thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develop , or if condition persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Wet hands and forearms .
Apply 5 milliliters ( teaspoonful ) or palmful to hands and forearms .
Scrub thoroughly for 15 seconds .
Rinse and repeat .
