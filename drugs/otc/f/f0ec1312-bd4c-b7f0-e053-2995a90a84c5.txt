Active Ingredient Benzocaine 20 % Benzethonium chloride 0 . 2 % Purpose Topical anesthetic Topical antiseptic Uses For the temporary relief of pain and itching , and to help protect against skin infection in : • minor burns • minor skin irritations • minor cuts and scrapes • insect bites • sunburns Warnings For external use only Flammable • keep away from fire or flame • contents under pressure • do not puncture , incinerate or expose container to temperatures above 120 o F Do not use • in or near the eyes or over large portions of the body • in case of deep or puncture wounds or on : • raw surfaces • blistered areas • animal bites • serious burns Stop use and ask a doctor if If condition persists or worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away Directions • clean the affected area • shake can well before using • hold can 6 to 12 inches away from the affected area and spray liberally • apply to affected area not more than 3 times daily • for adult institutional use only • not intended for use on children Other information • avoid inhaling • use only as directed • intentional misuse by deliberately concentrating and inhaling the contents may be harmful or fatal Inactive ingredients butane , dipropylene glycol , isobutane , propane Principal Display Panel [ MULTIMEDIA ] [ MULTIMEDIA ] Pyrocaine Burn Old [ MULTIMEDIA ] [ MULTIMEDIA ]
