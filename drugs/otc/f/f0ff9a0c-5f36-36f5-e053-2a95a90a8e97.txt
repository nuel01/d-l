Drug Facts Active ingredient Salicylic Acid 1 % Purpose Acne Treatment Uses • treats acne • clears acne blemishes • helps prevent development of new acne blemishes Warnings For external use only When using this product using other topical acne drugs at the same time or right after use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one medication should be used unless directed by a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If product is swallowed , call a doctor or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
