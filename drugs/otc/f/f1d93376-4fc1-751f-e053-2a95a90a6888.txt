ACTIVE INGREDIENTS SALICYLIC ACID 2 % PURPOSE ACNE MEDICATION USES • For the treatment of acne .
• Clears acne pimples .
• Helps prevent new acne pimples from forming .
WARNINGS • For external use only • When using this product skin irritation and dryness is more likely to occur if you use another topical acne medication at the same time .
If irritation occurs , only use one topical acne medication at a time .
• Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
DIRECTIONS • clean the skin thoroughly before applying this product • cover the entire affected area with a thin layer one to three times daily • because excessive drying of the skin may occur , start with one application daily , then gradually increase to two to three times daily if needed or as directed by a doctor • if bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to once a day or every other day .
OTHER INFORMATION store at 20 ° - 25 ° C ( 68 ° - 77 ° F ) [ see USP Controlled Room Temperature ] .
