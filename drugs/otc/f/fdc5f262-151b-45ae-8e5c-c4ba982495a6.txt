Colloidal Oatmeal 2 % Skin Protectant Uses temporarily protects and helps relieve minor skin irritation and itching due to : • eczema • rashes • insect bites • poison ivy , oak or sumac Warnings For external use only .
Do not use if you are allergic to sesame oil .
When using this product • do not get into eyes • if you currently use steroids or have used steroids in the recent past , it may take 1 to 3 months to see results Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
