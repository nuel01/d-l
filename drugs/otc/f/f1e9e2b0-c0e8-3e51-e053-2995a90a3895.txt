Directions Apply small amount to palm .
Briskly rub , covering hands with product until dry .
Uses Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin that could cause disease .
Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 62 % v / v Inactive Ingredients Water , Peg - 10 Dimethicone , Glycerin , Isopropyl Myristate , Polyquaternium - 11 , Disodium EDTA , Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice , Tocopheryl Acetate .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , contact a physician or poison control center .
Purpose Antibacterial Agent Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flames . Avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , flush eyes with water .
Stop use , and ask a doctor if , in rare instances , redness or irritation develops and persists for more than 72 hours .
