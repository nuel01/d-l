Warnings For external use only When using this product • avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes • do not apply to open wounds or damaged skin • do not bandage or use heating pad .
Stop use and ask doctor if • condition worsens • severe skin irritation occurs • pain persists for more than 7 days • pain clears up and then recurs a few days later If pregnant or breast feeding , ask a healthcare professional before use Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately Directions • rub on affected area .
