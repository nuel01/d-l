Inactives Purified water , carboxymethylcellulose sodium , spearmint oil , sucralose , phosphoric acid , sodium hydroxide .
Indication and Usage A once daily topical neutral aqueous solution for the prevention of dental caries .
After brushing with dentifrice , apply a thin ribbon of the formulation to teeth for at least1 minute using a toothbrush . Expectorate and do not eat , drink or rinse for at least 30 minutes .
Warnings DO NOT SWALLOW .
Keep outof reach of children . If more than used for treatment isswallowed , administer calcium ( e . g . milk ) and get medical help orcontact a Poison Control Centerright away .
For professional use only .
