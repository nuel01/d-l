ACTIVE INGREDIENT ( S ) Alcohol 80 % v / v PURPOSE Antiseptic USE ( S ) Health care personnel hand rub to help reduce bacteria that potentially causes disease .
WARNINGS For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
DO NOT USE • in children less than 2 months of age • on open skin wounds WHEN USING THIS PRODUCT When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse thoroughly with water .
STOP USE AND ASK DOCTOR IF Stop using and ask doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
DIRECTIONS • Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
