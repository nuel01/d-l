Warnings For external use only .
When using this product keep out of eyes .
Rinse with water to remove .
Stop use and ask doctor if rash and irritation develops and lasts .
Avoid contact with eyes .
If product gets into eyes , rinse thoroughly with water .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact poison control center right away .
Directions Apply generously before sun exposure and as needed .
For children under 6 months , ask a doctor .
Reapply as needed or after towel drying , swimming or perspiring .
Other Information Sun alert : Limiting sun exposure , wearing protective clothing , and using sunscreens may reduce the risk of skin cancer , and other harmful effects of the sun .
