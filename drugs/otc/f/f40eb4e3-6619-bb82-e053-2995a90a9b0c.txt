Warnings For external use only .
Contents under pressure .
Do not puncture or incinerate .
Do note store at temperatures above 120 F . Do not use on damaged or broken skin .

When using this product , keep out of eyes .
Rinse with water to remove .
Directions Hold container 4 to 6 inches from the skin to apply Spay liberally and spread evenly by hand 15 minutes before sun exposure Do not spray directly into face .
