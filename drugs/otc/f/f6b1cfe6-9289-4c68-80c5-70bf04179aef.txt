Active ingredient Triclosan 0 . 55 % Purpose Antiseptic Handwash Uses For hand washing to decrease bacteria on skin .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product Do not use in the eyes .
In case of contact , immediately flush with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develop .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours , consult a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions FOR INSTITUTIONAL AND FOOD PLANT USE .
Wet hands and apply a sufficient amount to cover hands with product .
Vigorously rub the surfaces of the lathered fingers , finger tips , areas between the fingers , hands and arms , then thoroughly rinse with potable water .
Follow by thoroughly drying of cleaned hands and arms before handling food .
Avoid contamination of food during use and storage .
