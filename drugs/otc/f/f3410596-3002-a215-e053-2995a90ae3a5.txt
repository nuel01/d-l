Drug Facts Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 1 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses : Decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings • For external use only .
When using this product • avoid contact with eyes .

If swallowed , get medical help or contact Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Open the flip top lid , remove one wipe to use .
• Wipe hands thoroughly the product and allow to dry without wiping .

Other information • Dispose of wipe in the proper container .
