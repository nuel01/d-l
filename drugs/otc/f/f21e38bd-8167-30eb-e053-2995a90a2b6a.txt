Drug Facts Active ingredients Zinc Oxide 19 % ( Non - Nano ) Purpose Sunscreen Uses • Helps prevent sunburn Warnings • For external use only .
• Do not use on damaged or broken skin .
• Stop use and ask a doctor if rash occurs .
• When using this product , keep out of eyes .
Rinse with water to remove .
Directions • Apply liberally 15 minutes before sun exposure .
• Reapply after 80 minutes of swimming or sweating or immediately after towel drying and at least every 2 hours .
• For children under 6 months of age ; ask a doctor .
Other info Protect this product from excessive heat & sunlight .
