Active Ingredients Benzocaine 6 % w / v Isopropyl Alcohol 60 % w / v Purpose Topical Anesthetic Antiseptic For temporary relief of pain and itching associated with minor burns , scrapes and insect bites .
Warnings For External Use Only CautionFlammable Do not use in the eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water Stop Use If irritation , redness or other symptoms develop .
Consult a doctor if the condition persists or gets worse .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Apply to affected area 3 - 4 times daily .
For adults and children 2 years of age and older .
Children under 2 years , consult physician .
