Repeat hourly if symptoms return .
• do not take more than 18 tablets in 24 hours • do not use the maximum dosage more than 2 weeks Warnings Ask a doctor or pharmacist before use if you are taking a prescription drug .
Antacids may interfere with certain prescription drugs If pregnant or breast - feeding baby , ask a health professional before use KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN .

Prompt medical attention is critical for adults as well as for children even if you do not notice any signs or symptoms .
