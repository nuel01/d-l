Active Ingredient Triclosan 1 % Purpose Antiseptic Use To decrease bacteria on the skin .
Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • on broken skin • otherwise than as directed .
When using this product • avoid with eyes or on mucous membranes Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • irritation or redness develops • if condition persists for more than 3 days Keep out of the reach of children .
If swallowed contact a doctor or Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions Wet skin .
Rub bar vigorously over the skin for approximately 2 minutes .
Rinse and repeat .
