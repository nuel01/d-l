Active Ingredients ( in each gram ) Bacitracin Zinc 400 units Neomycin Sulfate 5 mg ( equivalent to 3 . 5 mg .
Neomycin ) Polymyxin B Sulfate 5000 units first aid antibiotic Uses : First aid to help prevent infection in : • minor cuts • burns • scrapes Warnings : • For external use only Do not use : • in the eyes • if you are allergic to any of the ingredients , due to the possibility of anaphylactic shock • over large areas of the body • longer than 1 week unless directed by a doctor Stop use and ask a doctor : • before use in case of deep puncture wounds , animal bites or serious burns • if the condition persists or gets worse • if a rash or other allergic reaction develops KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions : • Clean the affected area .
• Apply a small amount of this product ( an amount equal to the surface area of the tip of a finger ) on the area 1 to 3 times daily .
• May be covered with a sterile bandage .
Inactive Ingredients : petrolatum .
