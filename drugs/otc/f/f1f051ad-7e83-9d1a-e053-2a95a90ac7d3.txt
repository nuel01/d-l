Drug Facts Active ingredients Lidocaine HCl 2 . 0 % Purpose Topical pain relief Use Temporarily relieves pain associated with minor burns .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • in eyes , if contact occurs rinse thoroughly with water • in large quantities , particularly over raw or blistered areas Stop use and ask a doctor if condition worsens , or persists for more than 7 days or clears up and returns • Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact Poison Control Center right away Directions • adults and children 2 years of age and older : spray an even layer of burn spray over affected area not more than 3 - 4 times daily • children under 2 years of age : consult a doctor Inactive ingredients aloe vera , diazolidinyl urea , propylene glycol , purified water ---------- Questions ?
