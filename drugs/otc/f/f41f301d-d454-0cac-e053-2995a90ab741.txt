Active ingredients Lidocaine HCl 4 % Purpose Lidocaine HCl - Topical anesthetic Uses temporarily relieves pain Warnings For external use only Do not use • on large areas of the body or on cut , irritated or blistered skin • on puncture wounds • for more than one week without consulting a doctor When using this product • use only as directed • do not get into eyes • do not bandage tightly or apply external heat to the area of use Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days • redness , rash , or irritation occurs If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
