Active Ingredient Dimethicone 1 % Purpose Skin Protectant Uses • helps prevent and temporarily protects and relieves chapped or cracked skin prone to psoriasis and vitiligo .
Warnings For external use only This product is not a sunscreen or sunblock .
Prolonged sun exposure can cause sunburn , skin damage , and cancer .
Healthy ( non - psoriasis or vitiligo affected ) skin should be covered with clothing or protected with a sunscreen .
• When using this product do not get into eyes • If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away Stop use and ask doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms do not improve after more than 7 days Keep out of reach of children .
