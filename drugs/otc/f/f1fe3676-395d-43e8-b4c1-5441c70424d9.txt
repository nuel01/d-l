Lidocaine HCl 4 % ............... Anesthetic Purpose Anasthetic Warning Keep out of reach of children Use For the temporary relief of pain and itching associated with minor burns , sunburn , minor cuts , scrapes , insect bites or minor skin irritations .
Warning Avoid contact with eyes .
If condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days , discontinue use and consult a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
Directions Adults and children 2 years of age and older : Apply Lidocoll spray not more than 3 to 4 times per day or moisten dressing prior to removal to reduce pain .
Children under 2 years of age consult a doctor .
Inactive Ingredients hydrolyzed fish collagen ( 15 % ) , purified water , benzethonium chloride and HCl .
