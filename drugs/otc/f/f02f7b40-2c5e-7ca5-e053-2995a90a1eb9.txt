Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antiseptic Hand Wash Uses • Kills 99 . 99 % of most common germs that cause illness in as little as 15 seconds .
• Sanitizing to decrease bacteria on skin .
• No rinsing required .
• Does not contain alcohol .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use in the eyes ; if in eyes , rinse promptly and thoroughly with water .
When using this product • Do not swallow .
• If swallowed , do not induce vomiting and if individual is conscious , give large quantities of water to drink and consult a physician immediately .
Stop using and ask a doctor Stop use and ask a doctor if skin irritation or redness persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children and pets Keep out of reach of children and pets .
Children must be supervised in use of this product .
Directions • Press pump to dispense product onto hands .
• Rub hands together allowing foam to contact all areas , especially around the nails .
• Continue until foam has disappeared and / or hands are dry .
Other information • Store at 20 to 25 ° C ( 68 to 77 ° F ) .
• Do not freeze .
• Dispose in accordance with all applicable federal , state and local regulations .
