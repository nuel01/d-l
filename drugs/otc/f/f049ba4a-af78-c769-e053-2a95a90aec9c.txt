Drug Facts Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 75 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses • To decrease bacteria on skin without soap or water • Recommended for repeated use Warnings For external use only .
Only on hands .
Flammable , keep away from fire or flame .
Do not use in or near eyes .
In case of contact , rinse thoroughly with water Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develop and persists for more than 72 hours Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
