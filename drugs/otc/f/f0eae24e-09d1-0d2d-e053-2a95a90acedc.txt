Active Ingredient ( in each wipe ) Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose : Antiseptic Purpose Antiseptic Use [ s ] Hand Sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For external use only Do not use • in children less than 2 months of age • on open skin wounds Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away Directions • Place the container on a hard flat surface .
Using your thumb , press and hold firmly in the center of the container for a second and release .
Wait for the towelette to rise completely out of the container .
Remove and unfold the towelette for use .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
Other information • Avoid freezing and excessive heat above 40 C ( 104 F ) • Store between 20 - 25 C ( 68 - 77 F ) Inactive ingredients sodium hydroxide , water [ MULTIMEDIA ] 73737 - 436 - 10 [ MULTIMEDIA ]
