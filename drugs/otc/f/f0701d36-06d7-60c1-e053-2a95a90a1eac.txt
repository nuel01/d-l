Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 62 % v / v .
Purpose Antiseptic Use • Hand Sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
• For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame Do not use • in children less than 2 months of age • on open skin wounds When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash appears and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away at 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 Directions • Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
