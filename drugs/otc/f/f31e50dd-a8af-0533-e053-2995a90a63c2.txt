Flammable : Do not use while smoking or near heat or flame .
Do not puncture or incinerate .
Contents under pressure .
Do not store at temperature above 120ºF .
When using this product • do not get into eyes or mouth • use only as directed Intentional misuse by deliberately concentrating and inhaling contents cans be harmful or fatal .

Do not use on children under two years of age unless directed by a doctor .
