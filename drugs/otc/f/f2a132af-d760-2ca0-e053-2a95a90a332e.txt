Active Ingredient Salicylic acid 40 % Purpose Wart Remvoer Uses • for the removal of common warts .
the common wart is easily recognized by the rough ' cauliflower - like ' appearance of the surface .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • if you are diabetic • have poor blood circulation • on irritated skin , on any area that is infected or reddened • on genital warts and warts on the face • on moles , birthmarks and warts with hair growing from them • on mucous membranes Stop use and ask doctor if discomfort persist Keep out of reach of children .
If , swallowed , get medical help or cotnact a poison Control Center right away .
