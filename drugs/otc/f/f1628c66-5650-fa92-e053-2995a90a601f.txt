White petrolatum 93 . 5 % Purpose : Skin protectant Uses : Temporarily protects minor : cuts , scrapes , burns .
Temporarily protects and helps chapped or cracked skin and lips .
Helps protect lips from drying effects of wind and cold weather .
Warnings : For external use only .
Stop use and ask a doctor if : condition worsens , symptoms last for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days .
Do not use on : deep or puncture wounds , animal bites , serious burns Keep out of the reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Apply as needed .
Inactive ingredients : Corn oil , light mineral oil , vitamin A palmitate , vitamin D . [ MULTIMEDIA ] [ MULTIMEDIA ]
