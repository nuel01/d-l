Warnings - For external use only - When using this product : Using other topical acne medication at the same time or immediately following the use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one medication should be used unless directed by a doctor .
If sensitive to the sun or when increased sun exposure is expected use a sunscreen when using this and other acne products .
- Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact poison center right away .
