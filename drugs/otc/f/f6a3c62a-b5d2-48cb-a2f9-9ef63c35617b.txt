Drug Facts Active ingredient Salicylic Acid 40 % Purpose Corn remover Use For the removal of corns Warnings For external use only Do not use • on irritated skin • on any area that is infected or reddened • if you have diabetes or poor blood circulation Stop use and ask a doctor if discomfort persists Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) right away .
Directions • wash affected area • may soak corn in warm water for 5 minutes • dry thoroughly • remove medicated pad from backing paper by pulling from center of pad • apply • repeat procedure every 48 hours as needed for up to 14 days ( until corn is removed ) Other information • store at room temperature • avoid excessive heat Inactive ingredients lanolin , polybutene , rosin ester , rubber Questions ?
