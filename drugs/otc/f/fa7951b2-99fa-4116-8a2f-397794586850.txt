Active ingredient Benzalkonium chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses Helps eliminate bacteria on hands Warnings For external use only .
When using this product Avoid contact with eyes .
In case of contact , rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop using this product if irritation or redness develops and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • Apply onto wet hands .
