Active ingredient Tolnaftate 1 % Purpose Antifungal Uses • proven clinically effective in the treatment of athlete ' s foot ( tinea pedis ) and ringworm ( tinea corporis ) • prevent recurrence of most athlete ' s foot with daily use • for effective relief of itching , burning and cracking Warnings For external use only .
Flammable : Do not use while smoking or near heat or flame .
Do not puncture or incinerate .
Contents under pressure .
Do not store at temperature above 120ºF .
Intentional misuse by delibrately concentrating and inhaling contents can be harmful or fatal .
When using this product • avoid contact with the eyes or mouth • use only as directed Stop use and ask a doctor if • irritation occurs • no improvement within four weeks ( for athlete ' s foot ) or two weeks ( for jock itch ) Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Do not use on children under two years of age unless directed by a doctor .
