Active Ingredient White Petrolatum USP ( 50 % ) Purpose Skin protectant Uses • For the temporary protection of minor cuts , scrapes , burns and sunburn .
• Helps to temporarily protect chafed , chapped , cracked or windburned skin and lips .
Warnings For External Use Only Do not use over deep or puncture wounds infections or lacerations .
Ask a doctor When using this product avoid contact with eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if condition worsens or does not improve within 7 days Keep out of reach of children if swallowed get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
