Drug Facts Active ingredient Sodium chloride 3 % Purpose Hypertonicity agent Uses : Temporary relief of corneal edema Warnings : For external use only Do not use Except under the advice and supervision of a doctor When using this product • It may cause temporary burning and irritation • Replace cap after use • To avoid contamination , do not touch top of container to any surface Stop use and ask a doctor if • Condition worsens or persists for more than 72 hours • You experience eye pain , changes in vision , continued redness or irritation of the eye Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
