Warnings For external use only .
Do not use Do not use in the eyes .
In case of eye contact , immediately flush with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash appears and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children Keep out of reach of children .
If swallow , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Apply a small amount , covering hands with product for 30 seconds .
