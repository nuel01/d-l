Description Nephron FA is a prescription folic acid supplement with additional nutrients for kidney dialysis patients .
Nephron FA is an elongated , white , film coated tablet imprinted with NT and scored on one side , plain on the other .
Precaution Folic Acid may obscure pernicious anemia or produce remission while neurologic progress may continue .
Suggested Dosage One tablet daily taken away from meals , or as directed by the physician .
