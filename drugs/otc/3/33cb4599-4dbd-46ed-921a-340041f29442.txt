Active Ingredients ( each swab ) 10 % Povidone Iodine Solution USP ( 1 % available iodine ) Purpose Topical Antiseptic Uses To treat minor skin cuts , and abrasions WARNINGS FOR EXTERNAL USE ONLY Ask a doctor before use if you have • deep or puncture wounds • serious burn Stop use and ask doctor if • redness , irritation , swelling or pain persists or increases • infection occurs Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions : Reverse cardboard sleeve then crush at dot between thumb and forefinger .
Allow solution to saturate tip and apply solution to injury .
