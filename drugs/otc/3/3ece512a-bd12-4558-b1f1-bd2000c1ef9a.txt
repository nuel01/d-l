Drug Facts Active ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 65 % Purpose Antiseptic Use Decrease bacteria on hands .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable . Keep product away from fire or flame .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes ; in case of contact , flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops and persists .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
