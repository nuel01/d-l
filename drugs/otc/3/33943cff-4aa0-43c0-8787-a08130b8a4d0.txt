Ask a doctor before use if you have • heart disease • thyroid disease • high blood pressure • diabetes • difficulty in urination due to enlargement of the prostate gland Ask a doctor or pharmacist before use if you are presently taking a prescription drug for high blood pressure or depression .
When using this product • do not exceed the recommended daily dosage unless directed by a doctor • do not put into the rectum using fingers or any mechanical device or applicator Stop use and ask a doctor • if bleeding occurs • condition worsens or does not improve within 7 days If pregnant or breastfeeding , ask a health professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
DIRECTIONS • Adults : when practical , cleanse the affected area by patting or blotting with an appropriate cleansing wipe .
Gently dry by patting or blotting with a tissue or a soft cloth before applying gel .
• When first opening the tube , puncture foil seal with top end of cap • Apply externally to the affected area up to 4 times daily , especially at night , in the morning , or after each bowel movement Children under 12 years : ask a doctor .
