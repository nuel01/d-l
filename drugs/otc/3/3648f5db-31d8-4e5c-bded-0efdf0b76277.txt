Williams Hand Sanitizer Gel Drug Facts Active ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 80 % v / v Purpose Antimicrobial Use to help reduce bacteria on the skin .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only .
When using this product , do not use in or near the eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash appears .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together briskly until dry .
• Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
Other Information • Store below 86 ° F ( 30 ° C ) • May discolor certain fabrics or surfaces Questions ?
