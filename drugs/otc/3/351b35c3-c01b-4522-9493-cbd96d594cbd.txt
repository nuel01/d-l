Do not use ■ on open wounds or abraded skin or while nursing ■ on a child under 12 years of age with arthritis - like conditions ■ near the eyes or on mucous membranes ■ with a heating pad or bandage tightly over the area Discontinue use and consult a doctor if ■ conditions worsen or symptoms persists for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days ■ redness or irritation develops Keep out of the reach of children .
