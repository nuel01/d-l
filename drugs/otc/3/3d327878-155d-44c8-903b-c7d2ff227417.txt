Lubricant Uses • For the temporary relief of burning and irritation due to dryness of the eyes .
• For protection against further irritation .
Warnings For external use only Do not use • if this product changes color or becomes cloudy .
• if you are sensitive to any ingredient in this product .
When using this product • Do not touch tip of container to any surface to avoid contamination .
• Replace cap after each use .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • you feel eye pain .
• changes in vision occur .
• redness or irritation of the eye ( s ) gets worse or lasts more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Instill 1 to 2 drops in the affected eye ( s ) as needed • Children under 6 years of age : Ask a doctor Other Information • Store at room temperature .
• Do not use if carton is open or neckband on the bottle is broken or missing .
