Drug Facts Active Ingredients Undecylenic Acid 10 % Purpose Antifungal Uses • Cures most athlete ' s foot , jock itch & ringworm • Relieves itching , burning , cracking , and discomfort which accompany these conditions Warning • For external use only .
• Not for diaper rash • When using this product avoid contact with eyes and mucous membrane .
• Do not use on children under 2 years of age except under the advice and supervision of a doctor .
Stop use and ask a doctor if : • Irritation occurs • There is no improvement within 4 weeks of treatment for athlete ' s foot and ringworm and 2 weeks of treatment for jock itch discontinue use and consult a doctor .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN .
In case of accidental ingestion seek professional assistance or contact a poison control center right away .
Directions • Clean affected area and dry thoroughly .
• Apply a thin layer over affected area twice daily ( morning & night ) or as directed by a doctor .
• For athlete ' s foot pay special attention to the space between the toes .
Wear well fitting , ventilated shoes and change shoes & socks at least once a day .
• For athlete ' s foot and ringworm , use daily for 4 weeks • For jock itch use daily for 2 weeks .
• Supervise Children in use of this product .
• Product not effective in the scalp or nails .
