Active Ingredient : White Petrolatum ( 929 . 3 mg ) Purpose : Emollient Uses : • first aid in • minor burns • scalds • sunburn • windburn • diaper rash • chafing • chapped skin • nipple care • abrasions • cuts • ulcers Warnings : For external use only .
Ask doctor before use if you have severe or extensive burns .
When using this product the treatment of ulcers , severe cuts or wounds should be under the direction of a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions : • cleanse affected area with mild soap and warm water • dry thoroughly • apply Vitamin A & Vitamin D ointment in a thin layer to the affected area directly to the skin or by spreading on sterile gauze or clean cloth • bandage loosely Other information : • do not use if seal is punctured or is not visible .
