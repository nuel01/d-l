Active Ingredients Lidocaine 0 . 5 % Uses External Analgesic Indications For temporary relief of pain associated with sunburn and minor skin burns .
Warnings For external use only , avoid contact with eyes .
Do not use in large quantities , particularly over raw surfaces or blistered surfaces Keep out of reach of children .
Stop use and ask a doctor if : • condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days • condition clears up and occurs again within a few days Directions • Adults and children 2 years and older : apply to affected area .
Do not use more than 3 - 4 x daily .
Repeat daily until healed .
