Children under 6 years : Use only a pea sized amount and supervise child ' s brushing and rinsing ( to minimize swallowing ) .
Children under 2 years : ask a dentist or doctor .
Warnings Warnings Stop use and ask a dentist if problems persists or worsens .
Sensitive teeth may indicate a serious problem that may require prompt care by a dentist .
