Active ingredient ( in each tablet ) Caffeine 200 mg Purpose Alertness aid Uses helps restore mental alertness or wakefulness when experiencing fatigue or drowsiness Warnings For occasional use only Caffeine warning : The recommended dose of this product contains about as much caffeine as a cup of coffee .
Limit the use of caffeine - containing medications , foods , or beverages while taking this product because too much caffeine may cause nervousness , irritability , sleeplessness , and , occasionally , rapid heartbeat .
Do not use • in children under 12 years • as a substitute for sleep Stop use and ask a doctor if fatigue or drowsiness persists or continues to recur .
If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of overdose , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) right away .
