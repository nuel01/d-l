DRUG FACTS Active Ingredients : lsopropyl Alcohol 75 % Purpose : Antiseptic Uses • Helps prevent cross contamination by hand contact • Helps prevent drying and chafing of skin • Recommended for repeat use Warnings For external use only Flammable , keep away from fire or flame .
When using this product do not use in or near eyes .
Discontinue use if irritation or redness develops .
Use in well - ventilted area .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek medical attention or contact poison control center immediately .
Directions : • Place a palmful of product in one hand • Spread on both hands and rub into the skin until dry ( 1 - 2 min . )
