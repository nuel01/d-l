Lots of people have sensitive teeth .
When enamel is worn away ( through eating everyday acidic food or drinks ) or gums recede , the dentin underneath becomes exposed .
This can lead to tooth sensitivity , for example , with cold and hot food and drinks .
What does Sensodyne do ?
Sensodyne provides daily care for sensitive teeth * plus all the benefits you would expect from a daily fluoride toothpaste – strong teeth and freshness .
