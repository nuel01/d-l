Drug Facts Active ingredient Salicylic acid 1 % Purpose Acne medication Use for the treatment of acne Warnings For external use only When using this product • avoid contact with the eyes .
If product gets into the eyes , rinse thoroughly with water .
• skin irritation and dryness is more likely to occur if you use another topical acne medication at the same time .
If irritation occurs , only use one topical acne medication at a time .
• limit use to the face and neck Stop use and ask a doctor if skin or eye irritation develops Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • wet face • dispense product into hands , lather and massage gently onto face and neck , avoiding the delicate eye area • rinse thoroughly with warm water one to three times daily and gently pat dry • because excessive drying of the skin may occur , start with one application daily , then gradually increase to two or three times daily if needed or as directed by a doctor • if bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to once a day or every other day Other information • keep tightly closed • store in a cool , dry place Inactive ingredients Water , Sodium Cocoyl Isethionate , Glycerin , Cetyl Alcohol , Sodium Lauroyl Sarcosinate , Stearyl Alcohol , Cocamidopropyl Betaine , Steareth - 21 , Steareth - 2 , Coco - Glucoside , Fragrance , Acrylates / C10 - 30 Alkyl Acrylate Crosspolymer , Sodium Hydroxide , Niacinamide , Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice , Disodium EDTA , Butylene Glycol , Cucumis Sativus ( Cucumber ) Fruit Extract , Potassium Sorbate , Sodium Benzoate Questions ?
