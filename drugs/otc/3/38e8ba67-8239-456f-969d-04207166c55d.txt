Bisacodyl Laxative Suppositories Disclaimer : Most OTC drugs are not reviewed and approved by FDA ; however , they may be marketed if they comply with applicable regulations and policies .
FDA has not evaluated whether this product complies .
Active Ingredient Bisacodyl , 10 mg per suppository .
Purpose Stimulant laxative .
Keep Out of Reach of Children If swallowed , get medical help , or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Stop use and ask a doctor if rectal bleeding occurs or if you fail to have a bowel movement after using a laxative .
This may be a sign of a serious condition .
Uses Rectally for relief of occasional constipation .
This product generally produces a bowel movement within fifteen ( 15 ) to sixty ( 60 ) minutes ( one hour ) .
Warnings Using this product may cause abdominal discomfort , faintness , rectal burning and mild cramps .
Frequent or continued use of this product may result in dependence on laxatives .
Do not use if experiencing nausea , vomiting , or rectal bleeding .
Do not use in children under 6 years of age or for more than one week unless directed by a doctor .
Ask a doctor before use if you are pregnant or breast feeding , or if a sudden change in bowel habits persists for more than two ( 2 ) weeks .
Directions Remove plastic wrap and insert rounded end first into the rectum .
Adults and children over the age of 12 – Dosage – 10 mg , one suppository , in a single daily dose .
Children 6 - 12 – Dosage – 5 mg , one - half suppository , in a single daily dose .
Children under 6 year of age – Consult with a doctor .
Inactive Ingredients Polyethylene glycol , propylene glycol diacetate .
Storage and Handling Store below 86 ° F ( 30 ° C ) .
