Active ingredient Purpose Menthol 10 . 5 % ..............................................................
Topical Analgesic Uses Temporary relief from minor aches and pains of sore muscles and joints associated with arthritis , backache , strains and sprains Warnings For external use only .
Flammable : Do not use while smoking or near heat or flame .
When using this product • avoid contact with the eyes or mucous membranes • do not apply to wounds or damaged skin • do not apply to the irritated skin • do not bandage • wash hands after use with cool water • do not use with heating pad or device Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days , or clear up and reoccur .
Keep out of reach of children .
If accidentally ingested get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately Directions • Adults and children 2 years of age and older : Apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
• Children under 2 years of age : Consult physician .
