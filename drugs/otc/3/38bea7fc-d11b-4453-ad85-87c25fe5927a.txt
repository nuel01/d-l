Drug Facts Active ingredients Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 10 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses • For hand washing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings • For external use only .
• Keep out of reach of eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water .
• Do not use if you are allergic to any one of the ingredients .
• Discontinue use if irritation or redness develops , and if condition persists for more than 72 hours consult a physician • Keep out of reach of children unless under adult supervision .
• If swallowed , get medical help , or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
