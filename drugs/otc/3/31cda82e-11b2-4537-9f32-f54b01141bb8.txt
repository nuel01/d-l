Active ingredients Calamine 8 % Pramoxine HCl 1 % Purpose Skin protectant Topical analgesic Uses temporarily relieves pain and itching associated with : • rashes due to poison ivy , poison oac or poison sumac • insect bites • minor skin irritation • minor cuts dries the oozing and weeping of poison ivy , poison oak and poison sumac Warnings For external use only .
When using this product do not get into eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens or does not improve within 7 days • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • shake well before use • adults and children 2 years of age and older : apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily • children under 2 years of age : ask a doctor Other information • store at 20 ° to 25 ° C ( 68 ° to 77 ° F ) Inactive ingredients SD alcohol 38 - B , camphor , diazolidinyl urea , fragrance , hypromellose , methylparaben , polysorbate 80 , propylene glycol , propylparaben , purified water , zanthan gum Package Label [ MULTIMEDIA ] [ MULTIMEDIA ]
