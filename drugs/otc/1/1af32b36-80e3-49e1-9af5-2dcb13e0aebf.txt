Active ingredient Calcium carbonates USP 500 mg Purpose Antacid Uses relieves : • heartburn • sour stomach • acid indigestion • upset stomach associated with these symptoms Warnings Ask a doctor or pharmacist before use if you are taking a prescription drug .
Antacids may interact with certain prescription drugs .
When using this product • do not take more than 15 tablets in 24 hours • do not use the maximum dosage for more than 2 weeks .
Keep out of Reach of Children Directions • Chew 2 - 4 tablets as symptoms occur , or as directed by a doctor .
Other Information • Store below 30 ° C ( 86 ° F ) .
• Keep the container tightly closed • For calcium and magnesium content see Supplement Facts .
