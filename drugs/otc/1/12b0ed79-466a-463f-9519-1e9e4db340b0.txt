Drug Facts Active ingredient Isopropyl Alcohol 70 % Purpose Antispetic Uses Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
Do not use • in children less than 2 months of age • on open skin wounds When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask for a doctor if irritation occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing Other Information • Store between 15 - 30 C ( 59 - 86 F ) • Avoid freezing and excessive heat above 40 C ( 104 F ) Inactive ingredients glycerin , deionized water Packaging [ MULTIMEDIA ] [ MULTIMEDIA ]
