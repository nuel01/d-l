Active ingredient Salicylic acid 2 % Purpose Psoriasis Uses • for the relief of skin itching , scaling , redness , flaking and irritation associated with psoriasis • helps prevent the recurrence of the symptoms of psoriasis Warnings For external use only When using this product avoid contact with the eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if condition worsens or does not improve after regular use of this product as directed Ask doctor before use if condition covers a large area of the body Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .

Use a sunscreen , wear protective clothing , and limit sun exposure while using this proudct and for a week afterwards .
