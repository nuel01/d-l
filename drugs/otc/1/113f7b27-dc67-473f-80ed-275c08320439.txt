Indications and Usage Temporarily relieves these symptoms due to hay fever or other upper respiratory allergies , runny nose , sneezing , itchy , watery eyes , itching of the nose or throat .
Warning If you have ever had an allergic reaction to this product or any of its ingredients or to an antihistamine containing hydroxyzine .
Dosage and Administration Adults and children 6 years andover One 10 mg tablet once daily ; do not take more than one 10 mg tablet in 24 hours .
A 5 mg product may be appropriate for less severe symptoms .
Adults 65 years and over : ask a doctor .
