Use Antiseptic Cleanser , Help decrease bacteria on skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable . Keep product away from fire or flame .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes ; in case of contact , flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops and persists .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Place enough product on your palm to thoroughly cover your hads .
Rub hands together briskly until dry .
Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
