DRUG FACTS Active ingredient Zinc oxide ( 20 % ) Purpose Sunscreen Uses Helps prevent sunburn .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use on damaged or broken skin .
If rash occurs , stop use and ask a doctor .
When using this product keep out of eyes .
Rinse with water to remove .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Ask a doctor for use on children under 6 months of age .
Directions Adults and children older than 6 months .
• Apply generously 15 minutes prior to sun exposure .
• Reapply every 2 hours or after swimming , towel drying , perspiring heavily , or washing .

Other Information Store at temperatures below 90 ° F .
