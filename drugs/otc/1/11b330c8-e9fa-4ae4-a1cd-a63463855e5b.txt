• Do not apply over large areas of the body or bandage .
• In case of deep or puncture wounds , animal bites or serious burns , consult a doctor .
Stop use and ask doctor if condition worsens ; or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days , clear up and occur again within a few days .
Do not use longer than one week unless directed by doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
If in eyes , flush with water for 15 minutes and call a physician .
Directions • Clean area .
• Apply to the bite area by pressing straight down repeatedly until liquid flows , then dab sparingly .
• If itching persists , apply again in 10 to 15 minutes .
• For ticks and bees , remove tick or stinger before treatment .
Other Information • Store at room temperature .
