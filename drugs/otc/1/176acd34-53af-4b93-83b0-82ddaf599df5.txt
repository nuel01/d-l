Active ingredient Salicylic acid 1 % Purpose Acne medication Uses • for the treatment of acne • clears blackheads Warnings For external use only .
When using this product • and other topical acne medication at the same time or immediately following use of this product , dryness or irritation of the skin may be increased .
If this occurs , only one medication should be used unless directed by a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Flammable , keep away from open fire or flame .
Directions • clean skin thoroughly before applying medication .
• moisten a cotton ball and smooth the cotton ball over your face one to three times daily .
