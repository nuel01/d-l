Drug Facts Active ingredients Chloroxylenol ( PCMX ) 0 . 6 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings For external use only When using this product • supervise children under 6 • do not use in the eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develop and persist for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Wet hands and forearms .
Apply 5 milliliters ( teaspoonful ) or palmful to hands and forearms .
Scrub thoroughly for 30 seconds .
Rinse and repeat .
