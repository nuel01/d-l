Active Ingredient Triclosan ( 1 % ) Purpose Antimicrobial Uses For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not get into eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask doctor if Skin or eye irritation persists for more than 72 hours Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .

• Wash vigorously all parts of the hand , fingers and nails for at least15 seconds .
