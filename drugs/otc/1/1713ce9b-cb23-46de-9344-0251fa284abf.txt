Drug Facts Active Ingredients ( each tablet ) Calcium Carbonate 420 mg Purpose Antacid Uses • relieves : heartburn • acid indigestion • sour stomach • relieves : upset stomach associated with these symptoms .
Warnings Ask a doctor or pharmacist before use if you are taking a prescription drug .
Antacids may interact with certain prescription drugs .
When using this product • do not take more than 19 tablets in a 24 hour period or use the maximum dosage of this product for more than two weeks except under advice and supervision of a doctor .
• If symptoms persist for more than two weeks , stop using this product and see a doctor .
Keep out of the reach of children .
Directions • Chew 2 tablets every 2 - 3 hours or as symptoms occur , repeat hourly if needed .
