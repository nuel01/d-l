Active ingredient ( s ) Sodium chloride , 9 . 0 mg / ml ( seawater derived ) Purpose Rinses and cleanses nasal passages Use For maintaining daily hygiene of the nasal cavity and rinsing away bacteria , viruses and impurities from the nose .
DIRECTIONS • Spray 1 - 2 times in each nostril , several times a day ( 2 - 6 times ) , and more often if necessary .
• Due to its gentle nature , the product is also suitable for everyday use to help maintain clear nasal passages ( drug free , non habit - forming , non - addictive ) .
WARNINGS • For nasal use only .
• To avoid contamination , do not touch the tip of the dispenser to any surface .
• Keep out of reach and sight of children .
WHEN USING THIS PRODUCT • For hygiene reasons , the product should only be used by one person ; do not share it with others .
OTHER INFORMATION Sealed for your protection .
Do not use if safety seal is broken or missing .
Do not expose to temperaturs exceeding 50 ° C / 122 ° F . Do not use the product after the expiry date .
