Uses - helps treat and prevent diaper rash - protects minor skin irritation due to diaper rash and helps seal out wetness Warnings For external use only .
When using this product - do not get into eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if : - condition worsens - symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within several days Directions - change wet and soiled diapers promptly - cleanse the diaper area and allow to dry - apply cream liberally as often as necessary with each diaper change , especially at bedtime or anytime when exposure to wet diapers may be prolonged Other information Store between 20 degrees to 25 degrees C ( 68 degrees to 77 degrees F ) Inactive Ingredients aloe barbadensis extract , benzyl alcohol , coconut oil , cod liver oil ( contains vitamin A and vitamin D ) , fragrance , glyceryl oleate , light mineral oil , ozokerite , paraffin , propylene glycol , sorbitol , synthetic beeswax , water [ MULTIMEDIA ] [ MULTIMEDIA ]
