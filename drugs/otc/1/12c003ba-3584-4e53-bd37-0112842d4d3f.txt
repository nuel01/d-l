Drug Facts Active Ingredient Salicylic Acid ( 0 . 5 % ) Purpose Acne treatment Uses • For the treatment of acne .
• Penetrates pores to eliminate most acne blemishes .
Warnings • Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
• For external use only .
Using other topical acne medication at the same time or immediately following the use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one medication should be used unless directed by a doctor .
Directions • Cleanse the skin thoroughly before applying medication .
• To unlock , hold collar while turning pump counterclockwise .
• Cover the entire affected area with a thin layer twice daily .
• If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to once a day or every other day .
