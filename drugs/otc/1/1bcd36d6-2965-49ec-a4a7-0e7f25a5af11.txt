Antifungal Uses • proven clinically effective in the treatment of athlete ' s foot ( tinea pedis ) , jock itch ( tinea cruris ) , ringworm ( tinea corporis ) • proven effective in the prevention of athlete ' s foot with daily use • for effectively relief of itching , burning and cracking Warnings For external use only When using this product avoid contact with the eyes .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • irritation occurs • there is no improvement within 4 weeks Do not use on children under 2 years of age except under the advice and supervision of a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
