Information for Patients This product is not to be administered orally ( mouth ) or in the ocular ( eye ) area .
If used improperly by oral administration the patient should be aware that the production of topical anesthesia may impair swallowing and thus enhance the danger of aspiration .
For this reason , any device ( including hands and fingers ) used to administer this product topically should be cleaned well before possible contact with eyes , intra - nasaly or mouth .
