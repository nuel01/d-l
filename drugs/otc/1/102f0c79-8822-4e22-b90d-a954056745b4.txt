Active ingredient Sodium fluoride 0 . 02 % ( 0 . 009 % w / v fluoride ion ) Purpose Anticavity Use • aids in the prevention of dental cavities Warnings Keep out of reach of children .
If more than used for rinsing is accidentally swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
