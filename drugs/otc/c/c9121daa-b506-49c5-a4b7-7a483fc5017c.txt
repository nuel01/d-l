Active Ingredient Triclosan 0 . 6 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses Helps reduce bacteria on the skin .
For external use only .
When using this product Avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop using this product and ask doctor if if irritation and redness develop .
Keep out of reach of children In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions Pump onto dry hands , work into a lather and rinse thoroughly .
