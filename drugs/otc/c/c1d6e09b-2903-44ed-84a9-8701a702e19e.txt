• Higher SPF gives more sunburn protection .
• Retains SPF after 80 minutes of activity in the water or perspiring .
Warnings For external use only When using this product Keep out of eyes .
Rinse with water to remove .
Stop use and ask a doctor if Rash or irritation develops and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , contact a Poison Control Center immediately or get medical help right away .
Directions Apply liberally before sun exposure .
• Reapply as needed or after towel drying , swimming or perspiring .
• Children under 6 months of age : ask a doctor .
Other Information High protection sun product .
Sun Alert : Limiting sun exposure , wearing protective clothing , and using sunscreens may reduce the risks of skin aging , skin cancer , and other harmful effects of the sun .
