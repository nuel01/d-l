Active ingredient Chloroxylenol 0 . 5 % Purpose Antiseptic handwash Uses • For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings • For external use only Do not use • In eyes .
When using this product • If in eyes , rinse promptly and thoroughly with water .
• Discontinue use if irritation and redness develop .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • Skin irritation and redness occurs for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Wet hands and forearms .
• Apply 5 ml ( teaspoonful ) or palmful to hands and forearms .
• Scrub thoroughly for 20 seconds .
• Rinse and repeat .
