Warnings For external use only Do not use • Allergy Alert : if you are allergic to any ingredients of this product or chili peppers contact a doctor before use • On wounds or to damaged skin • With , or at same time as , other analgesic products • With a heating pad When using this product • You may experience a burning sensation which is normal and related to the way the product works .

• Do not use immediately before or after activities such as swimming , showering , or sunbathing • Use only as directed .
• Avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes or rashes .
• Wash hands after putting product on body and before touching your face or eyes .
