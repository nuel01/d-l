Purpose First aid antiseptic Uses • First aid to help prevent the risk of infection in minor cuts , scrapes , and burns .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable , keep away from spark , heat and flame .
Ask a doctor before use for deep wounds , animal bites , or serious burns .
When using this product • Do not get into eyes • Do not apply over large areas of the body • Do not use longer than 1 week unless directed by a doctor Stop use and ask a doctor if condition persists or gets worse .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek professional assistance or contact a Poison Control center immediately .
