Drug Facts Active ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 80 % Purpose Antiseptic Use for hand washing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable , keep away from fire or flame .
Do not use in the eyes .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • Irritation and redness develop .
• Condition persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Wet hands thoroughly with product and allow to dry without wiping .
Other information • Do not store above 104 ° F ( 40 ° C ) .
• Keep the container tightly closed in a cool , well - ventilated place .
