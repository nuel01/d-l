Drug Facts Active ingredient Zinc Oxide ( 20 % w / w ) Purpose Skin Protectant Uses • helps treat and prevent diaper rash • dries the oozing and weeping of : • poison ivy • poison oak • poison sumac Warnings For external use only When using this product • do not get into eyes • do not use over large area of the body Stop Use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again in a few days • if you are allergic to any of these ingredients Do not use on • deep puncture wounds • animal bites • serious burns Keep out of reach of children .
