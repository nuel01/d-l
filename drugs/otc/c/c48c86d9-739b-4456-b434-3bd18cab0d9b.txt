Drug Facts Active ingredient Povidone Iodine 10 % , equal to 1 % available Iodine Purpose Antiseptic Uses First aid to help prevent infection due to minor cuts , scrapes and burns .
Keep out of reach of children if swallowed , get medical help of contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Hold the swab vertically , with the color ring tip upwards .
Hold in the center of the tube with one hand at the color ring with the other .
• Gently snap the tip with the color ring .
Formula inside the tube flows down and fills the opposite tip .
• Apply the product to the affected area .
• Discard swab after use .
Warnings For external use only .
Keep out of eyes and ear canal .
Non - Sterile .
Discontinue use if irritation occurs .
