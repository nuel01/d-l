Warnings For external use only Do not use for the treatment of diaper rash When using this product do not get into the eyes .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days • symptoms clear up and occur again within a few days Do not begin use of any other hydrocortisone product unless you have consulted a doctor .
