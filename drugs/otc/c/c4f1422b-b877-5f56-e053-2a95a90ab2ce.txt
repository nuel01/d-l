Active ingredients Chloroxylenol 0 . 3 % Antibacterial Use for handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings : For external use only : hands only .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops and condition persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions : Apply onto wet hands , lather for at least 30 seconds and rinse thoroughly .
Other information : Avoid freezing and excessive heat above 40 ℃ ( 104 ℉ ) .
