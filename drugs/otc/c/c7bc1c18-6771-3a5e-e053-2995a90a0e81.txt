Active Ingredient Ichthammol USP 10 % Purpose Drawing Salve Uses for drawing or removing splinters , silvers , or similar items .
Warnings For external use only Do not use in eyes Ask a doctor before use if you have deep puncture wounds or serious burns Stop use and ask doctor if redness , irritation , swelling or pain persists .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Cleanse affected area .
Apply on gauze bandage once or twice a day .
Repeat as necessary .
Do not rub or massage affected area .
Other information Store at room temperature .
