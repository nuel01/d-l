Active Ingredients Benzethonium Chloride - 0 . 20 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses • Handwash to decrease bacteria on the skin .
• Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings • For external use only .
• When using this product , do not use in or near eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes throroughly with water .
Avoid contact with broken skin .
Do not inhale or ingest .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Wet hands and forearms .
Apply one pump to hands and forearms thoroughly for 15 seconds or more .
Rinse and repeat .
For children under 6 , use only with adult supervision .
