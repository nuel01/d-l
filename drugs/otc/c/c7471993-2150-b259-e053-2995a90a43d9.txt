Drug Facts Active ingredients Capsaicin 0 . 25 % Menthol 5 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Uses For the temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with : • simple backache • arthritis • strains • bruises • sprains .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product • avoid contact with the eyes and other mucous membranes • do not apply to wounds or damaged skin • do not bandage tightly • use only as directed Do not use • more than one patch at a time • at the same time as other topical analgesics Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Adults over 18 years : • use one patch .
