Active ingredient Calcium Carbonate USP 500 mg Purpose Antacid Uses relieves : • heartburn • sour stomach • acid indigestion • upset stomach associated with these symptoms Warnings Ask a doctor or pharmacist before use if you are presently taking a prescription drug .
Antacids may interact with certain prescription drugs .
When using this product • • do not take more than 15 tablets in 24 hours • • If pregnant do not take more than 10 tablets in 24 hours • • do not use the maximum dosage for more than 2 weeks except under the advice and supervision of a doctor .
Keep out of Reach of Children Directions • • Adults and children 12 years of age and over : chew 2 - 4 tablets as symptoms occur , or as directed by a doctor .
• • Do not swallow tablets whole , • • Do not take for symptoms that persist for more than 2 weeks unless advised by a doctor .
Other Information • • Each tablet contains : elemental calcium 200 mg • • Store at room temperature .
Keep the container tightly closed Safety sealed : Do not use if printed seal under cap is torn or missing .
