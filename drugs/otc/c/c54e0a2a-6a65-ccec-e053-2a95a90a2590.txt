Salicylic Acid 2 % Acne Treatment Uses Treats acne .
Helps clear acne blemishes , blackheads , and whiteheads .
Helps prevent new acne blemishes .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use on damaged or broken skin or on large areas of the body .
Using other topical acne products at the same time or right after use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one drug should be used unless directed by a doctor .
Stop use and ask a doctor if skin irritation or sensitivity develops or if irritation increases .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help and contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Precautions Do not spray on face .
Avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water .
If you experience irritation , rinse from skin and discontinue use .
Protect skin daily with a broad - spectrum sunscreen rated SPF 25 or greater .
Ask a pediatrician before using this product on children .
