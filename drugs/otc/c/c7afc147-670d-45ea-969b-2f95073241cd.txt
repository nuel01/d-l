Active Ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 1 % Purpose Antiseptic Use To decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product keep out of eyes .
In case of contact eyes with water .
Stop use and consult a doctor if irritation or redness develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Pump onto dry skin .
Lather vigorously for 20 seconds .
• Rinse hands and dry thoroughly .
