Drugs Facts Active Ingredients Purpose Benzocaine 20 % Anesthetic Uses For temporary relief of oral pain associated with : · toothache · canker sores · minor dental procedures · minor injury of the mouth and gum caused by dentures or orthodontic appliances .
Directions Apply with cotton applicator or finger tip to affected gum area up to 4 times a day or as directed by your dentist .
Do not use for more thatn 7 days .
Warnings Do not use this product if you have a history of allergy to local anesthetic such as Procaine , Benzocaine or other “ Caines ” .
Not recommended for children under the age of 2 .
