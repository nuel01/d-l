Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Antiseptic Uses Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Water ( aqua ) , propylene glycol , hydroxyethylcellulose , phenoxyethanol , alcohol Directions Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces . Rub hands together until dry .
For external use only .
Do not use on open skin wounds or children under 2 months of age .
When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
ln case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs . These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a poison control center immediately .
Other information Store in a cool dry place , away from direct sunlight and heat .
