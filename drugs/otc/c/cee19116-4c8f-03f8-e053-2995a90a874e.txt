Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 70 % v / v .
Purpose Antiseptic Uses To decrease bacteria on skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
Do not use • On open skin wound .
• On children less than 2 months of age .
When using this product keep out of eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash appears .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • Wipe hands thoroughly with product and allow to dry .
• Children under 6 years of age must be supervised when using this product .
OTHER INFORMATION • Store below 40 ° C ( 104 ° F ) .
