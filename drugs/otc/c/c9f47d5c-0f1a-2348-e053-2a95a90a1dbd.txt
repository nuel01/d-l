Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 066 % Purpose Disinfectant Use Hand sanitizing to help reduce bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
When using this product avoid contact with the eyes .
In case of contact , flush eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develops and persists .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • Put enough product in the palm to cover hands & rub together briskly the liquid is dry .
