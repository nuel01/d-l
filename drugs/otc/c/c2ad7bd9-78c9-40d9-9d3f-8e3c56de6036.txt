Active Ingredient Benzocaine 20 % ...................................................... Oral Anesthetic Uses For the temporary relief of occasional minor irritation and pain associated with canker sores and minor dental procedures .
Warnings • Keep out of reach of children • If more than used for pain is accidentally swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
• Do not use if you have a history of allergy to local anesthetic such a procaine , butacaine , benzocaine or other " caine " anesthetics .
• Flammable .
Do not use near fire or flame .
Avoid smoking during use .
• Avoid spraying in eyes .
Dosage A single metered spray dispenses 50 mg of Opahl - S Topical Anesthetic .
Directions • Do not exceed recommended dosage • Firmly insert extension tube into spray can valve and ensure it fits securely into the actuator opening • position the extension tube 1 - 2 inches from the affected area .
• Spray 1 / 2 second ( press and immediately release the actuator ) • Anesthesia is accomplished in approximately 20 seconds .
Other Information • Do not store at room temperature above 49 oC ( 120 oF ) • For professional use only .
