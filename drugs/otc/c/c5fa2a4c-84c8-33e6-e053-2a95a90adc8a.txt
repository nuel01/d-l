Drug Facts Active ingredient Zinc oxide 4 . 9 % Purpose Skin protectant Uses • helps treat and prevent diaper rash • protects minor skin irritation due to diaper rash and helps seal out wetness Warnings For external use only When using this product • do not get into eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
