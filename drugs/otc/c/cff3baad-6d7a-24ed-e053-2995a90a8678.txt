sodium chlorite citric acid , water Disinfectant · Deodorizer KEEP OUT OF REACH OF THE CHILDREN How to Use ( How to Activate ) - The stick contains a main liquid solution and an ampoule inside that includes active ingredients in powder form .
Bend the middle part of the stick to break the ampoule .
Once the ampoule inside the stick cracks , sufficiently shake the stick to mix the powder with the solution well .
As it turns yellow , the stick is activated and the pure chlorine dioxide is released through the surface of the stick .
After 3 ~ 4 weeks of usage , the stick will turn transparent which indicates that it is no longer effective .
Please dispose the used stick into a general trash bag .

■ Do not use in eyes .
