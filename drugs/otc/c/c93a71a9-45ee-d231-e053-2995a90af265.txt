Active Ingredients Menthol 4 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Uses Temporarily relieves minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with arthritis .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use on wounds or damaged skin and avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed consult a physician .
Directions Adults and children 12 years of age and older .
Apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
Children under 12 years of age - consult a physician .
May stain fabrics .
Inactive Ingredients Chamomile oil , cotton seed oil , emulsifying wax , jojoba oil , magnesium oxide , olive oil , purified water , sage oil , shea butter , silver stearate , sorbic acid ( preservative ) , vitamin B oil , zinc oxide .
