Drug Facts Active Ingredient Organic Ethyl Alcohol 70 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses • Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat and flame .
When using this product avoid contact with face , and broken skin .
In case of eye contact , flush with plenty of water and seek medical advice .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center .
Directions • Pour onto hands , wetting throughly with product .
Rub into skin until dry .
• Children under 6 years of age should be supervised by an adult when using this product .
Inactive Ingredients : Purified Water ( Aqua ) , Lavandula Angustifolia Oil * , Propanediol , Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice * , Fragrance , Hydroxyethylcellulose , Isopropyl Myristate , AminoMethyl Propanol .
