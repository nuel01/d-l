Active ingredient Benzalkonium chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antiseptic Use For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin Warnings For external use only .
When using this product • Avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • Irritation or redness develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Pump into wet hands .
• Lather vigorously for at least 20 seconds .
• Rinse & dry thoroughly .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
