Drug Facts Active ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 62 % Purpose Antiseptic Use Help reduce bacteria on skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable . Keep product away from fire or flame .
Do not use on damaged or broken skin .
Keep out of eye .
In case of eye contact , immediately flush with water then contact physician .
Stop use and ask a doctor if rash occurs .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Wet hands thoroughly with product and rub hands together briskly until dry .
