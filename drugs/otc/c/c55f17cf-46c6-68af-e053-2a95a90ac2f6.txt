Acne Treatment Using other topical acne medications at the same time or immediately following the use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one drug should be used unless directed by a doctor .
Avoid direct contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , flush throughly with water .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed get medical help or contact Poison Center right away .
