DRUG FACTS Active Ingredients Kaolin 4 . 0 % Purpose Skin Protectant Uses ▪ Helps prevent diaper rash ▪ Protects chafed skin ( or minor skin irritation ) due to diaper rash ▪ Helps protect from wetness that causes diaper rash ▪ Temporarily helps relieve minor skin irritation due to diaper rash .
Warnings For external use only Do not use on damaged or broken skin .
When using this product keep out of eyes .
Stop use and ask a doctor if ▪ condition worsens ▪ symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact Poison Control Center right away .
Directions ▪ Change wet and soiled diapers promptly ▪ Cleanse the diaper area ▪ Allow to dry ▪ apply ointment liberally as often as necessary , with each diaper change , especially at bedtime or any time when exposure to wet diapers may be prolonged .
Other Information Keep in a cool place to prevent the paste from melting .
