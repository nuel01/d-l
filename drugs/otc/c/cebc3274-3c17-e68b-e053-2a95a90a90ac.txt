Active Ingredients Methyl Salicylate 15 % Lidocaine 4 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Use For temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with : • arthritis • simple backache • muscle strains • sprains • bruises • joint pain Warnings For external use only Use only as directed .
Read and follow all directions and warnings on this carton .
Avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes .
Do not use • at the same time as other topical analgesics • on open wounds , cuts , damaged or infected skin • with bandage or heating pad Consult Doctor before use Allergy alert : If prone to allergic reaction from aspirin or salicylates , consult a doctor before use .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days • If pregnant or breast - feeding or if you have sensitive skin , ask a health professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact Poison Control Center right away .
