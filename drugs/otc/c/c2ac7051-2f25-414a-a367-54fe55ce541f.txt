Active Ingredient Triclosan 0 . 6 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses * For washing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
For external use only .
When using this product * avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse with water .
Stop using this product and ask doctor if if irritation or redness develops and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact Poison Control Center immediately .
