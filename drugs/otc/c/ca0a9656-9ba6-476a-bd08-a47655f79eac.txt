Warning For external use only – hands Flammable Keep away from fire and flame Do not use • on children less than 2 months of age .
• on open skin wounds .
When using this product keep out of eyes , ears and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be a sign of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right way .
Directions Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands until dry .
Supervise children under 6 years of age who are using this product to avoid swallowing .
