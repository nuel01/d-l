Drug Facts Active Ingredient Isopropyl Alcohol 70 % v / v Purpose Antiseptic Uses Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
WARNING Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
This product is intended for external use only .
When using this product , do not use in or near the eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Discontinue use if irritation or redness develops .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours , consult a doctor .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
DIRECTIONS • Apply enough product in your palm to thoroughly cover your hands .
• Rub hands together briskly until dry .
• Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
