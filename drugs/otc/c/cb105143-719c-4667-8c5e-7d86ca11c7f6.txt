Active Ingredient Triclosan 0 . 3 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses for washing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product Avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush with water .
Do n't use this product on infants under 6 months of age .
Stop use and ask a doctor If irritation and redness develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • wet bar with water • lather vigorously and wash skin • rinse and dry thoroughly .
