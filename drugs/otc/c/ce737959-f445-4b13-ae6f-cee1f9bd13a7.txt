• - For external use only .
• - If condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days , discontinue use of this product and consult a physician .

• - Pregnancy - Breast Feeding Warning : If pregnant or breast feeding , ask a physician before use .

• - Do not use with heating devices .

• - KEEP THIS AND ALL MEDICINE OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN .

Directions : Adults and children 12 years and older • - Apply and gently massage painful joints and / or muscles .
