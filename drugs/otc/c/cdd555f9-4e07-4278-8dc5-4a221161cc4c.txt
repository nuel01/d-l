Active ingredient Triclosan 0 . 5 % Purpose Antiseptic handwash Uses For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product Do not use in the eyes .
If accidental eye contact occurs , rinse with water .
If eye irritation persists , get medical attention .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develop .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours , consult a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Use as part of a daily cleansing routine .
To decrease bacteria on the skin , apply sufficient amount to cover hands with product .
Wash hands .
Rinse hands thoroughly with potable water .
