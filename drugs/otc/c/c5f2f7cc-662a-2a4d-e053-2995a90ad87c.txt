Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % w / w Purpose Antiseptic Uses For hand washing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only When using this product do not use in or near the eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash appears and lasts Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
