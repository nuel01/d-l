Directions Apply liberally and evenly onto face and / or body prior to sun exposure and massage into skin for complete coverage .
Avoid the eye area .
Leave ot dry and apply a second time to ensure a generous application .
An insufficient amount of product reduces the level of protection .
Re - apply frequently to maintain protection , especially after swimming , perspiring , or using a towel .
Over - exposure to tht sun is dangerous .
Keep babies and young children out of direct sunlight , and well covered .
Do not stay too long in the sun , even while using a sunscreen product , because it does not provide you 100 % protection .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse them immediately and thoroughly .
