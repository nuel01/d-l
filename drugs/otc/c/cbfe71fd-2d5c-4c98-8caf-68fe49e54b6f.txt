Avoid contact with the eyes Do not use • if you are allergic to any of the ingredients • over large areas of the body Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 1 week , or clear up and occur again within a few days • rash or other allergic reaction develops Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
