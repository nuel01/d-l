Dosage and Admnstration Directions : Refill your container .
1 - 4 pumps into your palm .
Rub foam until spread evenly over the entire surface of your hands .
Continue rubbing until dry .
Use frequently to avoid spreading germs .
Warnings Warnings : For External Use Only Do not use in eyes .
In case of eye contact , immediately flush with water and seek medical advice .
Discontinue use if skin irritation or redness develops .
Not for use on sensitive areas of the body or burns .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a poison control center right away .
