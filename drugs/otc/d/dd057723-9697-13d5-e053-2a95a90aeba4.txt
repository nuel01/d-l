Directions Apply liberally 15 minutes before sun exposure Reapply : after 80 minutes of swimming or sweating , immediately after towel drying , at least every 2 hours Sun protection measures .
Spending time in the sun increases your risk of skin cancer and early skin aging .
To decrease this risk , regularly use a sunscreen with a Broad Spectrum SPF value of 15 or higher and other sun protection measures including : • limit time in the sun , especially from 10 am to 2 pm • wear long sleeved shirts , pants , hats and sunglasses Children under 6 months : ask a doctor Inactive Ingredients aloe barbadensis leaf juice , carbomer , cetyl alcohol , disodium EDTA , fragrance , gluconolactone , glycerin , glyceryl stearate , oleth - 4 phosphate , PEG 100 stearate , phenoxyethanol , polyethylene , propylene glycol , sodium benzoate , sodium hydroxide , theobroma cacao seed butter , tocopheryl acetate , water , xanthan gum Other Information Protect product from excessive heat and direct sun .
