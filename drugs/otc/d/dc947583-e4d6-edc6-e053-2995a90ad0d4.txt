Active ingredient Alcohol 62 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses Decrease bacteria on hands .
Warnings Flammable : Keep away from heat of flame .
For external use only .
Do not use • In children less than 2 months of age • On damaged skin or broken skin When using this product Avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor If irritation and redness develop or increase .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Cente right away .
Directions • Rub a dime size drop into hands .
