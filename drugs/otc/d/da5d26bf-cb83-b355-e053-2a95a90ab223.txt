ACTIVE INGREDIENT SALICYLIC ACID 10 % PURPOSE WART REMOVER USES • WART REMOVER WARNINGS FOR EXTERNAL USE ONLY .
DO NOT USE ON CHILDREN UNDER 2 YEARS OF AGE UNLESS DIRECTED BY A DOCTOR .
WHEN USING THIS PRODUCT AVOID CONTACT WITH THE EYES .
IF CONTACT OCCURS , RINSE THE EYES VERY THOROUGHLY WITH WATER .
STOP USE AND ASK A DOCTOR IF IRRITATION OCCURS OR THERE IS NO IMPROVEMENT WITHIN 4 WEEKS .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN .
IF SWALLOWED , GET MEDICAL HELP OR CONTACT A POISON CONTROL CENTER RIGHT AWAY .
DIRECTIONS Simply unscrew the top and fill the dropper tip with liquid .
Once full , apply Warticide to the wart or warts until the infected area is lightly covered with gel .
Gently work Warticide into the wart with a finger and then wash hands after application is finished .
Allow 3 - 4 minutes for the solution to absorb .
