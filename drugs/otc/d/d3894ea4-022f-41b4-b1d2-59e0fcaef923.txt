Antiseptic Antiseptic / Antimicrobial hand sanitizer spray • Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin that could cause disease • Recommended for repeated use Warnings Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only .
When using this product do not use in or near eyes .
In case of eye contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if skin irritation or redness develops and persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of the Reach of Children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
