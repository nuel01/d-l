Drug Facts Active Ingredients Thymol 0 . 05 % Purpose Oral Analgesic / Antimicrobial Uses : Relieves sore throat pain , pain associated with canker sores , inflamed throat and mouth tissue , encourage healing of irritated mouth and throat sores and helps to reduce infection .
Warnings : Stop use and ask your dentist or doctor if swelling , rash or fever develops .
Keep out of reach of children Do not administer to children under 3 years of age unless directed by your doctor .
Directions : For oral use only as directed by your health care professional .
Shake gently prior to each use .
Open mouth wide and insert applicator into mouth .
Spray 2 to 3 pump sprays into mouth and throat area then swallow .
Repeat every few hours or as needed .
Other Information for cancer patients : For use by cancer patients being treated with chemotherapy and / or radiation .
For relief of lesions of the oral cavity associated with Muscositis .
Safe and effective treatment for muscitis pain in patients with cancer .
Spray in mouth , let stand for a few seconds then swallow .
Other Information : This formula is dark red in color and may temporarily color the mouth area .
This is normal .
Store between 20 - 25 ° C ( 68 - 77 ° F ) .
Do not refrigerate Inactive Ingredients : Croton Lechleri , Deionized Water , Glycerin , Glycyrrhiza Leaf Extract , Honey , Lavandula Angustifolia Extract , Salvia Officinalis [ MULTIMEDIA ] [ MULTIMEDIA ]
