Petrolatum 36 % Skin Protectant Temporarily protects and helps relieve chafed , chapped , or cracked skin .
For external use only .
Do not use on deep or puncture wounds .
When using this product avoid contact in eyes , rinse with water to remove , contents under pressure , do not puncture or incinerate , do not store at temperatures above 120 F .
Stop use and ask a doctor if condition worsens , symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days .
Ask a healthcare or professional before use .
If swallowed , seek medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Shake well .
Hold 4 - 6 inches from the skin and apply a thin continuous coating .
Apply as needed .
