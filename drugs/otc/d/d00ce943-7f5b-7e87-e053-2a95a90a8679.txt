Product Information / Drug Facts Directions : Shake well before use .
Apply liberally 15 minutes before sun exposure .
Children under 6 months old : Ask a doctor .
Re - apply every 2 hours .
Use water - resistant sunscreen if swimming or sweating .
Store in a cool dry place .
Warnings / Cautions / Sun safety : Spending time in the sun increases your risk of skin cancer or early skin ageing .
This product has been shown only to prevent sunburn , not skin cancer or early skin ageing .
Do not use on damaged or broken skin .
When using this product keep out of eyes .
Rinse with water to remove .
Stop use and ask a doctor if rash occurs .
Always wear a hat , protective clothing and sunglasses .
Keep out of reach of children .
Use : Helps prevent sunburn , If used with other sun protection measures decreases the risk of skin cancer .
