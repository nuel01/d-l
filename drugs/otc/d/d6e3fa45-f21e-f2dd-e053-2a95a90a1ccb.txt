Active Ingredient - Ethyl Alcohol 62 % v / v Purpose - Antbacterial Agent Uses - Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin that could cause disease .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flames .
Avoid contact with eyes .
If eye contact occurs , flush with water .
Stop use if , in rare instances , redness or irritation develop .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours , consult a physician .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , contact a physician or poison control center .
Directions Apply small amount to palm .
Briskly rub , covering hands with product until dry .
Inactive Ingredients - Water , PEG - 10 Dimethicone , Glycerin , Isopropyl Myristate , Polyquaternium - 11 , Disodium EDTA , Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice , Tocopheryl Acetate .
