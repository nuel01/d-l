Active Ingredients Menthol USP 4 % Camphor USP 2 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Uses For the temporary relief of pain Warnings FOR EXTERNAL USE ONLY .
Do not use : • On eyes or on mucous membranes • On wounds , damaged or irritated skin • If you are allergic to Menthol , Camphor or any ingredients listed below When using this product : • Use only as directed • Do not bandage or cover with any type of wrap except clothing • Do not use with heating pad or devices , or apply external heat Stop use and ask a doctor if : • Localized skin reactions occur , such as rash , itching , redness , irritation , pain , swelling and blistering • Conditions worsen • Symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days If pregnant or breastfeeding : Do not use this product .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions : • Rub a thin layer into affected areas up to 4 times daily .
