Active ingredient ( each gram contains ) Bacitracin zinc 500 units Purpose First Aid Antibiotic Uses first aid to help prevent infection in minor • cuts • scrapes • burns Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • if you are allergic to any of the ingredients • in the eyes • over large areas of the body Ask a doctor before useif you have • deep or puncture wounds • animal bites • serious burns Stop use and ask a doctor if • you need to use longer than 1 week • the condition persists or gets worse • a rash or other allergic reaction develops If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) right away .
