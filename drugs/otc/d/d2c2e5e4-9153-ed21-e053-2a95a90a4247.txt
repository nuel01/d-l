Drug Facts Active ingredient Ethanol 62 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses • For hand washing to decrease the bacteria on skin .
• Recommended for repeated use Warnings Flammable , keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops , or if condition persists for more than 72 hours .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
