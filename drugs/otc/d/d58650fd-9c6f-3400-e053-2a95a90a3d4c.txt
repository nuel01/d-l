Dosage and Administration Before use , spray a short stream in the air or on intact skin to test spray pattern and to clear nozzle .
To dispense , aim nozzle at the affected area and spray from a distance of 2 to 4 inches , or as is comfortable .
Cover with clear cap after each use Warnings Do not use on burn injuries .
Do not use in body orifices , ear canals , eyes or mouth .
Not for injection .
For individual use only .
Apply as needed .
Consult a healthcare professional for continued wound care management and for use on children younger than 2 years of age .
