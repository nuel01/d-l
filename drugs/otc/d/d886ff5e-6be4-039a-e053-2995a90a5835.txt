Active Ingredients Lidocaine HCl 0 . 5 % Phenol 0 . 5 % Purpose Topical Anesthetic Antiseptic Uses • first aid to help prevent the risk of skin infection in minor cuts , scrapes , or burns .
Warnings For external use only Do not use in eyes , do not apply over large areas of the body .
In case of deep puncture wounds or serious burns , consult a doctor .
Stop use and ask a doctor if redness , irritation , swelling , or pain persists or increases or if infection develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Clean the affected area .
Apply a small amount of this product ( an amount equal to the surface area of the tip of a finger ) on the area 1 to 3 times daily .
May be covered with sterile bandage .
Other Information • Store at controlled room temperature 15ºC to 30ºC ( 59ºF to 86ºF ) .
