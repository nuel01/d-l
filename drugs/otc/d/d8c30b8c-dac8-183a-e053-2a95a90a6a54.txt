Drug Facts Active ingredients Menthol 0 . 44 % Zinc Oxide 20 . 6 % Purpose External analgesic / Anti - itch Skin protectant / Anorectal astringent Uses A moisture barrier that prevents and helps heal skin irritation from : • urine • diarrhea • perspiration • fistula damage • feeding tube site leakage • wound drainage ( peri - wound skin ) • minor burns • cuts • scrapes • itching Warnings For external use only • not for deep or puncture wounds • avoid contact with eyes Stop use and ask a doctor • if condition worsens or does not improve within 7 days .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion contact a physician or poison control center immediately .
Directions • cleanse skin gently with mild skin cleanser • pat dry or allow to air dry • apply a thin layer of ointment to reddened or irritated skin 2 - 4 times daily , or after each incontinent episode or diaper change to promote comfort and long lasting protection .
