Active Ingredient Isopropyl alcohol 70 % v / v Purpose Antiseptic Use To help reduce bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammability warning : Keep away from heat or flame .
Do not use on children less than 2 years of age ( unless directed by a doctor ) .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop use and consult a doctor if irritation develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Dispense in your palm and rub hands together briskly until dry .
