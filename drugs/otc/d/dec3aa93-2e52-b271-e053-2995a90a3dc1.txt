Active ingredient Salicylic Acid 0 . 5 % Purpose Acne treatment Uses Treats acne Dries up acne pimples Helps prevent new acne pimples Warnings For external use only When using this product using other topical acne medications at the same time or immediately following the use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one medication should be used unless directed by a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Clean the skin thoroughly before applying • Cover the entire affected area with a thin layer 1 to 3 times daily • Avoid contact with eyes .
• Because excessive drying may of the skin may occur , start with 1 application daily , then gradually increase to 2 or 3 times daily if needed or as directed by a doctor • If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to once a day or every other day .
