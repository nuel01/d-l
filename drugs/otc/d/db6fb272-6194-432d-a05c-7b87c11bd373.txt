Drug Facts Active ingredient Salicylic Acid 2 % Purpose Acne treatment gel Uses • for the treatment of acne • dries up acne pimples • helps prevent new acne pimples Warnings For external use only When using this product • using other topical acne medications at the same time or immediately following use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one medication should be used unless directed by a doctor .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • irritation becomes severe Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
