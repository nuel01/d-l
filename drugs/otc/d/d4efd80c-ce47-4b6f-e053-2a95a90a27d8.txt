WARNINGS For External Use Only DOSAGE & ADMINISTRATION Adults and children over 12 years : clean and dry affected area .
Carefully remove backing from patch .
Apply sticky side of patch to affected area .
Wear one patch up to 8 hours .
Repeat as necessary , no more than 4 times daily , no more than 7 consecutive days .
Children 12 years or younger , consult a physician .
