Active Ingredient Salicylic Acid .... 2 % Purpose Atopic Dematitis treatment Uses Relieves and helps prevent • itching • irritation • redness due to jock itch Warnings For external use only When using this product avoid contact with eyes .

Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Apply to the affected area one to three times a day or as directed by a doctor .
