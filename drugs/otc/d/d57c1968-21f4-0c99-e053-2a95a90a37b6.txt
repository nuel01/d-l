Warnings For external use only .
Ask a doctor before use if you have • deep puncture wounds • animal bites • serious burns When using this product • do not use in or near the eyes • do not apply over large areas of the body or in large quantities • do not apply over raw surfaces or blistered areas • avoid prolonged storage above 40 C ° ( 104 ° F ) Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days , or clear up and occur again within a few days .
Keep out of reach of children .
if swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
