Usage : Intended for use as a general purpose topical antiseptic for first - aid treatment of minor cuts and abrasions .
In case of deep or puncture wounds consult a physician .
Discontinue use and get medical attention if redness , irritation or infection develops .
Active ingredient : hydrogen peroxide , 3 % w / v in water .
For External Use Only .
Do not use in eyes or over large areas of the body .
Keep out of the reach of children .
