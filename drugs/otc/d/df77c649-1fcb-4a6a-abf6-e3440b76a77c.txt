If the symptoms being treated does not subside or if redness , irritation , swelling , bleeding or other symptoms develop or increase , discontinue use and ask a doctor .
Do not use • in or near the eyes • in children under 2 years of age When using this product • do not use more than directed unless directed by a doctor • do not put this product into the rectum by using fingers or any mechanical device or applicator Stop use and ask a doctor if • bleeding occurs • condition worsens or does not improve within 7 days If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
