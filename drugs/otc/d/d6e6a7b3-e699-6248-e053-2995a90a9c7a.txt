Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 65 % Purpose Antimicrobial Uses Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek professional assistance or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Avoid contact with eyes .
In case of contact , flush thoroughly with water .
Do not inhale or ingest .
Avoid contact with broken skin .
Discontinue use if irritation or redness develops .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours , consult a doctor .
Directions Apply product onto hands , briskly rub hands together until dry without wiping .
For children under 6 , use only under adult supervision .
Inactive Ingredients purified water , glycerin , carbomer , aloe .
