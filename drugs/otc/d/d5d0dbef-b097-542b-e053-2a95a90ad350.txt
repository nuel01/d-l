Indications for use : For hospital and professional use only .
Medi - Fect is intended to be used as a hand - wash to reduce bacteria that can potentially cause disease .
Recommended for repeated use .
Ingredients : 70 % v / v ethyl alcohol , propylene glycol , emolients ( polysorbate 80 , cetyl alcohol , acetylated lanolin alcohol ) , carbomer , diazolidinyl urea , methyl paraben , aloe vera and propyl paraben .
Contains emollients and skin conditioners .
Contains no added fragrance or dyes .
