Ear drying aid Use dries water in the ears and relieves water - clogged ears after • swimming • showering • bathing • washing the hair Warnings Flammable : Keep away from fire or flame Do not use in the eyes Ask a doctor before use if you have • ear drainage or discharge • ear pain , irritation or rash in the ear • dizziness • recently had ear surgery Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation ( too much burning ) or pain occurs Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) right away .
