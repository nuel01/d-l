Pain Reliever Uses for the temporary relief of pain and discomfort caused by boils Warnings For external use only Do not use for more than 3 days Ask a doctor before use if you have boils on the lips , nose , cheeks , or forehead When using this product avoid contact with the eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if • fever occurs • redness around the boil develops • condition worsens or does not improve • symptoms presist for more than 3 days • symptoms clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of the reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
