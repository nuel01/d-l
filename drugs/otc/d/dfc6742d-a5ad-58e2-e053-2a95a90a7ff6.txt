Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 70 % v / v Purpose Antiseptic Use For hand washing , to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable : Do not while smoking or near heat of flame When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
If product gets into eyes , rinse with water to remove .
Avoid contact with broken skin .
Do not inhale or ingest .
Stop use and ask a doctor if rash or irritation develops and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • Wet hands thoroughly with product and allow drying without wiping .
