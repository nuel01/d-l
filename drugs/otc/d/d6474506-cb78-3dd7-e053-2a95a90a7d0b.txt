This is a hand sanitizer manufactured according to the Temporary Policy for Preparation of Certain Alcohol - Based Hand Sanitizer Products During the Public Health Emergency ( CoViD - 19 ) ; Guidance for Industry .
The hand sanitizer is manufactured using only the following United States Pharmacopoeia ( USP ) grade ingredients in the preparation of the product ( percentage in final product formulation ) consistent with World Health Organization ( WHO ) recommendations : • Benzalkonium Chloride ( 0 . 23 % w / v ) ( active ingredient ) • Glycerol ( 2 % v / v ) • Lauramine Oxide ( 1 , 5 % v / v ) • Disodium Cocoamphodiacetate ( 2 % v / v ) • Hexylene Glycol ( 1 % v / v ) • Sodium Chloride ( 0 . 4 % v / v ) • PEG - 150 Distearate ( 3 % v / v ) • Cocamidopropyl Betaine ( 5 % v / v ) • Cetrimonium Chloride ( 0 . 3 % v / v ) • Magnesium Nitrate ( 0 . 0018 % v / v ) • Methylchloroisothiazolinone and Methylisothiazolinone ( 0 . 0014 % v / v ) • Tetrasodium EDTA ( 0 . 03 % v / v ) • Citric Acid ( 0 . 05 % v / v ) • Sterile distilled water or boiled cold water .
The firm does not add other active or inactive ingredients .
Different or additional ingredients may impact the quality and potency of the product .

Directions • Wet your hands with clean water . Take some Deepfresh Antibacterial liquid soap and foam it .
Thoroughly rub your hands between fingers , all inside and outside surfaces , nails .
