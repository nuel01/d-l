Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 1 % Purpose Antimicrobial Uses Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin that could cause disease Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops , or if condition persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Pump a small amount of gel into the palm of hand Rub thoroughly over all surfaces of both hands Rub hands together until dry .
