Active Ingredient Colloidal Oatmeal 0 . 02 % Purpose Skin Protectant Uses Temporarily protects and helps relieve minor skin iritation and itching due to eczema .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product do not get into eyes .
Stop use and ask a doctor if : • Conditions worsen • Symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days .
Keep out of reach of children .
