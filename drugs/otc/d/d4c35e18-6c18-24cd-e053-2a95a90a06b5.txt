Drug Facts Active ingredient Chloroxylenol 0 . 5 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses • For hand washing to decrease the bacteria on skin .
• Recommended for repeated use Warnings For external use only Do not use in the eyes .
In case of contact , flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • irritation and redness develop • condition persists for more than 72 hours Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
