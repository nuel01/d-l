Drug Facts Active ingredients Ethyl alcohol 70 % v / v Purpose Antimicrobial Use • Hand Sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially cause disease .
• For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings Flammable • Keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only .
When using this product • Do not use it in or near the eyes • In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if skin irritation occurs Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help in a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions Put enough product in your palm to cover hands and rub hands together briskly until dry .
Other information • Store below 110 ° F ( 43 ° C ) • May discolor certain fabrics Inactive Ingredients Triethanolamine , Carbomer , Polysorbate , Water , Glycerin .
