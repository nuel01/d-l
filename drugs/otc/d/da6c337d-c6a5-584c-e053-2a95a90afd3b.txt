CVS Povidone - Iodine Drug Facts Active Ingredient Povidone - Iodine 10 % ( 1 % Available iodine ) Purpose First Aid Antiseptic Uses First aid to help prevent infection in in minor : cuts , scrapes and burns .
Warnings For external use only Do not use in the eyes .
Over large areas of the body .
Ask a doctor before use in case of deep or puncture wounds , animal bites , or serious burns .
Stop use and consult a doctor if : The condition persists or gets worse .
Need to use this product for more than 1 week .
Keep out of reach of children .
• If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Clean the affected area .
Apply a small amount to the affected area 1 to 3 times daily .
May be covered with a sterile bandage .
If bandaged , let dry first .
Inactive Ingredient Citric acid , Dibasic sodium Phosphate , Glycerin , Nonoxynol - 9 , Purified water , sodium hydroxide .
