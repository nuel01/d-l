DIRECTIONS Usual dosage is one treatment per tooth .
After prophylaxis treatment , remove the pellicle of the concerned tooth by using 2 % sodium hypochlorite for 10 seconds .
rinse the tooth surface with water .
Remove inorganic deposits by pushing together the two cylinders and apply product with sponge on treatment area .
After treatment time of five minutes have patient expectorate residues .
For maximum benefits for the prevention of caries , instruct patient not to rinse , eat or drink for 30 minutes .
For patients with rampant or active caries , repeat treatment once or twice every six months .
