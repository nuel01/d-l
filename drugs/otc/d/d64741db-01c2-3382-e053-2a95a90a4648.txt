This is a hand sanitizer manufactured according to the Temporary Policy for Preparation of Certain Alcohol - Based Hand Sanitizer Products During the Public Health Emergency ( CoViD - 19 ) ; Guidance for Industry .
The hand sanitizer is manufactured using only the following United States Pharmacopoeia ( USP ) grade ingredients in the preparation of the product ( percentage in final product formulation ) consistent with World Health Organization ( WHO ) recommendations : • Alcohol ( ethanol ) ( USP or Food Chemical Codex ( FCC ) grade ) ( 75 % , volume / volume ( v / v ) ) in an aqueous solution denatured according to Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau regulations in 27 CFR part 20 .
• DICAPRYLYL CARBONATE ( 0 . 01 % v / v ) .
• OCTADECANOIC ACID , ( OCTADECYLIMINO ) DI - 2 , 1 - ETHANEDIYL ESTER ( 0 . 1 % v / v ) .
• POLYSORBATE 20 ( 0 . 2 % v / v ) .
• GLYCERYL OLEATE ( 0 . 01 % v / v ) .
• LAURYL GLUCOSIDE ( 0 . 1 % v / v ) .
• FATTY - ACID AMIDE HYDROLASE ( 0 . 1 % v / v ) .
• GLYCERIN ( 0 . 5 % v / v ) .
• PANTHENOL ( 0 . 1 % v / v ) .
• Sterile distilled water or boiled cold water .
The firm does not add other active or inactive ingredients .
Different or additional ingredients may impact the quality and potency of the product .
