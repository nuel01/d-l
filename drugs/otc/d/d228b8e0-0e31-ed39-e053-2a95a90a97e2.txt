Purpose Moisturizer Properties Sorafine ® Cremogel is a hydrating and nourishing Cream - Gel for oily and acne prone skin .
Soothing jojoba extract .
Indications Serafine ® Cremogel is used as a moisturizing and nourishing Gel - Cream for oily and acne prone skin , it is recommended for dry and sensitive skin , eczema , flaky skin , and similar skin problems .
Serafine ® Cremogel prevents skin dryness and reduces roughness , relieves itching , gives a feeling of comfort and softness .
Serafine ® Cremogel is distinguished by its non - fat composition , and contains jojoba oil which is suitable for oily skin , because of the similarity between its composition and the natural oils of the skin ; which gives you a wonderful feeling of softness .
Precautions Keep out of reach of children Warnings • For external use only • Do not apply to broken or irritated skin .
Contraindications Hypersensitivity to any of the components .
Side effects Sorafine ® Cremogel has no known side effects , it is used safely .
Dosage and administration Apply Sorafine ® and spread it well , it will help the skin to offset the loss of natural oils by washing or using certain medications , and also helps to stop and prevent irritation that soap can cause to the skin .
Can be used as often as needed .
