Drug Facts Active Ingredients Salicylic acid 2 % Purpose Acne treatment Uses • For the management of acne .
• Helps clear up acne blemishes .
Warnings For external use only .
• Using other topical acne medications at the same time or immediately following the use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one medication should be used unless directed by a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Wet face with lukewarm water .
Create lather in palms , then massage face with foam to cleanse .
Rinse thoroughly .
For best results , use with DHC Acne Spot Therapy .
