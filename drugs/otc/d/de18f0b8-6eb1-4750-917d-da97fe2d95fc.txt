Drug Facts Active ingredient 0 . 75 % Triclosan Purpose Antiseptic Use Healthcare Personnel Handwash to decrease transient bacteria on the skin before contact with patients under medical care or treatment .
Warnings For external use only Discontinue use if irritation and redness develop .
If irritation persists for more than 3 days , consult a physician .
When using this product do not get it in the eyes ; causes eye irritation upon direct contact .
In case of eye exposure , rinse thoroughly with water .
If eye irritation persists , contact a physician .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek professional assistance or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions Apply a small amount to hands .
Wash hands , rinse thoroughly with water , and repeat as necessary between patient contacts .
