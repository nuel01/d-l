WARNINGS • For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from sources of fire or flame .
• When using this product do not use near eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
• Stop use and ask a doctor if skin irritation develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , seek medical advice or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
DIRECTIONS • Apply onto palm of hand .
• Cover hands with product by thoroughly rubbing and allow to air dry without wiping .
• Use undiluted .
• Supervise children under 6 years • Not recommended for infants OTHER INFORMATION • Store below 86 ° F / 30 ° C • Keep out of direct sunlight .
