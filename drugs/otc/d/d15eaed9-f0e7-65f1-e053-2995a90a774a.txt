Purpose Moisturizer Properties Aquax ® Cream is an emollient and moisturizer that nourishes the skin .
Indications Aquax ® Cream is used for dry and sensitive skin , in case of eczema and other similar skin problems ; Aquax ® Cream helps prevent further water loss from the skin , preventing thereby skin dryness and reduces roughness , relieves itching and gives a sensation of comfort .
Contraindications Hypersensitivity to any of the components .
Side effects Aquax ® Cream has no known side effect , because using it is safe for all members of the Family , Children and Adults .
Dosage and Administration Apply Aquax ® Cream on the skin and spread it well , it will help the skin to offset the loss of natural oils caused by washing , and prevents irritation that soap can cause to the skin .
Aquax ® Cream can be used as often as needed .
Precaution Keep out of reach of children Warnings • For external use only • If you are allergic to any of the ingredients , you should check with your doctor or pharmacist before you use the product .
