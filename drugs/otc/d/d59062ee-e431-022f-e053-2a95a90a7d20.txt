Drug Facts Active ingredient ( in each 15 mL ) Phenylephrine HCI 2 . 5 mg Purpose Nasal Decongestant Uses ■ temporarily relieves : ■ stuffy nose ■ sinus congestion and pressure ■ temporarily restores freer breathing through the nose Warnings Do not exceed recommended dosage .
Do not use ■ in a child who is taking a prescription monoamine oxidase inhibitor ( MAOI ) ( certain drugs for depression , psychiatric or emotional conditions , or Parkinson ’ s disease ) , or for 2 weeks after stopping the MAOI drug .
If you do not know if your child ’ s prescription drug contains an MAOI , ask a doctor or pharmacist before giving this product .
Ask a doctor before use if the child has ■ heart disease ■ high blood pressure ■ thyroid disease ■ diabetes Stop use and ask a doctor if ■ nervousness , dizziness , or sleeplessness occur ■ symptoms do not improve within 7 days or occur with a fever Keep out of reach of children .
In case of overdose , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
