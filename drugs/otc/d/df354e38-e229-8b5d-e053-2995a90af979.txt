• Do not use if you are allergic to any of the ingredients .
• Do not use longer than 1 week unless directed by a doctor Stop use and ask a doctor : • if the condition persists or gets worse , or if a rash or other allergic reaction develops .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions : • clean the affected area • apply a small amount of this product ( an amount equal to the surface area of the tip of a finger ) on the area 1 to 3 times daily • may be covered with a sterile bandage Other Information : • Tamper Evident .
