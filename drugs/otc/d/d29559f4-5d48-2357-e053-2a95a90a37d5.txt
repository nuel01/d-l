Active ingredient Lidocaine 4 % w / w Purpose Topical Anesthetic Uses For the temporary relief of pain .
Warnings For external use only Flammable Keep away from sparks , heat and fire .
Cap tube tightly when not in use .
Stop use and ask a doctor if : • allergic reaction occurs • condition worsens or does not improve within 7 days • symptoms clear up and return within a few days • you notice any unusual effects Keep out of the reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Apply externally to affected area up to 3 to 4 times a day .
Children under 2 years of age consult a doctor .
