Drug Facts Active ingredient Benzalkonium chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose First aid antiseptic Uses • Antiseptic cleansing of face , hands and body without soap and water .
• First aid to help prevent infection in minor cuts , scrapes and burns .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • in the eyes • over large area of the body Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation , redness or other symptoms develop or gets worse .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
