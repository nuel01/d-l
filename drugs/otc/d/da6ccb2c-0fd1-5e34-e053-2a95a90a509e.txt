Adults & Children over 12 yrs of age : tilt head to the side and place 10 drops into the ear canal .
Tip of applicator should not enter into the ear canal Keep drops in ear for several minutes by keeping head tilted sideways or placing cotton in the ear .
Use twice daily for up to 4 days if needed , or as directed by a doctor .
Any wax remaining after treatment may be removed by gently flushing the ear with warm water , using a soft rubber bulb ear syringe .
Children under 12 yrs of age : Consult a physician .
For occasional use as an aid to soften , loosen and remove excessive earwax .
Earwax Removal Aid Warnings For external use only Ask a doctor before use if you have : ear drainage or discharge ear pain , irritation , or rash in the ear recently had surgery dizziness an injury or perforation ( hole ) of the eardrum Carbamide Peroxide 6 . 5 % Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Extract Chamomilla Recutita ( Matricaria ) Flower Extract Glycerin Mineral Oil Oxyquinoline Sulfate Purified Water Keep this and all drugs out of the reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
