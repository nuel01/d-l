Sun Protection Measures .
Spending time in the sun increased your risk of skin cancer and early skin aging .
To decrease this rick , regularly use a sunscreen with a broad spectrum SPF of 15 or higher and other sun protection measure including : • Limit time in the sun , especially from 10 a . m . - 2 p .
m . • Wear long sleeve shirts , pants , hats and sunglasses .
