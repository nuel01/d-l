Active Ingredient : Chloroxylenol 0 . 2 % Purpose : Antimicrobial Uses : For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings • For external use only .
• Avoid contact with eyes .
In case of contact , flush thoroughly with water .
• Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops .
Warnings • Keep out of children ' s reach except under adult supervision .
In case of accidental ingestion contact a physician or Poison Control Center rightaway .
Directions : • Dispense into hands , wet as needed .
• Lather vigorously for at least 15 seconds • Wash skin , rinse thoroughly and dry .
