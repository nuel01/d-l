Active Ingredient Purpose Sodium monofluorophosphate 0 . 76 % ---------------------------------------------------- Anticavity ( Total Fluoride Content - 1000 ppm Approx . )
Uses Regular brushing with fluoride toothpaste helps protect teeth and roots against cavities Warnings Warnings Keep out of reach of children under 6 years of age .
In case of accidental overdose , seek professional assistance or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions Adults and children 2 years and older Brush Teeth thoroughly after meals , twice a day , or use as directed by a dentist or physician .
Children under 6 years To minimize swallowing use a pea - sized amount and supervise brushing until good habits are established .
Children under 2 years Ask a dentist or physician .
