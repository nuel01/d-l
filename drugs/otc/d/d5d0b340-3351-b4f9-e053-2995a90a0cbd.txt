Indications for Use Indications for use : AloeSafe is intended to be used as a waterless hand sanitizer to reduce microorganisms that can cause disease .
Recommended fo repeated use .
Ingredients Ingredients : Ethyl alcohol 62 % ( active ingredient ) , purified water , carbomer , aloe vera gel , diisopropylamine , vitamin E , and lavender oil .

Warnings Warnings : Flammable , keep away from fire or flames .
For external use on hands only .
Avoid contact with face , eyes and broken skin .
If this occurs , flush thoroughly with water .
Discontinue use if irritation or redness develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of ingestion contact Poison Control Center immediately .
