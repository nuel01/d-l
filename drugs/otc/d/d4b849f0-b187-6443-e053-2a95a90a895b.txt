Active ingredient BENZALKONIUM CHLORIDE 0 . 13 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses • For handwashing to reduce bacteria on the skin .
Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings For external use only Avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush eyes with water .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops and persists .
Directions • Apply foam sanitizer to hands .
• Rub over surfaces of both hands for 15 seconds .
• No rinsing required .
Inactive ingredients Water , Coco - Glucoside , Laurtrimonium Chloride , Cocamidopropylamine Oxide , Citric Acid , Fragrance .
