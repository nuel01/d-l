Active Ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride , 0 . 13 % Purpose First Aid Antiseptic USE To prevent infection in minor cuts , scrapes and burns Warnings : For external use only Do not use : • For deep puncture wounds , animal bites , or serious burns • In eyes or over large area of the body Stop use and ask a doctor if : • Irritation , redness , or symptoms develop • the condition persists or gets worse Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions : Tear pouch and clean the affected area .
