Active Ingredients Iodine 2 % , Sodium Iodide 2 . 4 % , Alcohol 47 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses To help prevent infection in minor cuts , scrapes and burns .
WARNINGS : For external use only .
DIRECTIONS : Clean the affected area .
Apply a small amount to the affected area 1 to 3 times daily .
May be covered with a sterile bandage .
If bandaged , let dry first .
Inactive ingredient : Purified Water .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek professional assistance or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
