Active Ingredient Sodium fluoride 0 . 20 % ( w / w ) Purpose Anticavity Use • aids in the prevention of dental cavities Warnings Keep out of reach of children under 6 years of age .
Do not use on patients with a milk protein or hydroxybenzoates allergy .
In case of allergic reaction ; stop use , rinse mouth with water and seek medical advice .
If you accidentally swallow more than used for brushing , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away Warnings Keep out of reach of children under 6 years of age .
Directions • adults and children over 12 : brush teeth with your normal toothpaste at least twice a day , or as directed by your dental professional or doctor , rinse mouth after brushing , dispense a pea - sized portion of MI Paste Plus onto your finger , apply thoroughly , coating the upper teeth with an even layer , repeat for lower teeth , allow to remain on teeth for 3 minutes .
Do not rinse .
