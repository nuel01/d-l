Purpose Antiseptic handwash Use for handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin Warnings For external use only Flammable Keep away from fire or flame , heat , sparks , and sources of static discharge .
When using this product If in eyes , rinse promptly and thoroughly with water discontinue use if irritation and redness develop Do not use in eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if eye or skin irritation or redness occurs for more than 72 hours .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
