Drug Facts Active ingredient Alcohol 70 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses • Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable , keep away from fire and flame .
Do not use • on children less than 2 months of age • on open skin wounds When using this product • keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develops and persists .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , contact a doctor or Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Apply a dime sized amount to hands .
Rub hands together until dry .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
