Active ingredient Benzoyl Peroxide 2 . 5 % Purpose Acne Treatment Uses for treatment of acne Warnings For external use only Do Not Use • if you have very sensitive skin • are sensitive to benzoyl peroxide .
When Using This Product • avoid unnecessary sun exposure and use a sunscreen • avoid contact with the eyes , lips , mouth • avoid contact with hair and dyed fabrics , which may be bleached by this product • skin irritation may occur , characterized by redness , burning , itching , peeling , or possibly swelling .
Irritation may be reduced by using the product less frequently or in a lower concentration Stop and Ask a Doctor • if irritation becomes severe .
Keep Out of Reach of Children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Use every evening .
Wet face with water .
Apply a dime - sized amount into hands and gently wash your face ; take care to avoid the eye area .
Rinse well .
• If going outside , apply sunscreen after using this product .
If irritation or sensitivity develops , stop use of product and ask a doctor .
