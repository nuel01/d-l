Active Ingredient Lidocaine Hydrochloride 4 % Purpose External Analgesic Use For temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints .
Warnings For external use only .
If condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days , discontinue use of this product and consult a physician .
Do not use in large quantities , particularly over raw surfaces or blistered areas .
Avoid contact with the eyes .
Rinse with water to remove .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN .
Directions Apply 2 pumps to foot where pain is felt .
Apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
Children under 2 years of age : consult a physician .
