Drug Facts Active ingredient Alcohol 70 % ( v / v ) Purpose Antiseptic Uses • helps reduce bacteria on hands that potentially can cause disease • for use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable , keep away from heat and flame .
Do not use • on children less than 2 months of age • on open wounds When using this product • keep out of eyes , ears and mouth • in case of eye contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be a sign of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces • rub hands together until dry .
• for children under 6 , use adult supervision .
