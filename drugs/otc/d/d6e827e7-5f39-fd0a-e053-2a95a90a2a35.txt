Directions Apply a small amount , covering hands with product for 30 seconds .
Add water , lather and rinse .
After use , hands are to be thoroughly rinsed with potable water before handling food in the plant .
Avoid contamination of food during use .
Uses : For handwashing to decrease bacteria on skin .

Active Ingredient 0 . 13 % Benzalkonium Chloride Keep Out Of Reach Of Children Warnings : For external use only .
Avoid contact with eyes .
In rare instances of redness or irritation , discontinue use .
If condition persists for more that 72 hours , consult a physician or poison control center .
