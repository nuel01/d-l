Active Ingredients Ethyl Alcohol 80 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses • For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin • recommended for repeated use Warnings For external use only Flammable , keep away from fire or flame .
Contents under pressure Do not puncture of incinerate Do not expose to heat or store at temperatures above 120 oF / 50 oC or in enclosed places that could overheat .
Do not use in or near eyes .
In case of contact rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if , irritation and redness develop and persists for more than 72 hours Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • spray in palm of hand ans rub over entire surface of hands • briskly rub hands together until dry • supervise children in the use of this product • spray product generously over hands , rub together until product drys .
