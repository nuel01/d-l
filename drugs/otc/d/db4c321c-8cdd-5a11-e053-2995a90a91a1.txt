ACTIVE INGREDIENTS Methyl Salicylate 30 % Menthol 10 % Camphor 4 % PURPOSE TOPICAL PAIN RELIEF USES Temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with arthritis , backache , strain , sprains , and bruises .
WARNINGS Avoid contact with eyes .
If condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days , discontinue use of this product and consult a doctor .
Do not use in large quantities , particularly over raw surfaces or blistered areas .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN .
INACTIVE INGREDIENTS 1 , 2 - Hexanediol , Acrylates / C10 - 30 Alkyl Acrylate Crosspolymer , Caprylhydroxamic Acid , Capryloyl Glycerin / Sebacic Acid Copolymer , Dimethyl Isosorbide , Menthyl Ethylamido Oxalate , Propanediol , Steareth - 2 , Steareth - 21 , Stearyl Alcohol , Stearyl Glycyrrhetinate , Vanillyl Butyl Ether , Water , Xanthan Gum , Blue 1 , Yellow 5 , Green 3 DIRECTIONS Adults and children over 12 : Apply a thin layer and massage into painful area until thoroughly absorbed .
Repeat as necessary , but no more than 4 times daily .
After applying , wash hands with soap and water .
Children under 12 : Ask a doctor .
