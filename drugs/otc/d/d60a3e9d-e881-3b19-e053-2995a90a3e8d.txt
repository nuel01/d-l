Warnings WARNINGS : For external use only .
When using this product : Discontinue use if excessive irritation of the skin develops .
Avoid contact with eyes and mucus membranes .
Do not bandage tightly .
Do not use with heating pad .
Stop use and ask a doctor if : ▪ Condition worsens .
▪ Severe skin irritation occurs .
▪ Pain persists for more than seven days .
▪ Pain clears up and the recurs within a few days .
If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health care professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control immediately .
Uses For temporary relief of the minor aches and pains in muscles and joints related to rheumatism , arthritis , backaches , strains , sprains , sports and other minor injuries .
