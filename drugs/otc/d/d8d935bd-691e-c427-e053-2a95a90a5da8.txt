Drug Facts Active ingredient Alcohol 80 % v / v Purpose Antiseptic Uses • Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin that potentially can cause disease .
• For use when water soap & towel are not available .
Warnings Flammable , Keep away from heat or flame .
For external use only .
Do not use • In or near the eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
• On children less than 2 months of age .
• On open skin wounds .
Stop use and ask a physician , if irritation or rash appears and lasts .
Directions • Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
Supervise children less than 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Other information • Store between 59 - 86 ° F ( 15 - 30 ° C ) .
• Avoid freezing and excessive heat above 104 ° F ( 40 ° C ) .
