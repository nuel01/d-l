Product Information / Drug Facts Directions : Shake well before use .
Apply liberally 20 minutes before sun exposure .
Re - apply your BB every 1 - 2 hours , after swimming or exercise .
Store in a cool dry place .
Warnings / Cautions / Sun safety : When using this product keep out of eyes .
If contact occurs rinse with water to remove .
Stop use and seek medical advise if rash occurs .
Wear sun - smart options such as hat and sunglasses .
Do not use on damaged or broken skin .
Can aid in the prevention of premature skin ageing .
Keep out of reach of children .
Use : Helps prevent sunburn , If used with other sun protection measures decreases the risk of skin cancer .
