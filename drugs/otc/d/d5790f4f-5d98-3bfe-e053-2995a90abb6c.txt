Active Ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 115 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin Warnings For external use only Do not use • in the eyes • if you are allergic to any of the ingredients When using this product if eye contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if if irritation and redness develops and persist for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children if swallowed get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) right away .
Directions • Storage , Store at room temperature • Dispensing Remove lid and throat seal .
Replace lid and lift tab at front of lid .
Pull up corner of center sheet and thread through dispenser slit in lid .
Close lid to retain moisture .
• Use .
Apply wipe thoroughly to hands as desired .
Allow to dry without wiping .
• Disposal .
Dispose of used wipes in a trash receptacle after use .
Do not flush .
