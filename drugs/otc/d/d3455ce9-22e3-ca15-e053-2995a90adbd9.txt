Drug Facts Active Ingredient Ethyl alcohol 68 % v / v Purpose Antimicrobial Use Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin .
Warnings Non - medical , for external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from the fire or flame .
When using this product do not use in or near the eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash appears and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Centre right away .
Inactive Ingredients Aqua , Glycerol , Carbomer , Triethanolamine .
Direction Put enough product in your palm to cover hands and rub hands together briskly until dry .
