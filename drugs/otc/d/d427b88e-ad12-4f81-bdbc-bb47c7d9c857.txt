Active ingredient Chloroxylenol 1 . 0 % Purpose Antiseptic handwash Uses For hand washing to decrease bacteria on skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use in the eyes .
In case of contact , immediately flush with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develop .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours , consult a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Wet hands and apply liberal quantity to hands .
Lather hands thoroughly .
Make sure product contacts all skin surfaces .
Rinse hands thoroughly with potable water .
