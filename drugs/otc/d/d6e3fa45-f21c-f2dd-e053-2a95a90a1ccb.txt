Active Ingredient - Benzalkonium Chloride Purpose - Antibacterial Uses - For handwashing to decrease bacteria on skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , flush with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if , in rare instances , redness or irritation develop and persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , contact a physician or poison control center .
Directions - Apply small amount , covering hands with product for 30 seconds .
Add water , lather , and rinse .
Inactive Ingredients - Water , Laurtrimonium Chloride , Cocamidopropyl Betaine , PEG - 6 Cocamide , Cocamidopropyl PG - Dimonium Chloride Phosphate , Fragrance , Methylisothiazolinone , Iodopropynyl Butylcarbamate , Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice , Tocopheryl Acetate , Yellow 5 , Red 33 .
