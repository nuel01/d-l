Drug Facts Active ingredient Sodium monofluorophosphate 0 . 76 % ( 0 . 1 % w / v fluoride ion ) Purpose AntiCavity Toothpaste Use aids in the prevention of dental cavities Warning Keep out of the reach of children under 6 years of age .
If more than used for brushing is accidentally swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately Directions • Do not swallow • Supervise children as necessary until capable of using without supervision • Instruct children under 12 years of age in good brushing and rinsing habits ( to minimize swallowing ) • Adults and children 6 years of age and older : Brush teeth thoroughly , preferably after each meal or at least twice a day , or as directed by a dentist or doctor • Children under 6 years of age : Do not use unless directed by a doctor or dentist Inactive Ingredients Sorbitol , Hydrated Silica , Demineralized Water , Sodium Lauryl Sulphate , Sodium Bicarbonate , Sod .
