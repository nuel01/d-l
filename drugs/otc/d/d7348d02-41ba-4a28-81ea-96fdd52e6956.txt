Purpose First aid antiseptic Uses first aid to help prevent the risk of infection in minor cuts , scrapes and burns Warnings For external use only .
• if taken internally , serious gastric disturbances will result Flammable , keep away from fire or flame .
• use only in a well - ventilated area ; fumes may be toxic Ask a doctor before use if you have deep or puncture woods , animal bites , or serious burns .
When using this product • do not get into eyes • do not apply over large areas of the body • do not use longer than one week unless directed by a doctor Stop use and ask a doctor if condition persists or gets worse Keep out of reach of children .
In case of ingestion , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
