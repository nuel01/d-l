Active Ingredient Triclosan 0 . 15 % Purpose Antibacterial Use ■ For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only When using this product ■ Avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develops .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact Poison Control Center right away .
Directions ■ Pump into hands , wet as needed .
■ Lather vigorously for at least 15 seconds ■ Wash skin , rinse thoroughly and dry .
