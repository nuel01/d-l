Active Ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antibacterial Use For hand sanitizing to reduce bacteria on the skin .
WARNINGS For external use only When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops .
keep out of reach of children Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a poison control center right away .
Directions ● Pump into hands , wet as needed .
● Lather vigorously for at least 15 seconds .
● Wash skin , rinse thoroughly and dry .
Inactive ingredients water , cetrimonium chloride , glycerin , lauramidopropylamine oxide , cocamide MEA , sodium chloride , PEG - 120 methyl glucose dioleate , citric acid , tetrasodium EDTA , methylchloroisothiazolinone , methylisothiazolinone .
