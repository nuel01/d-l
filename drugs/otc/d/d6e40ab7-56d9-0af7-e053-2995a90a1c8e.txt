Active Ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % w / w Purpose Antibacterial Agent Uses Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin that could cause disease .
Warnings For external use only .
Avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , flush with water .
Stop use if , in rare instances , redness or irritation develop .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours , consult a physician .
Directions To decrease bacteria on skin , apply a small amount to palm .
Briskly rub , covering hands with product until dry .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , contact a poison control center .
