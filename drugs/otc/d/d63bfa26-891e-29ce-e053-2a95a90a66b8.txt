Active Ingredients Ethyl Alcohol 75 % Purpose Uses Hand Sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on skin .
Warnings For external use only . Keep out of reach of children . Children must be supervised if using this product .
Flammable . Keep away from fire or flame .
Avoid contact with eyes . Discontinue use if irritation or redness occurs . If irritation continues for more than 72 hrs , please consult a doctor .
Directions Place enough in your palm to thoroughly spread on both and rub into the skin until dry .
Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
