This is a hand sanitizer and surface disinfectant manufactured , tested , and registered in the EU .
The hand sanitizer is manufactured using only the following ingredients in the preparation of the product ( percentage in final product formulation ) : • BAC ( Benzalkonium Chloride ) less than 1 . 6 % • DDAC ( Didecyldimonium Chloride ) less than 0 . 4 % • Purified water The firm does not add other active or inactive ingredients .
Different or additional ingredients may impact the quality and potency of the product .
