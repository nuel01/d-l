Recommended for single use .
Do not freeze .
For external use only , Store at room temperature .
Do not use in ears , eyes or mouth .
Avoid contact with the eyes .

Stop use and ask a doctor if redness or irritation develop and persist for more than 72 hours , ● Keep out of reach of children .
