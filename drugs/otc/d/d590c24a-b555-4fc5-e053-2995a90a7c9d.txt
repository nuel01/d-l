Warnings For external use only .
Do not inhale or ingest .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame when using this product .
Do not use • in children less than 2 months of age • on open skin wounds When using this product keep away from eyes .

Stop use and ask a physician if skin irritation occurs .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • Apply to hands thoroughly and rub vigorously until dry .
• For children under 6 years , use only with adult supervision .
