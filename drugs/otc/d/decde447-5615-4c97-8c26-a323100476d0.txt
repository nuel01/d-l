ACTIVE INGREDIENT Titanium Dioxide 5 % PURPOSE SUNSCREEN USES Helps prevent sunburn .
WARNINGS If rash occurs stop use and seek medical attention .
Do not use on broken or damaged skin .
When using this product keep out of eyes .
Rinse with water to remove .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a poison control center right away .
For external use only .
Keep out of reach of children .
Sun Alert Limiting sun exposure , wearing protective clothing , and using sunscreens may reduce the risks of skin aging , skin cancer , and other harmful effects of the sun .
DIRECTIONS Apply generously 15 minutes before sun exposure .
Reapply at least every 2 hours .
For use on children less than 6 months of age , consult a health care professional .
Use a water resistant sunscreen if swimming or sweating .
