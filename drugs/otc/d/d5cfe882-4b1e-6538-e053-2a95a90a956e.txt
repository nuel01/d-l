Active Ingredient Isopropyl Alcohol , 70 % Directions Apply directly to skin .
Discontinue use and consult a physician if irritation , redness or infection develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
Warnings Danger : Highly flammable liquid and vapor .
Keep away from heat , sparks , open flames and hot surfaces .
No smoking .
Keep container tightly closed .
Use only non - sparking tools .
Take precautions against static discharge .
Wear protective clothes and eye protection .
In case of fire , use fire extinguishers approved for alcohol fires .
Other Information Keep tightly closed and protected from light .
Store at room temperature .
For external use only .
Does not contain ethyl alcohol .
