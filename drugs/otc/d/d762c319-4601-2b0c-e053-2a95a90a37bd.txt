Durg Facts Active ingredient Benzalkonium chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antiseptic Use For hand washing to decrease bacteria on the skin Warnings For external use only Do not use in the eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if • irritation and redness develop • condition persists for more than 72 hours Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Wet hands and forearms .
Apply 5 milliliters ( teaspoonful ) or palmful to hands and forearms . scrub thoroughly for at least 30 seconds .
Rinse and repeat .
