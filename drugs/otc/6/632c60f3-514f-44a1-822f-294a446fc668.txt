When using this product • use only as directed • do not swallow • do not bandage tightly Stop use and ask a doctor • if condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) right away .
Directions Adults and children 12 years of age and older : Apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
Children under 12 years of age : consult a doctor .
