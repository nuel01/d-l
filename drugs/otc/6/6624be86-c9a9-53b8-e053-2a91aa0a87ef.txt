Active ingredient Sodium Fluoride 0 . 24 % ( fluoride ion 0 . 15 % w / v ) Purpose Anticavity Use aids in the prevention of cavities Warnings Keep out of reach of children under 6 years of age .
If more than used for brushing is accidentally swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
