Active Ingredients DRUG FACTS Active Ingredients : Chloroxylenol 1 . 0 % w / w Purpose Purpose : Antibacterial Keep Out Of Reach Of Children Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek medical attention or contact poison control center immediately .
Uses Uses • Hand wash to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause diseases • Help prevent cross contamination by hand contact • Recommended for repeat use Warnings Warnings : For external use only - Harmful if swallowed .
When using this product : Do not use in or near eyes .
If eye contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Discontinue use if irritation or redness develops .
If irritation persists for more than 72 hours , consult a physician .
Directions Directions : For one step hand washing and sanitizing , wet hands with water .
Apply a generous amount of product in palm of hand and work into lather .
Rinse thoroughly with potable water .
Dry hands completely .
