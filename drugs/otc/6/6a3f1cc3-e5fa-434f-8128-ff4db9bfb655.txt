SPORTSCREME ® Deep Penetrating Pain Relieving Rub Active ingredient Trolamine Salicylate 10 % Purpose Topical analgesic Uses temporarily relieves minor pain associated with : • arthritis • simple backache • bursitis • tendonitis • muscle stains • muscle sprains • bruises • cramps Warnings Allergy alert : Do not use if you are allergic to salicylates ( including aspirin ) unless directed by a doctor .
When using this product • use only as directed • do not bandage tightly or use with a heating pad • avoid contact with the eyes or mucous membranes • do not apply to wounds or damaged skin Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days • redness is present • irritation develops If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
