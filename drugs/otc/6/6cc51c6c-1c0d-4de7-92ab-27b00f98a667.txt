Active ingredients Diphenhydramine HCl 2 % Zinc Acetate 0 . 1 % Purpose External analgesic Skin protectant Uses • for the temporary relief of pain and itching associated with minor skin irritations • dries the oozing and weeping of poison : ivy , oak , sumac Warnings For external use only Flammable Keep away from fire of flame .
Do not use • on large areas of the body • with any other product containing diphenhydramine , even one taken by mouth Ask a doctor before use on chicken pox or measles When using this product do not get into eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if condition worsens or symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • do not use more than directed • adults and children 2 years of age and older : apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily • children under 2 years of age : ask a doctor Other information • store at 20 ⁰ - 25 ⁰ C ( 68 ⁰ - 77 ⁰ F ) Inactive ingredients alcohol , glycerin , povidone , purified water , tris ( hydroxymethyl ) aminomethane Questions & comments ?
