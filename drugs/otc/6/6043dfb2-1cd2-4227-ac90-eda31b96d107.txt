Drug Facts Active ingredient Purified water USP 97 % W / V Purpose Eyewash Use for flushing the eye to remove loose foreign material .
Warnings For external use only Do not use • • if solution changes color or becomes cloudy • • if you have open wounds in or near the eyes .
Get medical help right away .
When using this product • • do not touch tip of container to any surface to avoid contamination • • do not reuse ; once opened , discard Stop use and ask a doctor if • • continued redness or irritation of the eye Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Flush the affected eye as needed , controlling the rate of flow of solution by pressure on the bottle .
