Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 62 . 0 % Purpose Antimicrobial Uses - Helps reduce bacteria on the skin that could cause disease .
Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
When using this product do not use in or near the eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if : irritation and redness develops and lasts more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions - Place enough product in palm to thoroughly cover your hands .
Rub hands together briskly until dry .
Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
Other information : Store below 110 Fahrenheit degree ( 43 degrees Celsius ) May discolor certain fabrics or surfaces .
