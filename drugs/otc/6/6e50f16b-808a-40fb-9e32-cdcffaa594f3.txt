Use only as directed .
Excessive use or prolonged exposure may cause irritation to skin .
Discontinue use if rash , redness , or itching occurs .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat and flame .
When using this product • Keep out of eyes .
In case of contacting with eyes , immediately flash with water and call a doctor .
• Avoid contacting with a broken skin .
Stop using and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek professional assistance or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
