Drug Facts Active ingredient Povidon - Iodin 10 % ( w / w ) ( equivalent to 1 % titratable iodine ) Purpose Antiseptic Uses • First aid antiseptic to help prevent infection in minor cuts , scrapes and burns Warnings • For external use only Do not use • in the eyes or apply over large areas of the body • longer than 1 week unless directed by a doctor • on individuals who are sensitive or allergic to iodine Stop use and ask a doctor if • irritation and redness develop • condition persists more than 72 hours • in case of deep or puncture wounds , animal bites or serious burns Keep out of reach of children .
