Drug Facts Active Ingredients Trolamine salicylate 10 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Uses Temporary relief of pain associated with : • Joint and Muscle Inflammation • Nerve damage • Fitness and Sports Injuries Warnings For external use only .
Allergy alert If prone to allergic reaction from aspirin or salicylates , consult a doctor before use .
When using this product use only as directed • do not bandage tightly or use with a heating pad • avoid contact with the eyes or mucous membranes • do not apply to wounds or damaged skin Stop use and ask a doctor if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days • redness is present • irritation develops • condition worsens If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a healthcare professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , seek immediate medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
