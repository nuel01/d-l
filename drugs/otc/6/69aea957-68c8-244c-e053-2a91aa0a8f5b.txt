Active Ingredient Salicylic Acid 0 . 5 % Purpose Acne treatment Use for the treatment of acne Warnings ■ For external use only Keep out of eyes ■ If contact with eyes occurs , immediately flush with water .
Using other topical acne drugs at the same time or right after use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one drug should be used unless directed by a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children If accidentally ingested seek medical help immediately or contact your local Poison Control Center .
Directions ■ use twice a day ■ wet face ■ apply to hands , add water and work into a lather ■ massage face gently , then rinse thoroughly .
