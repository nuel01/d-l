Salicylic Acid 1 % Acne Tratment If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Clears up acne blemishes and helps prevent new acne blemishes .
For external use only .
Directions Cleanse skin thoroughly before applying medication . Cover the entire affected area with a thin layer 1 to 3 times dally .
Because excessive drying of the skin may occur , start with 1 application daily , then gradually increase to 2 to 3 times daily if needed or as directed by a physician .
If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to once a day or every other day .
