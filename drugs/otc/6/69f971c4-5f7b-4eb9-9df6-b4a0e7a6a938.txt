Active ingredients Avobenzone 1 . 5 % Octocrylene 7 % Terephthalylidene dicamphor sulfonic acid 0 . 5 % Titanium dioxide 2 . 5 % Warnings For external use only .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse thoroughlly with water .
Do not use on broken skin .
Stop use and ask a doctor if rash occurs .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed get medical help or contact a poison control center right away .
Directions Adults and children over 6 months and up .
Apply generously and evenly 15 minutes before sun exposure .
Reapply at least every 2 hours , after 40 minutes of swimming or sweating and immediately after towel drying .
