Drug Facts : Active Ingredients Salicylic Acid : 2 % Sulfur : 3 % Purpose Acne treatment Use Moisturizer for acneic skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use on damaged or broken skin .
When using this product keep out of eyes .
Rinse with water to remove .
Stop use and ask a doctor if rash occurs , irritation and redness develop and persist for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Apply to clean face at night .
• Repeat use every other day for the first 15 days , then use every night .
• A tingling effect may occur during application .

Use a sunscreen .
