Active ingredient Sodium chloride 5 % Purpose Hypertonicity agent Uses temporary relief of corneal edema Warnings For external use only Do not use except under the advice and supervision of a doctor .
When using this product • • it may cause temporary burning and irritation • • replace cap after use • • to avoid contamination , do not touch tip of container to any surface Stop use and ask a doctor if • • condition worsens or persists for more than 72 hours • • you experience eye pain , changes in vision , continued redness or irritation of the eye Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
