Active ingredient Sodium fluoride 0 . 05 % ( 0 . 02 % w / v fluoride ion ) Purpose Anticavity rinse Use aids in the prevention of dental cavities Warning Keep out of reach of children .
If more than used for rinsing is accidentally swallowed , seek professional assistance or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
