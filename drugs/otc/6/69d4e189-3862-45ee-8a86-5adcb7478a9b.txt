Sunscreen Use • Helps prevent sunburn • Higher SPF gives more sunburn protection • Provides high protection against sunburn Warnings For external use only .
When using this product keep out of eyes .
Rinse with water to remove .
Stop use and ask a doctor if rash or irritation develops and lasts .
Keep out of the reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Apply generously and evenly to all areas 15 minutes before sun exposure • Spray on hands to apply to face • Reapply at frequent intervals and after swimming , sweating or towel drying • Ask a doctor before use on children under six months of age Other information Sun alert : Limiting sun exposure , wearing protective clothing , and using sunscreens may reduce the risks of skin aging , skin cancer , and other harmful effects of the sun .
