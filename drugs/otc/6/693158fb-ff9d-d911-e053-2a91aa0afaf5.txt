First aid antibiotic Uses first aid to help prevent infection in minor : • cuts • scrapes • burns Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • if you are allergic to any of the ingredients • in the eyes • over large areas of the body Ask a doctor before use if you have • deep or puncture wounds • animal bites • serious burns Stop use and ask a doctor if • you need to use longer than 1 week • condition persists or gets worse • rash or other allergic reaction develops Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • clean the affected area • apply a small amount of this product ( an amount equal to the surface area of the tip of a finger ) on the area 1 to 3 times daily • may be covered with a sterile bandage Other information • store at 15 ° to 25 ° C ( 59 ° to 77 ° F ) Inactive ingredients white soft paraffin Distributed by : UEC Medical 17211 Valley View Ave .
