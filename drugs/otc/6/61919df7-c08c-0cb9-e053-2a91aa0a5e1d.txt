To decrease this risk , regularly use a sunscreen with a broad spectrum SPF of 15 or higher and other sun protection measures including : ■ limit time in the sun , especially from 10 a . m . – 2 p . m . ■ wear long - sleeve shirts , pants , hats , and sunglasses ■ Children under 6 months of age : ask a doctor .
