Drug Facts Active Ingredient Menthol USP 6 % Purpose External Analgesic Uses For the temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with : • arthritis • strains • bruises • sprains • joint discomfort Warnings : For external use only .
Avoid contact with eyes .
Do not use • on wounds , damaged skin , or irritated areas • a heating pad in area where product is applied When using this product : • use only as directed • avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes • do not bandage or cover tightly • do not use with other topical products without first consulting a healthcare professional Stop use and ask a doctor if : • conditions worsen , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days • conditions clear up and occur again within a few days If pregnant or breast - feeding ask a health professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions : • Adults and children 2 years of age and older : Apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
Directly massage into achy points of the body .
Wash hands after use with cool water .
Product will gradually activate and warm to provide long - lasting relief .
• Children under 2 years of age : consult a doctor .
