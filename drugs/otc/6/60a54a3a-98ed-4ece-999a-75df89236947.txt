To decrease this risk , regularly use a sunscreen with a Broad Spectrum SPF value of 15 or higher and other sun protection measures including : • limit time in the sun , especially from 10 a . m . - 2 p . m . • - wear long - sleeved shirts , pants , hats and sunglasses Other information Protect this product from excessive heat and direct sun .
