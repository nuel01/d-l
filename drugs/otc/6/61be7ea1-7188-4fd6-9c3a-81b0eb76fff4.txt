Active Ingredient Hydroquinone 2 % Purpose Skin Lightener Cream Uses For gradual fading of dark ( brownish ) spots .
Warnings Avoid contact with eyes .
Do not use on children under 12 years of age .
Some users may experience a mild irritation .
If skin Irritation becomes severe , stop use and consult a doctor .
Directions Apply all over your body twice daily .
Pay special attention to problem areas like knees and elbows .
