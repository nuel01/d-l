Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 70 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses Hand Sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
Directions Apply liberally to front and back of hands .
Allow to dry .
Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings For external use only : hands .
Flammable , keep away from fire or flame .
When using this product avoid contact with the eyes .
In case of contact , Rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness appears and lasts .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Do not use : in children less than 2 months of age and on open wounds .
Do not inhale or ingest .
Supervise children under 6 years of age when using the product to avoid swallowing .
