Hand Sanitizer Drug Facts Active Ingredients Ethanol ( 70 % v / v ) Purpose Antimicrobial Use ( s ) • Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings • For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
Do not use • On children less than 2 months of age • On open skin wounds When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Tear open and apply product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub into the skin until dry .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age who are using this product to avoid swallowing .
