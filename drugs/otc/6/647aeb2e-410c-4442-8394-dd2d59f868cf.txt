Drug Facts Active Ingredients Menthol 16 % Camphor 11 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Topical Analgesic Uses Temporary relief of minor aches and pains associated with : • simple backache • arthritis • bruises • strains • sprains Warnings For external use only Do not use • on wounds or damaged skin • with a heating pad When using this product • use only as directed • avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes • do not bandage tightly Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • redness or severe skin irritation occurs • symptoms persist for more than 7 days • symptoms clear up and occur again within a few days If pregnant or breast - feeding ask a healthcare professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions Adults and children 12 years of age and older : apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
Children under 12 years of age : do not use ; consult a doctor .
