Lidocaine HCl , USP 4 % Topical Anesthetic Use Temporarily relieves minor pains Warnings For external use only Do not use • on large areas of the body or on cut , irritated , or swollen skin • on puncture wounds • for more than one week without consulting a doctor When using this product • use only as directed .
Read and follow all directions and warnings on this label • do not allow contact with eyes • do not handage or apply local heat ( such as heating pads ) to the area of use .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a doctor before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
