Juglans Regia ( Walnut ) Shell Powder ( 0 . 5 % ) Exfoliation Keep out of reach of children - Do not swallow .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek professional assistance .
Delivers gentle exfoliation for cleansing results .
Helps to remove dead skin .
Moisturizes the skin .
Keep skin soft and smooth .
Warnings For external use only Do not use when Your skin is red , inflamed , irritated or painful When using this product  Do not apply on other parts of the body  Avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
 Do not apply directly to wound or open cut .
Stop use and ask doctor if rash or irritation on skin develops and lasts .
Store at room temperature Directions After washing the face , apply the adequate amount on well dried skin avoiding around eyes and mouth .
Gently massage onto the face with the fingertip dipping water slightly .
Rinse clean with lukewarm water .
Use 1 - 2 times a week .
