ACTIVE INGREDIENT Povidone Iodine USP 10 % ( equivalent to 1 % titratable iodine ) PURPOSE Antiseptic USE First Aid Antiseptic to help prevent infection in minor cuts , scrapes and burns WARNINGS For external use only .
Do not use • in the eyes or over large areas of the body • longer than 1 week unless directed by a doctor Stop use and ask a doctor • if condition persists or gets worse • in case of deep puncture wounds , animal bites or serious burns Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
DIRECTIONS Clean the affected area .
Apply a small amount of this product to the area 1 - 3 times daily .
May be covered with a sterile bandage .
