Tolnaftate 1 % For External use only Do not use on children under 2 years of age unless directed by a doctor When using this product avoid contact with the eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if • irritation occurs • there is no improvement within 4 weeks Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away Directions : • clean the affected area and dry thoroughly • apply a thin layer over the affected area twice daily ( morning and night ) • supervise children in the use of this product • for athlete ’ s foot , pay special attention to the spaces between toes .
Wear well - fitting , ventilated shoes , and change shoes and socks at least once daily • use daily for 4 weeks .
If condition lasts longer , ask a doctor • to prevent athlete ’ s foot , apply to the feet once or twice daily • this product is not effective on the scalp or nails Keep out of reach of children .
