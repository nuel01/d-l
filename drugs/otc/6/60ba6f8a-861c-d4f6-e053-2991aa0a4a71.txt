Active Ingredient Gentian Violet 2 % Purpose Antiseptic Purpose First aid antiseptic to help protect against skin infection in minor cuts , scrapes or burns Warnings For external use only Do not use in eyes ; with bandages ; on large areas of the body ; longer than one week unless directed by a doctor ; stop use and consult a doctor if condition persists ; in case of deep puncture wounds , animal bites or serious burns , consult a doctor ; if known to be sensitive to any ingredients in the product ; do not apply to ulcerative lesions as this may result in tattooing of the skin .
Stop use and ask a doctor or pharmacist if skin irritation or sensitivity develops or increases or if the condition persists or gets worse .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions Clean the affected area .
Apply a small amount using a cotton - tipped applicator one to three times daily .
Do not bandage .
Other Information Store at controlled room temperature .
Will stain skin and clothing .
