Drug Facts Active ingredients Hydrocortisone , 0 . 5 % Purpose Anti - itch Uses • Provides temporary relief of itching associated with minor skin irritations , inflammation , and rashes from : • Eczema • Psoriasis • Insect bites • Seborrheic dermatitis • Soaps • Poison ivy • Poison oak • Poison sumac • Jewelry • Cosmetics • Detergents • Other uses of this product should be only under the advice and supervision of a doctor .
Warnings • For external use only .
Do not use • In the eyes .
• For the treatment of diaper rash .
When using this product do not begin the use of any other hydrocortisone product .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • Symptoms last for more than 7 days .
• The condition gets worse .
• Symptoms clear up and come back in a few days .
Keep out of the reach of children .
• If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Adults and children 2 years and older : apply to the affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
• Children under 2 years of age , do not use , ask a doctor .
Other information • Store at room temperature .
Protect from freezing and excessive heat .
