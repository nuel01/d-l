Active ingredient Menthol 1 percent Purpose Pain relieving gel Uses for the temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with simple backache , arthrists , strains , bruises , sprains Warnings For external use only .
When using this product • avoid contact with the eyes • do not handage tightly • do not apply to wounds or damaged skin • do not use with heating pads or other heating devices Stop use and ask a doctor if condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again with a few days .
