Keep oil , grease , and combustibles away .
Store and use with adequate ventilation .
Use only with equipment rated for cylinder pressure and cleaned for oxygen service .
Cylinder temperature should not exceed 125 ° F ( 52 ° C ) .
Open valve slowly .
Close valve after each use ; keep closed even when empty .
Always secure cylinder .
Install cap , if provided , when not in use .
Use a backflow prevention device in any piping .
Use per MSDS provided and Praxair safety booklets P - 3499 , P - 14 - 153 , and P - 15 - 073 ; obtain from your local supplier .
FIRST AID : IF INHALED , remove to fresh air .
If not breathing , give artificial respiration .
If breathing is difficult , qualified personnel may give oxygen .
Call a physician .
