ACTIVE INGREDIENT Salicylic Acid 1 % PURPOSE Acne Treatment USES For the treatment of acne .
Helps treat acne pimples and allows skin to heal .
WARNINGS For external use only When using this product skin irritation and dryness is more likely to occur if you use another topical acne medication at the same time .
If irritation occurs , only use one topical acne medication at a time .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a poison control center right away .
DIRECTIONS • Clean skin thoroughly before applying .
• Cover entire affected area with a thin layer 1 to 3 times daily .
• Because excessive drying of the skin may occur , start with 1 application , then gradually increase to 2 or 3 if needed or as directed by a doctor .
• If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to once a day or every other day .
