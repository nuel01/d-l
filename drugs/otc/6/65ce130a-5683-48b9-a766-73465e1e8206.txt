Active ingredient Salicylic acid 2 % Purpose Acne treatment Uses • for the treatment of acne • reduces the number of acne blemishes , blackheads , and whiteheads and allows skin to heal Warnings For external use only When using this product skin irritation and dryness is more likely to occur if you use another topical acne medication at the same time .
If irritation occurs , only use one topical acne medication at a time .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
