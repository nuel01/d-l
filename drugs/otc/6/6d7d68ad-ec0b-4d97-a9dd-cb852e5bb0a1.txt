Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 1 % Purpose Antiseptic Use Antiseptic Cleanser , helps decrease bacteria on skin .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product Avoid contact with the eyes ; in case of contact , flush eyes with water Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops and persists .
Keep out of reach of children .
If ingested get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
