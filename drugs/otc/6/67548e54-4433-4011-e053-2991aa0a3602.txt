Active ingredients Jojoba oil ( 0 . 9 % ) Chamomile Extract ( 0 . 9 % ) Tea tree oil ( 0 . 7 % ) Aloe Extract ( 0 . 7 % ) Purpose Skin protectant Anti - itchy Anti - bacterial Humectant Uses Moisturizes skin and helps to relieve skin irritation and itching for sensitive skin .
Warnings For external use only Do not use when skin is red , inflamed , irritated , or painful When using this product • avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water • do not apply directly to wound or open cut Stop use and ask a doctor if • rash or irritation on skin develops and lasts • condition does not improve or worsen after regular use of this product as directed Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) right away .
Directions 1 .
Wet soap to generate foam to cover desired part of face or body 2 .
Massage foam well into the skin rubbing gently .
3 .
Rinse after 3 - 5 min .
4 .
Pat dry with soft cloth , leaving bit of moisture .
5 .
Repeat 2 - 3 times daily as needed .
