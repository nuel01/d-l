Povidone - Iodine USP 10 % ( 0 . 16 % when diluted ) Antiseptic For temporary relief of minor itching and irritation Do not use if sensitive to iodine For vaginal use only Stop use and ask a doctor if • symptoms persist for more than 7 days you have pain or tenderness in the lower part of the abdomen and pelvis • you have vaginal discharge or bleeding , chills , nausea , or fever • your vaginal discharge is an unusual amount , color orodor • you have frequent and painful urination • douching results in pain , soreness , swelling , redness , itching , excessive dryness or irritation If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use Use twice weekly unless directed by a physician . Add 1 / 2 fluid ounce ( one tablespoonful ) to 1 quart of lukewarm water . For other uses consult a physician .
