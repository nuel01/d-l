Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol , 80 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses For hand cleaning to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
No Smoking .
When using this product avoid contact with face , eyes and broken skin .
If eye contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water and seek medical advice .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops .
Contents under pressure .
Do not puncture or incinerate .
Do not store at temperatures above 120 ° F ( 50 ° C ) .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed get medical help , or contact a poison control center immediately .
Directions • apply a palmful to hands • scrub thoroughly until dry Inactive Ingredients water panthenol , glycerin , cetyl alcohol , propylene glycol .
