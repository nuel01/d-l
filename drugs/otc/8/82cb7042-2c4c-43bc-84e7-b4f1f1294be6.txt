Active Ingredient Menthol 5 . 0 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Patch Uses For the temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with • simple backache • arthritis • strains • bruises • sprains Warnings For external use only When using this product • avoid contact with eyes • do not apply to wounds or damaged skin • do not bandage tightly Stop use and ask a doctor if condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
