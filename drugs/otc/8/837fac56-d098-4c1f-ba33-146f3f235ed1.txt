Active ingredient Salicylic acid 17 % Purpose Corn and callus remover Uses for the removal of corns and calluses ; relieves pain by removing corns and calluses .
Warnings : For external use only .
Keep out of reach of children .
Inactive ingredients D & C yellow # 11 , methylparaben , methyl salicylate , propylparaben , white petrolatum Tamper Evident Do not use if seal is broken or missing .
Directions Apply a thin coat of the medication to cover the entire wart , callus , or corn using the applicator if provided .
Be careful to apply it only to the affected area and not the surrounding skin .
Let dry for 5 minutes .
Repeat this procedure 1 to 2 times daily for up to 2 weeks for corns and calluses and 12 weeks for warts or as directed by your doctor .
