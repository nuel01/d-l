Active Ingredient Triclosan 0 . 46 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses Helps reduce bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product Avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop using this product and ask doctor If irritation and redness develop .
Keep out of reach of children In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help and contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions Pump onto dry hands , work into a lather and rinse thoroughly .
Other information Store at room temperature .
