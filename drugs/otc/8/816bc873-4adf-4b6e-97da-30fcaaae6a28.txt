Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses Helps eliminate bacteria on hands .
Warnings For exteral use only When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop using this product and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develop and last .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • use only to refill a Foaming Hand Soap pump bottle .
• apply to dry hands • work into a rich foamy lather and rinse thoroughly .
