In case of deep puncture wounds , animal bites , or serious burns , consult a doctor .
Do not use • for more than 7 days unless told to do so by a doctor • more than directed • if you are allergic to any ingredient in this product When using this product • avoid contact with the eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
