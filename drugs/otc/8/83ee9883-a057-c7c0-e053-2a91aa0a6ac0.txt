First aid antiseptic Uses • helps prevent the risk of skin infection in minor cuts , scrapes and burns Warnings For external use only Do not use • in or near the eyes • over large areas of the body • on individuals who are allergic or sensitive to iodine • longer than 1 week unless directed by a doctor Ask a doctor before use if you have • deep puncture wounds • animal bites • serious burns Stop use and ask a doctor if • irritation or redness develops • condition persists for more than 72 hours Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) right away .
Directions • clean the affected area • apply a small amount to the affected area 1 - 3 times daily • may be covered with a sterile bandage .
If bandaged , let dry first .
