Drug Facts Active Ingredients Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % V / V .
Purpose First aid antiseptic Use • For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings • For external use only .
• Flammable , keep away from fire or flame Do not use In the eyes or over large areas of the body .
