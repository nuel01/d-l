Lubricant Uses • for the temporary relief of burning and irritation due to dryness of the eye • for protection against further irritation Warnings for external use only When using this product • remove contact lenses before use • do not use if this solution changes color or becomes cloudy • do not touch tip of container or any surface to avoid contamination • replace cap after each use Stop use and ask a doctor if • you feel eye pain • changes in vision occur • redness or irritation of the eye lasts • condition worsens or last more than 72 hours If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
