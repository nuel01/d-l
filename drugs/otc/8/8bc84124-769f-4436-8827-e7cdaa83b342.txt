Active ingredient : Triclosan 0 . 3 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses : Hand soap to help decrease bacteria on the skin with rinsing by water after use .
Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings : For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
Do not apply around eyes .
Do not use in ears or mouth .
When using this product , avoid contact with eyes .
In case of contact , flush eyes with water .
Stop use or ask for a doctor if redness or irritation develops and persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
Children must be supervised in use of this product .
Directions : Pump as needed into your palms and thoroughly spread on both hands .
Rub into skin until dry .
Other information : Do not store in temperature over 118 F .
