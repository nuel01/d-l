Purpose External Analgesic Towelette .
Active Ingredient Pramoxine hydrochloride 1 % w / w Uses Temporarily relieves itching and burning due to minor skin irritations .
Warnings For external use only .
Store at room temperature .
Avoid contact with eyes .
Stop use and ask doctor if condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days , or clears up and occur again within a few days .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Remove cloth from foil pouch .
Unfold and gently wipe to clean external vaginal area .
Use each towelette only once and then throw away .
Use daily whenever desired to be refreshed .
