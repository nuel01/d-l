Active Ingredients Capsaicin 0 . 25 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Use : For the temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with arthritis .
Warnings • For external use only .
• Avoid contact with eyes and mucus membranes .
• It can take 1 to 2 weeks before therapeutics effect is felt .
• For use beyond 6 weeks , consult a health care practitioner Do not • Apply to wounds or damaged skin .
• Bandage tightly .
• Inhale .
• Apply with external heat , such as an electric pad , as this may result in excessive skin irritation or skin burn .
When using this product • Use only as directed .
• Keep out of reach of children .
If overdose or accidental ingestion occurs , call a poison control center immediately .
• Consult a healthcare practitioner prior to use if you are pregnant or breastfeeding .
Stop use and ask a doctor if you experience hypersensitivity , rashes and / or burning discomfort , headache or redness or if symptoms worsen or re - occur with a few days .
Directions • Adults : Apply 2 times per day Avoid taking a hot bath or shower just before or after applications as a burning sensation may result .
Product can be rinsed off with cool water and soap .
