Drug Facts Active ingredient Titanium Dioxide ( 4 . 5 % ) Purpose Sunscreen Uses • helps prevent sunburn • if used as directed with other sun protection measures ( see Directions ) , may decrease the risk of skin cancer and early skin aging caused by the sun .
Warnings For external use only Do not use on broken or damaged skin .
When using this product keep out of eyes .
Rinse with water to remove .
If irritation develops , discontinue use .
Do not use on infants under 6 months old .
Stop use and ask a physician if too much skin irritation or sensitivity develops or increases .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Apply evenly 15 minutes before sun exposure .
• Reapply at least every 2 hours • Use a water resistant sunscreen if swimming or sweating • After cleansing in the morning , gently pat around the entire eye area , blending inward from the outer corner .
