Uses • for the treatment of acne • helps clear up acne blemishes • helps prevent new acne blemishes from forming Warnings For external use only When using this product • avoid contact with eyes .
If product contacts eyes , rinse thoroughly with water .
• if excessive skin irritation develops or increases , discontinue use .
If irritation persists , consult a health care practitioner .
• when using other topical acne products at the same time or immediately following use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one product should be used unless directed by a health care practitioner .
Sunburn Alert : Use only as directed .
This product contains an alpha hydroxy acid that may increase your skin ' s sensitivity to the sun and particularly the possibility of sunburn .
Please limit sun exposure and apply a sunburn protectant while using this product and for a week afterwards .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
