Drug Facts – Extra Strength Gold Bond Body Lotion Active ingredients Menthol 0 . 5 % Purpose Anti - itch Uses temporarily relieves the pain and itching associated with : • minor skin irritations • minor burns • minor cuts • insect bites • sunburn • scrapes Warnings For external use only Do not use on • deep or puncture wounds • animal bites • serious burns • large areas of the body When using this product • do not get into eyes or nose • not for prolonged use Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days • redness , irritation , swelling or pain persists or increases Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
