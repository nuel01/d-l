Drug Facts Active ingredient Alcohol 70 % v / v Purpose Antiseptic Use ( s ) ■ Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
Do Not Use ■ on children less than 2 months of age ■ on open skin wounds When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctorif irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions ■ Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
■ Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
Other information ■ Store between 15 - 30 ° C ( 59 - 86 ° F ) ■ Avoid freezing and excessive heat above 40 ° C ( 104 ° F ) Inactive Ingredients Acrylates / C10 - 30 Alkyl Acrylate Crosspolymer , Aminomethyl Propanol , Glycerin , Tocopheryl Acetate , Purified Water .
