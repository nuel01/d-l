Active ingredient ( per tablet ) Calcium carbonate 1000 mg Purpose Antacid Uses relieves • • heartburn • • acid indigestion • • sour stomach • • upset stomach associated with these symptoms Warnings Ask a doctor or pharmacist before use if you are presently taking a prescription drug .
Antacids may interact with certain prescription drugs .
When using this product • • do not take more than 7 tablets in 24 hours • • If pregnant do not take more than 5 tablets in 24 hours • • do not use the maximum dosage for more than 2 weeks , except under the advice and supervision of a doctor Keep out of reach of children Directions • • adults and children 12 years of age and over : chew 2 - 3 tablets as symptoms occur , or as directed by a doctor .
Chew or crush tablets completely before swallowing .
