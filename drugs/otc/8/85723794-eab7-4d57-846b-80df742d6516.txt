See your dentist immediately .
These may be signs of periodtitis , a serious form of gum disease .
Stop use and ask a dentist gingivitis , bleeding , or redness persists for more than 2 weeks Keep out of reach of children If more than used for rinsing is accidentally swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
