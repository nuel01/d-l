Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antibacterial Use • Helps eliminate bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product • avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop using this product and ask doctor if • irritation or redness develops and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children .
• In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • Apply onto dry hands .
Work into a lather , rinse and dry thoroughly .
Other information • store at room temperature .
