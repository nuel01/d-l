Active Ingredients Allantoin 0 . 5 % , Menthol 0 . 1 % Purpose Allantoin - Skin Protectant Menthol - Topical Anesthetic Uses For temporary relief and protection from pain and itching associated with minor skin irritations , rashes , eczema and / or insect bites .
Warnings For external use only .
Avoid contact with eyes .
Do not apply to open wounds or damaged skin .
If symptoms persist for more than seven days , discontinue use and consult physician .
If pregnant or breast feeding , contact physician prior to use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , consult physician .
Directions Apply directly to affected area .
Do not use more than 4 times per day .
Do not bandage damaged skin .
Store at room temperature .
May stain fabrics .
