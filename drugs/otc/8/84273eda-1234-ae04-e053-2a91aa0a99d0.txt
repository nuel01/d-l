Drug facts Active ingredient Menthol USP 3 . 7 % Purpose Topical analgesic Uses temporarily relieves minor aches and pains of muscles and joints due to • arthritis • simple backaches • strains • bruises • sprains Warnings For external use only Flammable : Keep away from fire or flame .
Do not use • on wounds or damaged skin • with a heating pad • with other ointments , creams , sprays or liniments When using this product • avoid contact with eyes • do not bandage tightly • wash hands with cool water after use Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens or rash or irritation develops • symptoms last more than 7 days • condition clears up and occurs again within a few days Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
