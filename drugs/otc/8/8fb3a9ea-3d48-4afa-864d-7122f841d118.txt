Active ingredient Chloroxylenol 0 . 5 % Purpose Antiseptic handwash Uses • For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin Warnings • For external use only Do not use • In eyes When using this product • If in eyes , rinse promptly and thoroughly with water • Discontinue use if irritation and redness develop Stop use and ask a doctor if • Skin irritation or redness occurs for more than 72 hours Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
