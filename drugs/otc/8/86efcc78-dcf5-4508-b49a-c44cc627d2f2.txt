Active ingredient White Petrolatum USP ( 99 . 9 % ) Purpose Skin Protectant Uses • For the temporary protection of minor cuts , scrapes , burns and sunburn .
• Helps to temporarily protect chafed , chapped , cracked or windburned skin and lips .
Warnings For External Use Only .
Do not use over deep or puncture wounds , infections or lacerations .
Ask a doctor .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
Stop use and ask doctor if condition worsens or does not improve within 7 days .
Keep out of reach of children if swallowed get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Apply product as liberally , as often as necessary .
