Drug Facts Active ingredient Triclosan 0 . 25 % Purpose Antiseptic USE For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product Avoid direct contact with eyes .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse immediately with plenty of water Stop use and ask a doctor • If irritation persists , get medical attention .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , seek medical advice immediately and show this container or label .
Directions • Wet your hands with running water , get some hand sanitizer on hands , rub your hands away from each other , and rub the back of your hands , fingers , palms , and nail seams repeatedly in a circular motion .
• After about 2 minutes , rinse the foam with hands under running water .
