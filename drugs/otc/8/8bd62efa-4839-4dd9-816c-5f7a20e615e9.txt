Active Ingredients Octinoxate 7 . 3 % , Oxybenzone 1 . 65 % Use Helps prevent sunburn .
Warnings For external use only .
Stop use if skin rash occurs .
Keep out of reach of children .
Directions Apply evenly before sun exposure and as needed .
Apply lipstick following the curves of natural lip line .
