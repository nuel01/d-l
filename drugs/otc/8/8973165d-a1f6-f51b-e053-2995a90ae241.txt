Active ingredient Chloroxylenol 0 . 3 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses for hand washing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush with clean water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develops .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • pump into hands • lather vigorously for at least 15 seconds • rinse and dry thoroughly Inactive ingredients Water ( Aqua ) , Ammonium Lauryl Sulfate , Cocamidopropyl Betaine , Ammonium Chloride , Glycol Distearate , Fragrance , DMDM Hydantoin , Citric Acid , Tetrasodium EDTA , D & C Orange No . 4 .
