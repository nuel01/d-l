Drug Facts Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 70 % Purpose Antiseptic Use • Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin .
Warnings • For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from flame .
When using this product avoid contact with face and eyes .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop using and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Spray enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
• Children under 6 years of age should be supervised by an adult when using .
Other Information • Store below 110 ° F ( 43 ° C ) .
• May discolor certain fabrics or surfaces .
