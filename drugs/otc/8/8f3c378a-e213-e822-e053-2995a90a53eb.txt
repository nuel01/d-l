Active Ingredients ( in each gram ) Bacitracin Zinc 500 units Neomycin Sulfate 3 . 5 mg Polymyxin B Sulfate 10 , 000 units Pramoxine HCl 10 mg Purpose First aid antibiotic External analgesic Uses first aid to help prevent infection and for temporary relief of pain or discomfort in minor : • cuts • scrapes • burns Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • if you are allergic to any of the ingredients • in the eyes • over large areas of the body Ask a doctor before use if you have • deep or puncture wounds • animal bites • serious burns Stop use and ask a doctor if • you need to use longer than 1 week • condition persists or gets worse • symptoms persist for more than 1 week , or clear up and occur again within a few days • rash or other allergic reaction develops Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • adults and children 2 years of age or older : • clean the affected area • apply a small amount of this product ( an amount equal to the surface area of the tip of a finger ) on the area 1 to 3 times daily • may be covered with a sterile bandage • children under 2 years of age : ask a doctor Other information • store at room temperature • Lot No .
