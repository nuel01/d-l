Active ingredients Allantoin Spirulina Platensis Extract Purpose Skin protectant Uses Moisturizes skin .
Warnings For external use only Do not use when skin is red , inflamed , irritated , or painful When using this product - do not apply on other parts of the body avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water - do not apply directly to wound or open cut Stop use and ask a doctor if - Rash or irritation on skin develops and lasts Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) right away .
Directions Simply wash and dry your skin thoroughly .
Before applying the gel under each eye , remove clear plastic from each gel .
You may use either side of the gel .
Allow gel to work for 15 - 20 minutes .
Remove each gel and lightly massage any remaining serum into your skin .
