Active Ingredients Menthol - 12 % Camphor - 10 % Methyl Salicylate - 23 % Uses Temporarily relieves minor aches and pains of muscles and joints due to : • simple backache • arthritis • sprains • bruises • strains Warnings For External Use Only Do Not Use Do not use • on wounds or damaged skin • if allergic to salicylates When using this product Avoid contact with the eyes , mouth , or mucous membranes Do not bandage tightly Stop using and consult with physician if Condition worsens Symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of Reach of Children Safety Pregnant or lactating women consult a doctor before use In case of accidental ingestion contact a doctor or Poision control center immediately Directions Adults and children 2 years of age and older : apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily Children under 2 years of age : Do not use , consult a doctor Other Information Keep tightly closed Store in a cool and dry place Do not use if seal is broken Inactive Ingredients Bees wax , Peppermint oil , Eucalyptus oil , Nutmeg oil Packaging [ MULTIMEDIA ] [ MULTIMEDIA ]
