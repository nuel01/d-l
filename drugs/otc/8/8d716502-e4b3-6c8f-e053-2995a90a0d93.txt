Active ingredient ( each gram contains ) Purpose Bacitracin 500 units ......................................................................................... First Aid Antibiotic Uses first aid to help prevent infection in • minor cuts • scrapes • burns Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • in the eyes • over large areas of the body • if you are allergic to any of the ingredients • longer than 1 week unless directed by a doctor Ask a doctor before use in case of deep or puncture wounds , animal bites , or serious burns .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • you need to use longer than 1 week • condition persists or gets worse • rash or other allergic reaction develops If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) right away .
Directions • clean the affected area • apply a small amount of this product ( an amount equal to the surface area of the tip of a finger ) on the area 1 to 3 times daily • may be covered with a sterile bandage Other information • store at 20 ° to 25 ° C ( 68 ° to 77 ° F ) Inactive ingredients aloe vera gel , light mineral oil and petrolatum Distributed by : First Aid Research Corp .
