Active ingredient ( per tablet ) Calcium Carbonate 750 mg Purpose Antacid Uses relieves • • heartburn • • acid indigestion • • sour stomach • • upset stomach associated with these symptoms Warnings Ask a doctor or pharmacist before use if you are presently taking a prescription drug .
Antacids may interact with certain prescription drugs .
When using this product • • do not take more than 10 tablets in 24 hours • • if pregnant do not take more than 6 tablets in 24 hours • • do not use the maximum dosage for more than 2 weeks except under the advice and supervision of a doctor Keep out of reach of children .
Directions • • adults and children 12 years of age and over : chew 2 – 4 tablets as symptoms occur , or as directed by a doctor • • do not take for symptoms that persist for more than 2 weeks unless advised by a doctor Other information • • each tablet contains : elemental calcium 300 mg • • do not store above 25 oC ( 77 oF ) .
Keep the container tightly closed .
