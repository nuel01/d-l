Warnings • For use in the eyes only .
• Save box for complete information .
When using this product • avoid contamination , do not touch tip of container to any surface .
• replace cap after each use .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • you experience eye pain , changes in vision , continued redness or irritation of the eye .
• condition worsens or persists for more than 72 hours .
