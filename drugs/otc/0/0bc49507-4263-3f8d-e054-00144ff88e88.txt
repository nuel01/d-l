Do not use • on large areas of the body • with any other product containing diphenhydramine , even one taken by mouth Ask a doctor before use • on chicken pox • on measles When using this product do not get into eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of reach of children .
