Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antibacterial Use For washing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop using this product and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develops and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions Squeeze a small amount onto a wet wash cloth , sponge , pouf or hands and apply to body .
Work into a rich lather and rinse off .
