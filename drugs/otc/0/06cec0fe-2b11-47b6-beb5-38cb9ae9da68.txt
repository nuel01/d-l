Drug Facts Active Ingredient Purpose Ethyl Alcohol 62 percent Antiseptic Uses Instant hand sanitizer helps to reduce bacteria on the skin .
Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable , keep away from fire or flame .
When using this product keep out of eyes .
In case of eye contact , immediately flush eyes with water and call a doctor .
Stop use and ask a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
As with all drug products , children under 6 years of ages should be supervised when using instant hand sanitizers .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Place a small amount into one hand , spread over both hands to wrist and rub into the skin until dry .
