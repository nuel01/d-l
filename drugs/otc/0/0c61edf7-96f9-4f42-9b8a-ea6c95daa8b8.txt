• When using this product • do not get into eyes .
Stop use and ask doctor if : • condition worsens • symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days .

If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions When practical , gently cleanse and dry area .
• Adults : Apply externally to affected area up to 6 times daily or after each incontinent episode or diaper change .
