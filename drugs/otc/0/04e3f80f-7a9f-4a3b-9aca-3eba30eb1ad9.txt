Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol Purpose Antibacterial Uses Health care personnel hand rub to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause diseases .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame Do not use - In children less than 2 months of age - On open skin wounds .
When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water Directions - Place enough product onhands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
- Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
Other information - Store between 15 - 30 C ( 59 - 86 F ) - Avoid freezing and excessive heat above 40 C ( 104 F ) Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a poison control center right away .
