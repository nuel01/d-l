Acne Solution [ MULTIMEDIA ] [ MULTIMEDIA ] Pyrithione Zinc for the treatment of acne Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Clean the skin thoroughly before applying this product .
Cover the entire affected area with a thin layer one to three times daily Because excessive drying of the skin may occur , start with one application daily , then gradually increase to two or three daily if needed or directed by a doctor .
Warnings For external use only When using this product When using this product skin irritation and dryness is more likely to occur if you use another topical acne medication .
If irritation occurs , start with one application daily , then gradually increase to two or three times daily if needed or directed by a doctor .
If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to once a day or every other day .
