Active Ingredients Ethyl Alcohol 62 . 0 % w / w Purpose Antimicrobial Uses • • For hand sanitizing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
• • Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings – For External Use Only .
Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops , or if condition persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • • Pump enough foam into palm of hand to cover both hands .
• • Rub thoroughly over all surfaces of both hands .
• • Rub hands together briskly until dry .
• • Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
Inactive Ingredients : Water , PEG - 10 Dimethicone , Glycerine , Fragrance , Citric Acid , Vitamin E , Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice .
