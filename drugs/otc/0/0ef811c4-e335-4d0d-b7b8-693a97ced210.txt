DRUG FACTS Active Ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride .......... 0 . 13 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses : For hand sanitizing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product avoid contact with the eyes .
In case of contact , flush eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if Irritation and redness develop and persist .
Keep out of reach of children .
if swallowed get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Take wipe from pouch and rub thoroughly over all surfaces of both hands .
Rub hands together briskly to dry .
Dispose of wipe .
