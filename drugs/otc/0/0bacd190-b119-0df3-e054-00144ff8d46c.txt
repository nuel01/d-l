Drug Facts Active ingredients Menthol 1 % Pramoxine hydrochloride 1 % Purpose Topical analgesic Uses for temporary relief of pain and itching due to : • minor burns • sunburn • minor cuts • scrapes • insect bites • minor skin irritations • minor rashes due to poison ivy , poison oak , or poison sumac Warnings For external use only When using this product do not get into eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition gets worse • symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
