Drug Facts Active ingredient Triclosan 0 . 3 % Purpose Anti - Bacterial Uses For cleansing to decrease bacteria on skin Warnings • For external use only .
Do not use on infants under 6 months of age .

If signs of irritation or rash appear , discontinue use .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash persists or is excessive .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
