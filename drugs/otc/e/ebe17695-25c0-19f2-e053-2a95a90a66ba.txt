Do not use for the treatment of diaper rash .
Consult a doctor .
When using this product • avoid contact with eyes • do not begin use of any other hydrocortisone product unless you ' ve consulted a doctor Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days • condition clears up and occurs again within a few days Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
