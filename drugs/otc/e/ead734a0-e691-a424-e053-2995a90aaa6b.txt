First Aid Antiseptic Uses : Antiseptic cleansing of face , hands and body to decrease bacteria on skin without soap and water .
Antiseptic to help prevent further infection in minor cuts , scrapes , and burns .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use : • In the eyes .
If this happens , rinse thoroughly with water .
• Over large areas of the body • Longer than 1 week unless directed by a doctor Stop use and ask doctor if : • Irritation of redness develop and persists for more than 72 hours .
• the condition persists or gets worse Keep out of reach of children : If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Ask a doctor before use if you have : • Deep or puncture wounds • Animal bites • Serious burns Directions : Tear open packet , unfold and use as a washcloth .
