Active Ingredient PCMX 0 . 1 % .
Purpose Antiseptic Handwashing Uses Handwash to help decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use in the eyes .
In case of contact , immediately flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash appears and lasts .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Apply a small amount , covering hands with product for 30 seconds .
Add water , lather and rinse .
Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
Inactive Ingredients Water , Cocamidopropyl Betaine , Sodium Lauryl Sulfate , Sodium Laureth - 3 Sulfate , Sodium Chloride , Propylene glycol , Propanediol , Fragrance , Methylchloroisothiazolinone , Methylisothiazolinone , Yellow 5 , and Red 40 .
