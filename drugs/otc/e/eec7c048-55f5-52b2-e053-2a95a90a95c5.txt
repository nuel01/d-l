In case of contact , flush thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days Do not use on deep puncture wounds , animal bites , or serious burns .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
