If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) Uses  helps relieve the local itching and discomfort associated with hemorrhoids  temporarily shrinks hemorrhoidal tissue and relieves burning  temporarily provides a coating for relief of anorectal discomforts  temporarily protects the inflamed , irritated anorectal surface to help make bowel movements less painful Warnings For external and / or intrarectal use only Ask a doctor before use if you have ▪ heart disease ▪ high blood pressure ▪ thyroid disease ▪ diabetes ▪ difficulty in urination due to enlargement of the prostate gland Ask a doctor or pharmacist before useif you are presently taking a prescription drug for high blood pressure or depression When using this product do not exceed the recommended daily dosage unless directed by a doctor Stop use and ask a doctor if ▪ bleeding occurs ▪ condition worsens or does not improved within 7 days ▪ introduction of applicator into the rectum causes additional pain If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use .
