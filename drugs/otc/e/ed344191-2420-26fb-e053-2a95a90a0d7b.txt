Drug Facts Active Ingredient Lidocaine HCL 4 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Uses For the temporary relief of pain and itching associated with : • Sunburn • Minor Skin Irritation • Minor Burns • Cuts & Scrapes • Insect Bites Warnings For external use only .
Avoid contact with eyes .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Stop use and ask doctor : if condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days .
If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health care professional before use .
Do not use in large quantities , particularly over raw surfaces or blistered areas .
Directions : Adults and Children 2 years of age or older : • Clean the burn area with mild soap and water • Gently apply over bthe burn or wound • Use on affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily Children under 2 years of age : Consult a doctor Other Information Store at room temperature 15 - 30 ° C ( 59 - 86 ° F ) Inactive Ingredients 1 , 3 - Propanediol , Aloe Barbadensis ( Aloe ) Leaf Juice , Caprylyl Glycol , Chlorphenesin , Dimethyl Isosorbide , Hydroxyethyl Cellulose , Phenoxyethanol , Tocopheryl Acetate ( Vitamin E ) , Water .
