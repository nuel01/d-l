Active Ingredient Purpose Sulfur 8 . 0 % .... Acne treatment Uses • Helps clear up acne blemishes and helps prevent new acne blemishes from forming .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product skin irritation and dryness is more likely to occur if you use another topical acne medication at the same time .
If irritation occurs , only use one topical acne medication at a time .
Do not use on • broken skin • large areas of the skin When using this product • apply only to areas with acne Avoid eye area .
In case of eye contact , flush gently and thoroughly with water .
Keep out of reach of children .
