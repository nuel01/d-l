Indications and use Helps prevent sunburn .
If used as directed with other sun protection measures , decreases the risk of skin cancer and early skin aging caused by the sun .
Dosage and Administration : Apply liberally and evenly to all sun exposed areas of DRY skin 15 minutes before sun exposure .
Reapply after 80 minutes of swimming or sweating and immediately after towel drying .
Apply at least every 2 hours .
For children under 6 months , ask a physician .

Do not use on damaged or broken skin .
Keep out of eyes .
Rinse eyes thoroughly with water to remove .
Stop use and ask a physician if rash or irritation develops and lasts .
Store away from excessive heat and direct sun .
