Warnings Keep out of reach of children under 6 years of age .
If more than twice the recommended amount for tongue spraying is accidentally swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Do not spray into the eyes .
If spray gets into eyes , hold eyelids apart and flush the eye continuously with running water for at least 15 minutes .
