Drug Facts Drug Facts Active ingredient and Purpose Active ingredient Purpose Mythyl Salicylate 15 % Topical analgesic Use For the temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with • backaches • arthritis • strains • bruises • sprains Warnings For external use only .
Avoid contact with the eyes Do not • apply to wounds or damaged skin • bandage tightly Stop use and ask a doctor if condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days .
If pregnant or breast feeding , ask a health professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help , or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
