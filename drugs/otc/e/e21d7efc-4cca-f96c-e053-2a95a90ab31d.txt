CVS Decolorized Iodine Drug Facts Active Ingredient Alcohol 48 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses To help prevent infection in in minor : cuts , scrapes and burns .
Warnings Ask a doctor before use if you have deep or puncture wounds .
Animal bites .
Serious burns .
Stop use and consult a doctor if Condition persists or gets worse , or if using this product for more than 1 week .
For external use only .
Do not use in the eyes .
If contact occurs , flush with large amounts of water while lifting upper and lower lids .
Do not apply over large areas of the body .
Keep out of reach of children .
• In case of accidental ingestion , give milk , then a starch solution made by mixing two tablespoonfuls of cornstarch or flour to a pint of water , Contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • Clean the affected area .
Apply a small amount to the affected area 1 to 3 times daily .
May be covered with a sterile bandage .
If bandaged , let dry first .
Inactive Ingredient Ammonia , iodine , potassium iodide , purified water .
