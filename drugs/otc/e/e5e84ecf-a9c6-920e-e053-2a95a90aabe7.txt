SHEA MOISTURE APPLE CIDER VINEGAR ANTI - DANDRUFF PRE - WASH - salicylic acid liquid Drug Facts Active ingredient Salicylic Acid ( 1 . 8 % ) Purpose Anti - dandruff Uses helps prevent recurrence of flaking and itching associated with dandruff .
Warnings For external use only When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughtly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if condition worsens or does not improve after regular use of this product as directed .
Keep this and all drugs out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions For best results use at least twice a week or as directed by doctor .
Shake well before use .
Use as pre - shampoo treatment on either wet or dry scalp .
Use nozzle to apply directly onto scalp and massage using your fingertips .
Leave on ten minutes then wash hair as normal .
Other information Product contains menthol .
If you experience discomfort , please stop using .
