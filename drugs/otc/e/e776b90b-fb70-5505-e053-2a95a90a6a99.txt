Guaifenesin USP 100 mg Purpose Expectorant Uses helps loosen phlegm ( mucus ) and thin bronchial secretions to make coughs more productive Warnings Do not take this product for persistent or chronic couch such as occurs with smoking , asthma , chronic bronchitis , or emphysema , or where cough is accompanied by excessive phlegm ( mucus ) unless directed by a doctor . "
Stop use and ask a doctor if cough persists for more than 1 week , tends to recur , or is accompanied by a fever , rash or persistent headache , consult a doctor .
A persistent cough may be a sign of a serious condition .
If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use .
Keep out of the reach of children .
In case of overdose , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
