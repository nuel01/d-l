Drug Facts Active ingredient Salicylic Acid 0 . 5 % .
Purpose Acne Treatment Uses • for the treatment of acne .
• helps clear up acne pimples and allows skin to heal .
• helps prevent new acne pimples from forming .
Warnings For external use only When using this product • skin irritation and dryness is more likely to occur if you use another topical acne medication at the same time .
If irritation occurs , only use one topical acne medication at a time .
• avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • clean the skin thoroughly before applying this product .
• cover the entire affected area with a thin layer and rinse thoroughly one to three times daily .
• because excessive drying of the skin may occur , start with one application daily , then gradually increase to two or three times daily if needed or as directed by a doctor .
• if bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to once a day or every other day .
