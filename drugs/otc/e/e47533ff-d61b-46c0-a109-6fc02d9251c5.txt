Drug Facts Active ingredient Camphor 1 % Menthol 2 % Purpose external analgesic muscle and joint pain relief Keep out of reach children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Centre immediately .
Uses • Temporary soothing relief of aches and pains associated with sore , aching muscles , muscle strains and sprains , backaches , joint pains , arthritis and rheumatism .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • on open wounds , damaged , broken or irritated skin .
• with a heating pad or apply external heat .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • pain gets worse or lasts more than 14 days .
• skin redness , irritation or excessive dryness of the skin occurs .
If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use .
Directions • Adults and children 2 years and up : Apply generously and gently massage on the affected area 2 to 3 times daily as necessary .
• Children under 2 years : Consult a doctor before use .
Inactive ingredients Peppermint oil , Clove oil , Eucalyptus oil , Marjoram oil , Sage oil , Wintergreen oil , Sesame oil , Almond oil .
Other information • This product may cause allergic reaction in some individuals .
Test on small area before use .
• Store in cool dry place , away from direct sunlight at temperatures not exceeding 86ºF .
