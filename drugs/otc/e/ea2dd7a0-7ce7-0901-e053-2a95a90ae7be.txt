Directions Apply liberally 15 minutes before sun exposure Hold can 4 - 6 inches away from body , spray evenly to ensure complete coverage Do not spray into face .
Spray into hand and apply to face .
Use in well ventilated , but not windy areas Reapply : After 80 minutes of swimming or sweating Immediately after towel drying At least every 2 hours Sun Protection Measures Spending time in the sun increases your risk of skin cancer and early skin aging .
