Drug Facts Active Ingredients Zinc Oxide 6 % Titanium Dioxide 9 % Purpose Sunscreen Uses • Helps prevent sunburn • if used as directed with other sun protection measures ( see Directions ) , decreases the risk of skin cancer and early skin aging caused by the sun Warnings • For external use only .
When using this product • keep out of eyes .
Rinse with water to remove .
Stop using and ask a doctor if a rash or irritation develops and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or call a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Apply liberally and spread evenly 15 minutes before sun exposure .
