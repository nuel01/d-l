Active Ingredients Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Lidocaine HCl 0 . 5 % Purpose First Aid Antiseptic Topical Analgesic Uses • Temporary relief of pain associated with minor cuts , scrapes , and burns .

Warnings For external use only .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center directly .
