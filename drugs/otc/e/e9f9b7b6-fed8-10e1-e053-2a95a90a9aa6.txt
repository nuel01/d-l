Drug Facts Active Ingredients Alcohol 62 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses For hand washing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only .
When using this product , keep out of eyes .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse with water .
Stop use and ask doctor if irritation and redness develops and persists .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help promptly and contact Poison Control .
Directions Wet hands throughly with product and allow to dry without wiping .
Other information Store under 105 oF .
