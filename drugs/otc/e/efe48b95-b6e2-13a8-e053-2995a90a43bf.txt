Lubricant Use • for the temporary relief of burning and irritation of the eye due to dryness of the eye Warnings For external use only Do not use • if this solution changes color or becomes cloudy • if you are sensitive to any ingredient in this product When using this product • do not touch tip of container to any surface to avoid contamination • replace cap after each use Stop use and ask a doctor if • you feel eye pain • changes in vision occur • redness or irritation of the eye ( s ) gets worse or lasts more than 72 hours If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) right away .
