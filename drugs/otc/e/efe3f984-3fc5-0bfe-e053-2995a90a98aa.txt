Drug Facts Active ingredient Aluminum chlorohydrate 24 % ( anhydrous ) Purpose Antiperspirant Use reduces underarm wetness Warnings For external use only .
Flammable • do not spray near flame or while smoking • contents under pressure • do not crush , puncture or incinerate • do not expose to heat or store at temperature above 120 ° F Do not use on broken skin .
Ask a doctor before use if you have kidney disease .
When using this product • keep away from face and mouth to avoid breathing it • avoid spraying in eyes • use only as directed ; intentional misuse by deliberately concentrating and inhaling the contents can be harmful or fatal Stop use if rash or irritation occurs Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
