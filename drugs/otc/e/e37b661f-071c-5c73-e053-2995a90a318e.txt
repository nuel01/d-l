Purpose Salicylic Acid 2 . 00 % ..............................................
Acne Treatment Uses • Helps fight existing acne breakouts and prevents future acne breakouts .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product skin irritation and dryness are more likely to occur when using another topical acne product at the same time .
If irritation occurs , use only one acne product at a time .
Avoid eye area .
In case of eye contact , flush gently and thoroughly with water .
Do not apply to broken or irritated skin .
Keep out of reach of children .
