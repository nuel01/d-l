Drug Facts Active ingredient Hydrogen peroxide ( stabilized ) 3 % Purpose First aid antiseptic Use first aid to help prevent the risk of infection in : • minor cuts • scrapes • burns Warnings For external use only .
Ask a doctor before use if you have • deep or puncture wounds • animal bites • serious burns When using this product • do not get in eyes • do not apply over large areas • do not use longer than 1 week unless directed by a doctor Stop use and ask a doctor if condition persists or gets worse .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • clean the affected area • apply product on the affected area of the body 1 to 3 times a day • may be covered with a sterile bandage • if bandaged , let dry first Other information • do not apply internally .
• keep canister tightly closed and stored at room temperature .
