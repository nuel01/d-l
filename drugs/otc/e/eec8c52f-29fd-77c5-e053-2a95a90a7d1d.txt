Intentional misuse by deliberately concentrating and inhaling contents can be harmful or fatal • contents under pressure .
Do not puncture or incinerate .
Do not store at temperature above 120 ° F . Stop use and ask a doctor if • irritation occurs • there is no improvement within 2 weeks Do not use on children under 2 years of age unless directed by a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
