DESCRIPTION This daily hydrating lotion leaves skin feeling soft , smooth , and moisturized for up to 8 hours .
Protects skin from the effects of extreme weather and climate changes while SPF 15 shields from the harmful effects of the sun .
INDICATIONS AND USAGE To Use : Smooth on to cleansed face and throat daily before sun exposure .
WARNINGS Warning : For external use only .
Avoid contact with eyes .
Discontinue use if signs of irritation or rash appear .
If irritation or rash persists , consult a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
