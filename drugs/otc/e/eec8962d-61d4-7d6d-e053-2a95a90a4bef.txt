Drug Facts Active ingredient Miconazole nitrate 2 % Purpose Antifungal Uses • Cures most athlete ' s foot ( tinea pedis ) , jock itch ( tinea cruris ) and ringworm ( tinea corporis ) • relieves itching , cracking , burning and scaling Warnings For external use only Avoid contact with the eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if • irritation occurs • if there is no improvement within 4 weeks ( for athlete ' s foot and ringworm ) or 2 weeks ( for jock itch ) Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
