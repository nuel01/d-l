Drug facts Active ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 70 % Purpose Antimicrobial Uses : Hand Sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
Warnings : For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
May discolor certain fabrics .
Do not use In children under 2 months of age , and on open skin wounds .
When using this product Keep out of eyes , ears and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach children if swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Spray hands thoroughly with product and allow to dry without wiping • Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing • Do not use other than directed .
Other information • Store between 59 ° - 86 ° F ( 15 ° - 30 ° C ) • Avoid freezing and excessive heat above 104 ° F ( 40 ° C ) Inactive ingredients Water ( aqua ) , Vegetable Glycerin , Fragrance .
