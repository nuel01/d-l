INDICATIONS : Sodium Sulfacetemide 10 % Wash is intended for topical application in the following scaling dermatoses : seborrheic dermatisis and seborrhea sicca ( dandruff ) .
It also is indicated for the treatment of secondary bacterial infections of the skin due to organisms susceptible to sulfonamides .
DIRECTIONS FOR USE : Wash affected areas twice daily ( morning and evening ) or as directed by your physician .
Rinse thoroughly and pat dry .
