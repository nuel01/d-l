Active ingredient Menthol 10 . 0 % Purpose Topical analgesic Uses For the temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with : • simple backache • arthritis • strains • bruises • sprains Warnings For external use only .
Avoid contact with eyes Flammable : keep away from fire or flame Do not puncture or incinerate .
Contents under pressure .
When using this product • use only as directed • do not bandage tightly or use with heating pad • do not apply to wounds or damaged skin Stop use and ask doctor if • condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days • redness is present • excessive irritation of the skin develops If pregnant or breastfeeding , ask a health professional before use .
Keep our of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
