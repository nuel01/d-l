Drug Facts Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antimicrobial Use To decrease bacteria on the skin that could cause disease .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use in or near the eyes .
In case of contact with eyes , flush thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask doctor if skin irritation develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions Apply a small amount to palms and rub hands thoroughly until completely dry .
Other Information Do not freeze .
Inactive Ingredients Purified Water , Glycerin , Cocamidopropyl Betaine .
