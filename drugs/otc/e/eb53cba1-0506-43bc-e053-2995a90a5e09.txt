Active Ingredient ( s ) Isopropyl Alcohol 99 % v / v .
Purpose : First aid antiseptic Purpose First Aid Antiseptic Use first aid to help prevent the risk of infection in minor cuts , scrapes , burns .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat , flame or spark .
If taken internally , serious gastric disturbances will result .
Ask a doctor before use if you have deep or puncture wounds , animal bites or serious burns When using this product do not get into eyes do not apply over the large areas of the body do not use longer than week unless directed by a doctor If condition persists or gets worse .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
