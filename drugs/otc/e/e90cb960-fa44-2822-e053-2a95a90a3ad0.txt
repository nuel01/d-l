Flammable ; keep away from heat or flame Do not use on • deep or puncture wounds • animal bites • serious burns When using this product • avoid spraying in eyes • do not intentionally inhale • do not puncture or incinerate ; contents under pressure Stop using and see a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of reach of children .
