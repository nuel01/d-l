Active Ingredient Menthol 5 % Purpose Topical analgesic Use Temporarily relieves minor aches and pains of muscles and joints due to • Arthritis • Simple backache • Strains • Sprains • Bruises Warnings For external use only Do not use • On wounds or damaged skin • With a heating pad • If you are allergic to any ingredients of this product When using this product • Use only as directed • Avoid contact with the eyes , mucous membranes or rashes Stop use and ask a doctor if • Excessive redness or irritation is present • Conditions worsen • Symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days If pregnant or breast - feeding ask a health professional before use Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) right away .
