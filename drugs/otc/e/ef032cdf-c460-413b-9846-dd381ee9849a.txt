Active Ingredients Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 12 % Purpose Antiseptic Use First aid to help reduce the risk of infection in minor cuts , scrapes , and burns .
Warnings For external use only .
Stop use and consult a doctor if the condition persists or gets worse .
Do not use longer than 1 week unless directed by a physician .
In case of deep or puncture wounds , animal bites , or serious burn , consult a physician .
Keep out of reach of children . if swallowed get medical help or contact Poison control Center right away .
Directions Clean and dry the affected area .
Apply a sterile bandage on the area 1 to 3 times daily .
Other information : not intended for use on delicate or sensitive skin .
