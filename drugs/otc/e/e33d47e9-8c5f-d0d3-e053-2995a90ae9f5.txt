Active Ingredient ( in each use ) Benzalkonium Chloride . 13 % Purpose Antimicrobial Uses For hand washing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only When using this product Avoid contact with eyes .
In case of contact flush eyes with water .
Do not ingest , Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develop or condition persists .
Keep Out Of Reach Of Children If swallowed get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions Apply to wet hands .
Work into a lather and rinse thoroughly .
