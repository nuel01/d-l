Warnings For external only : When using this product ■ avoid contact with eyes ; if contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water .
■ If excessive skin irritation develops or increases , discontinue use ; if irritation persists , consult a health care professional .
■ it is recommended that prior to exposure to the sun , users cover with sunscreen areas to which AHAs have been applied ( i . e . glycolic acid , lactic acid ) and for one week afterwards .
■ using other topical acne products at the same time or right after use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one drug should be used unless directed by a health care professional .
