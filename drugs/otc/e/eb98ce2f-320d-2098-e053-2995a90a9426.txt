Drug facts Active ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 75 % Purpose Antimicrobial Uses : Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
Warnings : For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
Do not use : In children under 2 months of age .
And on open skin wounds When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
if swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
Other information • Store between 59 ° - 86 ° F [ 15 ° - 30 ° C ] • Avoid freezing and excessive heat above 104 ° F [ 40 ° C ] Inactive ingredients Aqua [ water ] , Glycerin , Carbomer , Aminomethyl propanol , Fragrance .
