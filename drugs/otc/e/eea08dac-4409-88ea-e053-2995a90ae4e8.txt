Active ingredients Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antimicrobial Uses • for hand sanitizing to decrease bacteria on the skin • recommended for repeated use Warnings For external use only .
Do not use if you are allergic to any of these ingredients .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
In case of contact , flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops , or if condition persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of the reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • adults and children 2 years and over : apply to hands : allow to dry without wiping • children under 2 years : ask a doctor before use .
