Directions Directions * Clean the skin before applying this product * Cover the entire affected area with a thin layer one to three times daily * Because excessive drying of the skin may occur , start with one application daily , then gradually increase to two or three times daily if needed or as directed by a doctor * If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce applications to once a day or every other day * If irritation occurs , cease use and consult a physician .
Use only as directed on unbroken skin .
Patch test prior to use .
