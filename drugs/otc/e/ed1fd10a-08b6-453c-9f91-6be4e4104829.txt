Active Ingredient Purified water 99 . 1 % Purpose Emergency eyewash and skin flush Uses For flushing the eye and skin to reduce chances of severe injury caused by acid , alkali , or particulate contamination .
Warnings For external use only Do not use • for injection • in intraocular surgery • internally • if solution changes color or becomes cloudy .
When using this product • avoid contamination , do not touch tip of container to any surface • do not reuse .
Once opened , discard .
Ask doctor if you have • eye pain • changes in vision • redness or irritation of the eye after use • an injury caused by alkali Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • do not dilute solution or reuse bottle • hold container a few inches above the eye or skin • control flow of solution by pressure on bottle • flush affected area as needed • continue to flush with water if necessary • obtain medical treatment Other Information • not for use as a contact lens solution • twist cap to open • use before expiration date marked on bottle • store at room temperature , 5 ° to 35 ° C ( 41 ° to 95 ° F ) .
