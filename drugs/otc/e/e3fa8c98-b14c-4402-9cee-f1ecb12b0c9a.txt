For At - Risk Skin Due to Incontinence Drug Facts Active ingredient Dimethicone , 2 % Purpose Diaper Rash Skin Protectant Uses Helps treat and prevent minor skin irritation due to diaper rash .
Helps seal out wetness .
Warnings When using this product • avoid contact with eyes • not to be applied over deep or puncture wounds or lacerations .
Consult a doctor • if condition worsens or does not improve within 7 days .
For external use only .
Keep this and all drugs out of the reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • change wet and soiled diapers promptly • spray affected area and gently wipe with clean , soft cloth • apply as often as necessary , with each diaper change , especially at bedtime or any time when exposure to wet diapers may be prolonged .
