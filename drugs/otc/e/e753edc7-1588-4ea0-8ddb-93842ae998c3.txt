Active ingredient Ethanol 60 % by volume Purpose Antiseptic handwash Uses • For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin Warnings For external use only FLAMMABLE .
Keep away from fire or flame , heat , sparks and sources of static discharge .
Do not use • In eyes • on deep cuts or puncture wound When using this product • If in eyes , rinse thoroughly with water • Discontinue use if irritation and redness develop Stop use and ask a doctor if • Eye or skin irritation or redness occurs for more than 72 hours Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
