Active Ingredients and Purpose Allantoin 0 . 5 % ( Skin Protectant ) , Lidocaine HCl 0 . 5 % ( External Analgesic ) Uses For temporary relief and protection from itching associated with minor skin irritations and rashes .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use on wounds or damaged skin .
Avoid contact with eyes or mucous membranes .
If symptoms persist for more than 7 days , discontinue use and consult a physician .
If pregnant or breast feeding , contact a physician prior to use .
Directions : Adults and children 12 years of age and older : Apply directly to affected area .
Do not use more than 4 times per day .
Do not bandage damaged skin .
Store at room temperature .
Children under 12 years of age : ask a doctor .
