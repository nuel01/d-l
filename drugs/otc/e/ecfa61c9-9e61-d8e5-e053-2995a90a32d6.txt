Drug Facts Active Ingredients Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Lidocaine HCl 4 . 0 % Purpose First Aid Antiseptic Topical Analgesic Uses First Aid to help prevent bacterial infection associated with contamination of the skin .
Temporarily relieves pain and itching due to : • minor cuts • scrapes • minor burns Warnings For external use only .
Avoid contact with eyes .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Stop use and ask doctor : if condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days .
