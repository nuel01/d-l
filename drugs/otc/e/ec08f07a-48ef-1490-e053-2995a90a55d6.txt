Drug Facts Active Ingredient Salicylic Acid , 2 % Purpose Antidandruff Use : Controls scalp itching and flaking due to dandruff Warnings For external use only When using this product avoid contact with eyes .

Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or call a Poison Control Center at once .
