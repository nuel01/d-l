Drug Facts Active ingredient ( in each gram ) Tetracycline hydrochloride 30 mg Purpose First Aid Antibiotic Use First aid to help prevent skin infection in minor cuts , scrapes , and burns .
Warnings For external use only .
May be harmful if swallowed .
Allergy alert : Do not use if allergic to any ingredient listed on this label .
Do not use • in the eyes • over large areas of the body • longer than 1 week unless directed by doctor Ask a doctor before use if you have • deep or puncture wounds • animal bites • serious burns Stop use and ask a doctor if condition persists or gets worse .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Clean the affected area .
• Apply a small amount of this product ( an amount equal to the surface area of the tip of a finger ) on the area 1 to 3 times daily .
• May be covered with a sterile bandage .
Other information • Keep product refrigerated to preserve its effectiveness and color • Stop use if product is misused : If the bottle is left open and / or if not refrigerated , the liquid will tend to turn black over time .
Discard the product if the liquid turns black due to misuse .
• This product is an OTC antibiotic for human use .
• Contains no alcohol , no animal ingredients .
• Blended for typical skin color .
• May stain cloth .
• No claims regarding stem cell healing are implied for this product .
