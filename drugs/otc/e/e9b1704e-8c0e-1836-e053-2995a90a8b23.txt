Active ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 62 % v / v Purpose Antiseptic Use • helps reduce bacteria that can cause skin infections Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
Do not use • in the eyes • on children under 2 years of age unless directed by a doctor Ask a doctor before use if injuries are • deep or puncture wounds • serious burns Stop use and ask a doctor if • redness , irritation , swelling or pain persists or increases • infection occurs Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek professional assistance or consult a poison control center immediately .
Directions [ MULTIMEDIA ] 1 .
Use a tissue to clean the inside of both nostrils , including the inside tip of nostril .
Discard .
2 .
Insert swab comfortably into right nostril and rotate for 30 seconds , covering all surfaces .
3 .
Using same swab , repeat step 2 with left nostril .
4 .
Do not blow nose .
If solution drips , gently wipe with a tissue .
