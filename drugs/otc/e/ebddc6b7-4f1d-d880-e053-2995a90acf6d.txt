Warnings For external use only .
Flammable , keep away from fire or flame .
Do not use • near eyes or mucous membranes • on deep or puncture wounds , animal bites , or serious burns consult a physician • on large areas of the body Stop use and ask a doctor if redness , irritation , swelling or pain persists or increases .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
