Drug Facts Active ingredients Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose First aid antiseptic Uses First aid to help prevent infection & for the temporary pain relief in minor cuts , scrapes and burns Warnings For external use only .
Flammable keep away from tire or flame Do not use • near eyes or mucous membranes • on deep or puncture wounds , animal bites , or serious burns • over large areas of the body • more than one week unless directed by a doctor Stop use and ask a doctor if condition persists or gets worse Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • clean affected area & spray 1 to 3 times daily • may be covered with a sterile bandage • not to be used on children under 12 years of age Inactive ingredients ethyl alcohol , purified water Questions or comments ?
