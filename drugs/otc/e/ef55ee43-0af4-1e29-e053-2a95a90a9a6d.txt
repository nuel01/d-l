Ask a doctor before use if you have • stomach pain , nausea , or vomiting • a sudden change in bowel habits that lasts over 14 days Ask a doctor or pharmacist before use if you are presently taking mineral oil Stop use and ask a doctor if • you have rectal bleeding or no bowel movement after using this product .
These could be signs of a serious condition .
