Keep away from fire or flame , heat , spark , electrical Ask a doctor before use if you have Deep or puncture wounds , animal bites or serious burns .
When using this product • do not get into eyes • Do not inhale • Do not apply over large ares of the body • Do not use longer than 1 week Stop use and ask a doctor if condition persists or gets worse Keep out of reach of children .

Caution Fumes can be acutely irritating to skin , eyes , and the respiratory system .
Do not apply to irritated skin or if excessive irritation develops .
Avoid getting into the eyes or on mucous membranes .
Avoid inhaling this product Directions • Clean the affected area .
• Apply a small amount of the product on the area 1 to 3 times daily .
• May be covered with a sterile bandage .
• If bandaged , let dry first .
