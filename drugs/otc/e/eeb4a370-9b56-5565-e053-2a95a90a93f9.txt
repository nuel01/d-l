Active ingredient ( in each tablet ) Simethicone 125 mg Purpose Antigas Use for the relief of pressure , bloating , and fullness commonly referred to as gas Warnings If pregnant or breast feeding , ask a health professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of overdose , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • chew thoroughly • adults and children 12 years and over : take 1 - 2 tablets as needed after meals and at bedtime .
Do not exceed 4 tablets in 24 hours except under the advice and supervision of a doctor .
• children under 12 years : ask a doctor Other information • Tamper Evident : Do not use if imprinted seal under cap is missing or broken .
