IVES SOLUTIONS ACNE CONTROL SPOT TREATMENT - ( Salicylic Acid ) lotion Active ingredient Salicylic Acid ( 2 % ) Purpose Acne Treatment Uses For the mangement of acne Helps prevent new acne pimples and acne blemishes from forming Warnings For external use only When using this product skin irritation and dryness is more likely to occur if you use another topical acne medication at the same time .
If irritation occues , only use one topical acne medication at a time .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Cleanse skin thoroughly before applying product Cover the entire affected area with a thin layer one to three times daily Because excessive drying of the skin may occur , start with one application daily , then gradually increase to two or three times daily , if needed or as directed by a doctor .
If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to once a day or every other day .
Other information Product contains menthol .
