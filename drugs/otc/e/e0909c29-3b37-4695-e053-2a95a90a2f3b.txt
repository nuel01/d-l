Drug Facts Active Ingredient Lidocaine HCL 4 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Uses For the temporary relief of pain and itching associated with : • Sunburn • Minor Bums • Minor Skin Irritation • Cuts & Scrapes • Insect Bites Warnings For external use only .
Avoid contact with eyes .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Stop use and ask doctor : if condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days .
If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health care professional before use .
Do not use in large quantities , particularly over raw surfaces or blistered areas .
Directions Adults and children 2 years of age or older • Clean the burn area with mild soap and water .
• Gently apply the Alocane • Pad over the burn or wound .
• Use on affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily • Children under 2 years of age consult a doctor .
