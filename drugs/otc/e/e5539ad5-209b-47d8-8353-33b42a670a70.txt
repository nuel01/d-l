DRUG FACTS Active ingredient : Ethyl alcohol 72 % v / v Purpose : Antiseptic Uses : Kills harmful bacteria and germs .
For personal hand hygiene to help prevent the spread of bacteria .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings : For external use only .
Flammability .
Keep away from open flame and sources of heat .
Do not use : on children less than 2 years old or on open skin wounds .
When using this product keep out of eyes , ears and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and consult a health care practitioner if irritation develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions : Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
Supervise children when they use this product to avoid swallowing .
