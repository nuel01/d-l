DRUG FACTS ACTIVE INGREDIENTS Menthol 0 . 11 % Zinc Oxide 5 . 1 % PURPOSE Analgesic , Anesthetic and Antipruritic Astringent USE Helps relieve local itching and discomfort of the perianal area .
Temporarily forms a protective barrier over inflamed tissues to help prevent drying of tissues .
For the temporary relief of pain , burning and skin irritation .
Can help distract the pain and may provide cooling sensation .
WARNINGS For external use only • If condition worsens or does not improve within 7 days , consult a doctor • Do not exceed the recommended daily dose unless directed by a doctor • In case of bleeding , consult a doctor promptly • Certain persons can develop allergic reactions to ingredients in this product .
If the symptom being treated does not subside or if redness , irritation , swelling , pain or other symptoms develop or increase , discontinue use and consult a doctor Keep out of reach of children .
DIRECTIONS When practical , cleanse the affected area with mild soap and warm water .
Pat or allow to air dry .
Apply a layer of GranuLotion ® up to 6 times daily for inflammation , 1 time per day for continued site maintenance .
Use a soft tipped applicator for hard to reach areas .
Repeat as needed to protect the skin or until the condition resolves .
