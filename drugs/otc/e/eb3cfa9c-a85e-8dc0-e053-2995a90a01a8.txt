Do not use • if you have ever had an allergic reaction to this product or any of its ingredients When using this product • avoid contact with the eyes and mucous membranes • do not bandage tightly • do not use with a heating pad , medicated patch or other types of local heat • do not apply to wounds or damaged , broken or irritated skin Stop use • condition worsens or irritation develops • pain , swelling or blistering develops where product was applied • redness or severe burning develops where product was applied • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a healthcare professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
