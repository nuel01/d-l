Drug Facts Active Ingredient Purpose Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Lidocaine HCL 4 % First Aid Antiseptic Topical Analgesic Uses : First aid to help prevent bacterial infection associated with contamination of the skin .
Temporarily relieves pain and itching due to : • Minor Burns • Minor Cuts & Scrapes • Minor Skin Irritations Warnings For external use only .
Avoid contact with eyes .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek medical help or call a Poison Control Center right away .
Stop use and ask doctor : if condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days .
If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health care professional before use .
Directions Adults and children 2 years of age and older : Apply to affected areas not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
Children under 2 years of age : consult a doctor .
Other Information Store at room temperature 15 ° - 30 ° C ( 59 ° - 86 ° F ) Inactive Ingredients 1 , 3 - Propanediol , Aloe Barbadensis ( Aloe ) Leaf Juice , Caprylyl Glycol , Chlorphenesin , Dimethyl Isosorbide , Hydroxyethyl Cellulose , Phenoxyethanol , Tocopheryl Acetate ( Vitamin E ) , Water .
