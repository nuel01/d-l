Drug Facts : Active Ingredient White Petrolatum USP 100 % Purpose Skin Protectant Indications For the temporary protection of minor cuts , scrapes , abrasions , burns , sunburns , chafed or chapped skin .
Helps treat and prevent diaper rash .
Caution In case of deep or puncture wounds or severe burns , consult a physician .
If irritation , swelling or pain persists , or if infection occurs , discontinue use and consult a physician .
For external use only .
Warnings Do not use in the eyes .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Keep out of reach of children .
Directions Cleanse area with mild soap and water .
Apply White Petrolatum liberally generously to affected area .
Other Information Store at room temperature 15 ° C - 30 ° C ( 59 ° F - 86 ° F ) .
Avoid excessive heat .
Description White Petrolatum USP is a first aid ointment that protects and revitalizes chapped and cracked skin areas .
It contains 100 % White Petrolatum USP to help seal moisture into the skin .
White Petrolatum USP Ointment can be applied to minor skin injuries including cuts , scrapes , rashes and burns .
Each pouch contains a 5 g unit dose individually sealed and barcoded .
