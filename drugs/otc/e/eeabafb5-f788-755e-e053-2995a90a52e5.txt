Drug Facts Active ingredient Sodium fluoride 0 . 02 % ( 0 . 01 % w / v fluoride ion ) Purpose Anticavity Use aids in prevention of dental cavities Warnings Stop use and ask dentist if oral irritation or tooth sensitivity occurs Keep out of reach of children .
If more than used for rinsing is accidentally swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Adults and children 12 years of age and older : • use twice a day after brushing your teeth with a toothpaste • vigorously swish 10 mL ( 2 teaspoonfuls ) of rinse between your teeth for 1 minute and then spit out • do not swallow the rinse • do not eat or drink for 30 minutes after rinsing • supervise children as necessary until capable of using without supervision • Children under 12 years of age : consult a dentist or doctor Other information • Protect from excessive heat ( 40 ° C / 104 ° F ) Inactive ingredients Water , alcohol ( 10 . 5 % v / v ) , sorbitol , hydrogen peroxide , PEG - 40 hydrogenated castor oil , poloxamer 407 , sodium saccharin , menthol , flavor , phosphoric acid , disodium phosphate , sucralose Questions ?
