DRUG FACTS Active Ingredient Menthol ( 5 % ) Purpose Topical Analgesic Uses Provides penetrating temporary relief for minor pain in muscles and joints associated with : • Backache • Arthritis • Bruises • Sprains Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • with a heating pad .
Do not apply to wounds or damaged skin .
Do not bandage tightly after use .
• Avoid contact with eyes .
• Discontinue use and consult a physician if condition worsens , or if symptoms persist more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a poison control center right away .
Directions Adults and children over the age of 2 : Apply generously to affected area .
Massage well into painful area .
Repeat as necessary , but not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
Children under the age of 2 : Do not use ; consult a physician .
