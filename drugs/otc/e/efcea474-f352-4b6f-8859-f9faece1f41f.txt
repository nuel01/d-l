Warnings : FOR EXTERNAL USE ONLY Do not use • Apply on wounds , irritated or damaged skin .
• Otherwise than as directed When using this product • Avoid contact with the eyes , mouth or mucous membranes .
• Do not bandage tightly .
Stop use and ask a doctor if : • Condition worsens , symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days .
• Excessive irritation of the skin develops .
• When using this product for arthritis : Pain persists for more than 10 days , redness is present or in conditions affecting children under 12 years of age If pregnant or breast feeding , see the advise of a health professional before using this product .
Keep out of reach of children to avoid accidental poisoning .
If swallowed , contact a Doctor or Poison Control Center immediately Directions Adults and children2 years of age and older : Apply to affected area not more than 3 - 4 times daily .
