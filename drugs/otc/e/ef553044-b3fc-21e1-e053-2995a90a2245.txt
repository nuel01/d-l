Drug Facts Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 12 % Purpose Antimicrobial Uses • Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria .
• For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • in children less than 2 months old .
• on open skin wounds .
When using this product do not get in eyes .
If contact is made rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash appears and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Apply to hands , allow to air dry without wiping .
• Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
Other information • Store in a cool , dry place .
• Avoid freezing and excessive heat .
Inactive ingredients Glycerin , Phenoxyethanol , Propylene Glycol , Purified Water Question ?
