Active Ingredient .
Benzalkonium Chloride ( 0 . 13 % ) Purpose Antibacterial Uses Helps eliminate bacteria on hands .
Warnings For external use only When using this product Avoid contact with eyes .
In case of contact , rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor .
If irritation or redness develops and lasts Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • apply onto wet hands ( only for 222 ml and 333 ml ) • Lather and rinse thoroughly .
