Drug Facts Active Ingredient Hydrogen Peroxide 3 . 0 % Purpose First aid antiseptic Uses First aid to help prevent infection in minor cuts , scrapes and burns Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • on large areas of the body • near eyes or mucous membranes • on deep or puncture wounds , animal bites , or serious burns Stop use and ask a doctor if • redness , irritation , swelling or pain persists or increases Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • spray over affected area • if bandaged , let dry first Other information • store at room temperature Inactive ingredient purified water Questions or Comments ?
