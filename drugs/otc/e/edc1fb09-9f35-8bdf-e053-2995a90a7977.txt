Active Ingredient Miconazole Nitrate 2 % Purpose Antifungal Uses • For the treatment of most athlete ' s foot , tinea pedis , jock itch , tinea crusis , ringworm , tinea corporis • Relieves itching , scaling , burning , discomfort and chafing associated with jock itch or itching , burning feet Warnings For external use only Do not use • on children under 2 years of age unless directed by a doctor • for diaper rash When using this product avoid contact with the eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if • irritation occurs • there is no improvement within 2 weeks when used for the treatment of jock itch • there is no improvement within 4 weeks when used for athlete ' s foot or ringworm If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center ( 1800 - 222 - 1222 ) right away .
