Ask a doctor before using any laxative if you have • abdominal pain , nausea or vomiting • a sudden change in bowel habits lasting more than 2 weeks • already used a laxative formore than 1 week Stop use and consult a doctor if you have • rectal bleeding • no bowel movement after using this product These symptoms may indicate a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
