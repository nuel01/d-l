Drug Facts Active ingredient Salicylic acid 2 % Purpose Acne treatment Uses • treats acne • clears acne blemishes • helps prevent development of new acne blemishes Warnings For external use only .
When using this product using other topical acne drugs at the same time or right after use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one medication should be used unless directed by a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
