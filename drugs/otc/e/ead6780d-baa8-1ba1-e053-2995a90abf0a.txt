Active ingredient Menthol 2 . 0 % Purpose Topical analgesic Uses • for the temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints Warnings For external use only When using this product • avoid contact with the eyes • do not apply to wounds or damaged skin • do not bandage tightly Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use .
