Camphor 10 % For the temporary relief of minor aches , muscle and joint pain associated with arthritis , strains , bruises and simple backache .
For external use only .
Do not drink .
If swallowed , immediately give 3 or 4 glasses of water .
Do not induce vomiting .
If vomiting occurs , give fluids again .
Do not give anything by mouth to an unconscious or convulsing person .
Get medical attention immediately .
Avoid contact with eyes or mucous membranes .
Do not apply to irritated skin .
Counterirritant In case of accidental ingestion , seek professional assistance or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
In case of eye contact , flush eyes with running water for 15 minutes , get medical attention .
Alcohol 84 % , Purified Water ( dilute 3 parts olive oil and mix well ) Adults and children 2 yrs , and older .
Apply to the affected area no more than 3 or 4 times daily .
Children under 2 yrs of age : consult a doctor before use .
