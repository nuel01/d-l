Drug Facts Active ingredient Lidocaine HCl 4 % Purpose Topical analgesic Uses for the temporary relief of pain .
Warnings For external use only When using this product • ◆ avoid contact with eyes • ◆ do not apply to wounds or damaged skin • ◆ do not bandage tightly • ◆ do not use with heating pads or other heating devices • ◆ as with any drug , if you are pregnant or nursing a baby , seek the advice of a health professional before using this product • ◆ do not use in large quantities , particularly over raw surfaces or blistered areas Discontinue use and consult a doctor if condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
