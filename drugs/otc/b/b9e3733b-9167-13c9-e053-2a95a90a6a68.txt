Drug Facts Active ingredients Salicylic Acid 1 . 8 % Purpose Acne Treatment Use • For the treatment of acne • Helps prevent new acne blemishes Warnings For external use only .
• Keep out of eyes .
Rinse with water to remove .
• Skin irritation and dryness is more likely to occur if you use another topical acne medication at a time .
Stop use and ask a doctor if rash occurs .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away Directions • Cleanse the skin thoroughly before applying medication .
• Cover the entire affected area with a thin layer one to three times daily .
• Because excessive drying of the skin may occur , start with one application daily , then gradually increase to two or three times daily if needed or as directed by a doctor .
• If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to once a day every other day .
