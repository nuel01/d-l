Drug Facts Active Ingredient : Benzalkonium chloride % 0 , 13 Purpose Antibacterial Use For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product • Do not use in the eyes .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • Irritation and redness develop .
If condition persist for more than 72 hours consult a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Wet hands and forearms .
Apply 5 milliliters ( 2 full pumps ) to hands and forearms .
Scrub thoroughly for 60 seconds .
Rinse and repeat .
