If you do not know if your prescription drug contains and MAOI , ask a doctor or pharmacist before taking this product .
Ask a doctor before use if you have • • diabetes • • high blood pressure • • heart disease • • thyroid disease • • trouble urinating due to an enlarged prostate gland When using this product • • do not use more than directed Stop use and ask a doctor if • • nervousness , dizziness , or sleeplessness occur • • symptoms do not improve within 7 days or occur with fever If pregnant or breast - feeding , • • ask a health professional before use .
