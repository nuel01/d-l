Active Ingredient ( s ) Alcohol 75 % ( v / v ) , Antibacterial agents Purpose Antiseptic , Hand Sanitizer Use 1 .
Can be sprayed on cotton ball or tissue paper for local disinfection .
2 .
Can be directly sprayed on the intact surface of the human body for disinfection .
3 .
For general objects , disinfection and spraying can be performed for 2 - 3 seconds .
Larger space disinfection will increase the spraying time accordingly .
4 .
After spraying , wait 2 - 3 minutes for best result .
Warnings Flammable . Keep away from the source of fire and flame . For external use only .
Do not use In children less than 2 months of age On open skin wounds , damaged , inflamed or broken skin .
When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth . In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs . People who are allergic to alcohol should use this product with caution .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs . These maybe signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children . If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Widely used in households , public elevators , car interiors , offices and other objects as well as glass and metal surfaces . This product is also safe to use on human skin as disinfectant and cleaning solution .
Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
