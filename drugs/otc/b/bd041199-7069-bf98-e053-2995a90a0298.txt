Drug Facts Active ingredient Alcohol 62 % Purpose Antiseptic Use • For hand washing to decrease bacteria on the skin , only when water are not available Warnings For external use only Flammable , keep away from fire and flame When using this product Do not use in eyes .
If contact occurs , flush eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develop .
Keep out of the reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact the Poison Control Center right away .
