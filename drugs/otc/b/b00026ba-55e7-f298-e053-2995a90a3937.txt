Drug Facts Active Ingredient Benzalkonium chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antiseptic Skin Sanitizer Uses • Sanitizer to help decrease bacteria on the skin .
• Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use in ears or mouth and avoid mucus membranes .
When using this product • Avoid contact with the eyes .
• In case of contact flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if redness or irritation develop and persist for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
Children should be supervised when using this product .
Directions • Apply a small amount into palms of hands and spread on both hands .
• Rub into skin until dry .
