Active ingredient Ethyl alcohol 62 % Purpose Skin Antiseptic Uses To decrease bacteria on hands .
Warnings Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only .
When using this product Do not use in the eyes .
In case of contact , immediately flush with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develop .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours , consult a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Apply 1 - 2 ml into hands , rub product vigorously over hands covering all surfaces .
Allow to air dry .
