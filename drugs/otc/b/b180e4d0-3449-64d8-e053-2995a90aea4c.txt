Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % v / v Purpose Hand Sanitizer , Antiseptic Use Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only Do not use if you are allergic to any of the ingredients When using this product do not use in or near the eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops and lasts for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions For adults and children 3 years and over , wipe hands and discard .
Allow hands to air dry .
