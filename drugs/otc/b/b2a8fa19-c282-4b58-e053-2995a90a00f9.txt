Directions Open the hard plastic snap cover to reveal the adhesive label that reads " OPEN " .
Peel back the adhesive label and remove one wipe at a time .
Wipe your hands with the antibacterial wipe until all of the solution is absorbed .
Seal the packaging tightly when done .
Storage Store in a cool , dry place .
Store out of reach of children .
Do not store near food , drinks , or animal feed .
Store away from direct sunlight and open flame .
