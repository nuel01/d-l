Drug facts Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 1 % Purpose Antimicrobial Uses • for hand sanitizing to decrease the bacteria on the skin • recommended for repeated use Warnings For external use only .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops , or if condition persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children if swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
