Ask your doctor or pharmacist before use if you are using blood thining medication , steroids , or non - steroidal anti - inflammatory drugs ( NSAIDS ) When using this product • do not use other topical products • avoid contact with eyes or mucous membranes • keep away from excessive heat Stop use or ask a doctor if • Condition worsens • Symptons persist for more than seven days If pregnant or breastfeeding ask a physican before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If put in mouth , get medical help or contact a poison control center right away .
