Drug Facts Active Ingredient Ethyl alcohol 75 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses • Hand sanitiser to help reduce bacteria .
• For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
Do not use • In children less than 2 months of age .
• On open skin wounds .
When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Centre right away .
Directions : • Apply to hands , allow to air dry without wiping .
• Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
