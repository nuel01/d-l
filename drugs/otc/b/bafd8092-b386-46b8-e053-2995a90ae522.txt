Drug Facts Active ingredients ( each gram contains ) Bacitracin zinc 400 units Neomycin sulfate 3 . 5 mg Polymyxin B sulfate 5 , 000 units Purpose First Aid Antibiotic Ointment Uses first aid to help prevent infection in minor : ● cuts ● scrapes ● burns Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • In the eyes • Over large areas of the body • If you are allergic to any of the ingredients Stop Use and ask a doctor if • Condition persists or gets worse • You need to use longer than 1 week • A rash or other allergic reaction develops Ask Doctor before use if you have • Deep or puncture wounds • Animal bites • Serious burns Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions ● clean the affected area and dry thoroughly .
● apply a small amount of this product ( an amount equal to the surface area of the tip of a finger ) on the area 1 to 3 times daily ● may be covered with a sterile bandage .
