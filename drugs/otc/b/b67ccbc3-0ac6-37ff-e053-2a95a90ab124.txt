Drug Facts Active ingredient Pyrithione Zinc 0 . 1 % Purpose Anti - dandruff , Anti - seborrheic dermatitis Uses • helps prevent recurrence of flaking , itching , irritation , scaling and redness associated with dandruff and seborrheic dermatitis .
Warnings For external use only .
Ask a doctor before use if you have a condition that covers a large area of the body .
When using this product • avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens or does not improve after regular use of this product as directed .
Keep this and all drugs out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • apply to affected areas one to four times daily or as directed by a doctor .
