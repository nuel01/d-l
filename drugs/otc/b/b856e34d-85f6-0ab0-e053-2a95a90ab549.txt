Drug Facts Active ingredients ( in each gram ) Bacitracin zinc ( bacitracin 400 units ) Neomycin sulfate ( neomycin 3 . 5 mg ) Polymyxin B sulfate ( polymyxin B 5 , 000 units ) Purpose First aid antibiotic Use • First aid to help prevent infection in minor cuts , scrapes , and burns .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • in the eyes • over large areas of the body • if you are allergic to any of the ingredient • longer than 1 week unless directed by a doctor .
Ask a doctor before use if you have • deep or punture wounds • animal bites • serious burns .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • the condition persists or gets worse • a rash or other allergic reaction develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Clean the affected area .
• Apply a small amount of this product ( an amount equal to the surface area of the tip of a finger ) on the area 1 to 3 times daily .
• May be covered with a sterile bandage .
