Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antibacterial Use ( s ) • For hand sanitizing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
• Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product avoid contact with the eyes .
In case of contact , flush eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develops and persists .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • Take wipe from pouch and rub thoroughly over all surfaces of both hands .
• Rub hands together briskly to dry .
• Dispose of wipe .
Other information • Store below 110 F ( 43 C ) .
• May discolor certain fabrics or surfaces .
Inactive ingredients Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice , Decyl Glucoside , Glycerin , Purified Water .
