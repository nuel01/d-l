Drug Facts Active Ingredients Isopropyl Alcohol 70 % V / V Purpose Antiseptic Use • For Preparation of the skin prior to injection and to decrease germs in minor cuts and scrapes .
• Non - sterile Solution .
• Applicator is sterile if package is intact .
Warnings • For external use only .
• Flammable , keep away from fire or flame .
Do not use • With electrocautery procedures .
• In the eyes .
If contact occurs , flush eyes with water .
Stop use If irritation and redness develops and persists for more than 72 hours , consult your health care practitioner .
Keep out of reach of children Except with adult supervision .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a poison control center right away .
Directions • Apply topically as needed to cleanse intended area .
• Discard after single use .
Other information Store at room temperature 15 ° C - 30 ° C ( 59 ° F - 86 ° F ) .
Inactive ingredients Purified water .
