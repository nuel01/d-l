Active ingredients ( in 10 mL ) Dextromethorphan hydrobromide 30 mg Guaifenesin 200 mg Purpose Dextromethorphan hydrobromide Cough suppressant Guaifenesin Expectorant Uses For the temporary relief of cough due to the common cold .
Helps loosen phlegm ( mucus ) and thin bronchial secretions to make coughs more productive .
