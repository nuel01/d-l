Active ingredient Salicylic acid 40 % w / w Purpose Corn remover Use • for the removal of corns Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • if you are a diabetic • have poor blood circulation • on irritated skin or any area that is infected or reddened Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
If discomfort persists see your doctor or podiatrist .
Directions • wash affected area : may soak corn in warm water for 5 minutes • dry area thoroughly • remove bandage strips by peeling backing off at slit • carefully adhere bandage with the medicated portion directly over corn • after 48 hours remove medicated bandage strip • repeat this procedure every 48 hours as needed ( until corn is removed ) for up to 14 days Other information store between 59 ° to 86 ° F ( 15 ° to 30 ° C ) Inactive ingredients acrylic adhesive , brown iron oxide , polyethylene foam Questions ?
