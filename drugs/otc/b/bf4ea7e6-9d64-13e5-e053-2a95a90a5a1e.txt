DROG DOSAGE INSTRUCTIONS FOR ADMINISTRATION : Adults and children 12 years and over : 1 tablet a day .
WARNINGS AND PRECAUTIONS : Do not exceed the recommended dosage .
If you are taking other medicines , consult your doctor .
Do not use loratadine with alcohol or other central nervous system depressants .
Children under 12 years old : do not use PEDIATRIC USE : Children under 12 years of age : do not use .
PREGNANCY AND BREASTFEEDING : Do not use during pregnancy or breastfeeding .
CONTRAINDICATIONS : Do not use in case of hypersensitivity to the ingredients .
ADVERSE REACTIONS : Fatigue , headache , dry mouth , nausea , gastritis , skin rash .
ACCIDENTAL INGESTION AND OVERDOSE : Reported symptoms in case of overdose : sleepiness , tachycardia , headache .
The treatment that should be started immediately is symptomatic and adjuvant .
Keep out of reach of children .
If symptoms persist for more than 5 days , consult your doctor .
Do not store above 86 ° F . Keep this container tightly closed .
