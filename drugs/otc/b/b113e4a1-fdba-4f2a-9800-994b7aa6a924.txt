Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses For hands to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not ingest Do not use in the eyes .
When using this product do not get in eyes .
In case of contact , immediately flush with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develop .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours consult a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Do not use if you are allergic to any ingredients .
Directions Apply a small amount to hands , add water and lather by rubbing hands together for 20 seconds , rinse with potable water .
