Drug Facts Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 percent Purpose Antibacterial Uses for hand washing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings for external use only .
When using this product : avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if : irritation and redness develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • pump into hands • lather vigorously for at least 15 seconds • rinse and dry thoroughly .
Inactive ingredients Water ( Aqua ) , Cocamidopropyl Betaine , Sodium Cocoamphoacetate , PEG - 150 Distearate , Fragrance , PEG - 40 Hydrogenated Castor Oil , Citric Acid , Methylisothiazolinone , Iodopropynyl Butylcarbamate , FD & C Yellow No . 5 , FD & C Blue No . 1 [ MULTIMEDIA ] [ MULTIMEDIA ]
