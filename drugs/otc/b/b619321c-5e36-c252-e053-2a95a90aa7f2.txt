PHMB Disinfectant Active Ingredient ( s ) Polyhexamethylene Biguanide 0 . 39 mg / mL .
Purpose : Antiseptic Purpose Antiseptic , PHMB Disinfectant Use PHMB Disinfectant to kill bacteria and virus that potentially can cause disease , and use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • Together with other disinfectants .
When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
