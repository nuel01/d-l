Drug Facts Active ingredient Ethanol ( alcohol ) 75 % Purpose Antiseptic Use hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin Warnings for external use only : hands Do not use in children less than 2 months of age .
When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness occurs .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Tear open , unfold and apply to hands .
• For children under 6 , use only under adult supervision .
Other information • Store between 15 - 30 ° C ( 59 - 86 ° F ) • Avoid freezing and excessive heat above 40 ° C ( 104 ° F ) Inactive ingredients Water , Didecyldimonium Chloride , Organic Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice , Tocopherol , Fragrance .
