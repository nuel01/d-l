CAUTION Do not use of cleaning skin , for personal cleansing or as a diaper wipe .
Causes moderate eyes irritation .
Avoid contact with eyes or clothing .
Wash thoroughly with soap and water after handling .
CAUTION Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .

CAUTION Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
FIRST AID : IF IN EYES : Hold eye open and rinse slowly and gently with water for 15 - 20 minutes .
Remove contact lenses , If present , after the first 5 minutes , then continue rinsing eye .
Call a poison control center for treatment advice .
Have the product container or label with you when calling a poision control center or doctor , or going for treatment .
PHYSICAL OR CHEMICAL HAZARDS : Do not use or store near heat or open flame .
