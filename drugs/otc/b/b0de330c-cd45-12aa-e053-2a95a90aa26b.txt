Active Ingredient ( s ) Benalkonium Chloride : 0 . 10 % Purpose Antiseptic Use For hand sanitizing to decrease bacteria on the skin Discard after single use Warnings For external use only .
When using this product , avoid contacting with eyes .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops and persists Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , contact a doctor or a poison control center right away .
