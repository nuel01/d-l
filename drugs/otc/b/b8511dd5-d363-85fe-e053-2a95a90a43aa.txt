Flammable .

When using this product • Do not use in eyes .
In case of contact with eyes , flush thoroughly with water .
• Avoid contact with broken skin .
Discontinue use if skin irritation and redness develop .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours consult a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
