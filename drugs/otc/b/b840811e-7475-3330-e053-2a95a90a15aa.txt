Active ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 70 % Purpose Antimicrobial Uses Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For external use only Do Not Use • In children less than 2 months of age • On open skin wounds When using this product When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use or ask a doctor if Stop use or ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age when using product to avoid swallowing .
