Active Ingredient Hamamelis Virginiana ( Witch Hazel ) Leaf Extract 2 . 5 % Purpose Skin Protectant Keep out of reach of children .
Indication and Usage Apply to the entire face and neck after face wash and toner .
Follow with moisturizer .
For both day and night .
Dosage and Administration Apply 2 ~ 4 drops at a time , smoothing in gently .
Warnings 1 .
In case of having following symptoms after using this product , stop using it immediately .
If the symptoms get worse , you need to consult a dermatologist .
1 ) In case of having problems such as red spots , swelling , itching or irritation during using this product .
2 ) In case of having the same symptoms above on the part where you put this product , which is exposed to direct sunlight .
2 .
Do not use it on the part where you have a scar , eczema or dermatitis .
3 .
For external use only .
