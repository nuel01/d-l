Directions • Spray liberally and spread evenly by hand 15 minutes before sun exposure • Hold container 4 to 6 inches from the skin to apply • Do no spray directly into face .
Spray on hands then apply to face • Do not apply in windy conditions • Use in well - ventilated areas • Reapply : • after 80 minutes of swimming or sweating • immediately after towel drying • at least every 2 hours • Sun Protection Measures .
