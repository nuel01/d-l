GENERAL WARNINGS AND PRECAUTIONS Warning : For emergency use only when administered by properly trained personnel for oxygen deficiency and resuscitation .
For all other medical applications , Rx Only .
Uninterrupted use of high concentrations of oxygen over a long duration , without measuring its effect on oxygen content of arterial blood , may be harmful .
Use only with pressure reducing equipment and apparatus designed for oxygen .
Do not attempt to use on patients who have stopped breathing , unless used in conjunction with resuscitative equipment .
