Active Ingredient Benzenethonium chloride USP 0 . 2 % Purpose Sanitizer Uses Handwash to help decrease bacteria on the skin .
Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings For external use only Do Not Use In the eyes .
When using this product Do not get in eyes .
If contact occurs , flush eyes with water and contact a doctor immediately .
If swallowed , seek medical attention .
Stop use and ask a doctor if Skin irritation develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
Children under 6 years old should be supervised by an adult when using this product .
