Active ingredient Benzalkonium chloride 0 . 13 % w / w Purpose Antiseptic Uses Suitable for wiping hands , face , skin and other unbroken skin .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product avoid to contact eyes , wounds and other sensitive parts .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
This product is insoluble in water , please put it in the trash after use .
