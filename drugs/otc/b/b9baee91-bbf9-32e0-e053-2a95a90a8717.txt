ACTIVE INGREDIENT : Sodium Fluoride 1 . 1 % ( 0 . 5 % Fluoride Ion ) INACTIVE INGREDIENTS : Purified Water , Glycerin , Sodium Phosphate Monobasic , Sodium Phosphate Dibasic , Sucralose , Xylitol , Sodium Benzoate , Titanium Dioxide , Sylodent 756 ( Silica ) , Casein , Microcrystalline Calcium Hydroxyapatite , Spearmint Oil , Peppermint Oil , Carboxymethyl Cellulose , Phosphoric Acid , Sodium Hydroxide DIRECTIONS : Adults and children six years of age and older should brush with Acclean Plus Home Care Gel once at bedtime ( or as directed by physician or dentist ) .
Apply a thin bead of gel to toothbrush and brush for at least one minute , making sure all tooth surfaces are covered .
ADULTS : Expectorate thoroughly .
Do not rinse , drink or eat for at least 30 minutes after use .
CHILDREN SIX YEARS OF AGE AND OLDER : Expectorate and rinse thoroughly .
If recommended by your dental professional , this product can be used in place of your regular toothpaste .
Read all instructions and prescribing information before using this product .
INDICATIONS : Helps prevent dental caries and hypersensitivity .
WARNING : DO NOT SWALLOW .
To avoid ingestion , supervision is required while children are using this product .
Swallowing excessive amounts of fluoride could cause dental fluorosis .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN AND INFANTS .
This product should not be used by individuals with milk allergies .
PRECAUTIONS : Federal ( USA ) Law prohibits dispensing without prescription .
Store at controlled room temperature 68 - 77 F ( 20 ° C - 25 ° C ) .
