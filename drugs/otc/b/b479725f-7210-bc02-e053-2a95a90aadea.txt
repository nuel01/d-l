Active ingredient 70 % v / v Ethyl Alcohol Purpose Antimicrobial Uses Hand sanitizer to help reduce microbes that potentially can cause disease • For use when soap and water are not available Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
Do not use • on children less than 2 months of age • on open skin wounds When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Place enough product on hand to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
Other information • Store between 15 - 30 ° C ( 59 - 86 ° F ) • Avoid freezing and excessive heat above 40 ° C ( 104 ° F ) Inactive ingredients Water , Isopropyl Alcohol , Carbomer , Natural Lavender , Aminomethyl Propanol .
