DRUG FACTS ACTIVE INGREDIENT Ethyl Alcohol 77 % v / v PURPOSE Antiseptic USE [ S ] Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
WARNINGS For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
Do not use • on children less than 2 months of age • on open skin wounds When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , mouth , or open orifices .
Rinse eyes thoroughly with water and contact a physician if needed .
Avoid ingestion .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
DIRECTIONS • Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
