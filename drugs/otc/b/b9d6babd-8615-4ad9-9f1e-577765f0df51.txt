Drug Facts Active ingredient [ s ] Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antimicrobial Use [ s ] Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin that could cause disease .
Warnings FOR EXTERNAL USE ONLY .
Do not use in the eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush thoroughly with water & seek medical attention .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops , or if condition persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Place enough product on your palm to thoroughly cover your hands .
Rub hands together until dry .
Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
Other information Skin cells may be shed naturally or by abrasion prior to 4 hours , exposing fresh , unprotected skin .
Reapply as needed .
Keep out of extreme temperatures .
