This is a hand sanitizer manufactured according to the Temporary Policy for Preparation of Certain Alcohol - Based Hand Sanitizer Products During the Public Health Emergency ( CoViD - 19 ) ; Guidance for Industry .
The hand sanitizer is manufactured using only the following United States Pharmacopoeia ( USP ) grade ingredients in the preparation of the product ( percentage in final product formulation ) consistent with World Health Organization ( WHO ) recommendations : • Alcohol ( ethanol ) ( USP or Food Chemical Codex ( FCC ) grade ) ( 70 , 5 % , weight / weight ( w / w ) ) in an aqueous solution denatured according to Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau regulations in 27 CFR part 20 .
• Glycerol ( 1 % w / w ) .
• Hydrogen peroxide ( 0 . 035 % w / w ) .
• Sterile distilled water or boiled cold water .
• Aloe barbadensis leaf juice 1 - 5 % • Hydroxyethyl acrylate / sodium acryloyldimethyl taurate copolymer 0 . 1 - 1 % • Squalane 0 . 1 - 1 % • Xanthan gum 0 . 1 - 1 % • Polysorbate 60 0 . 1 - 1 % • CITRUS LIMON PEEL OIL ( 0 , 07290 % ) • Sorbitan isostearate 0 - 0 . 1 % • Chlorhexidine digluconate 0 - 0 . 1 % • LACTIC ACID ( 0 , 01500 % ) Active Ingredient ( s ) Alcohol 70 , 5 % w / w .
Purpose : Antiseptic Purpose Antiseptic , Hand Sanitizer Use Hand Sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame Do not use • in children less than 2 months of age • on open skin wounds When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
