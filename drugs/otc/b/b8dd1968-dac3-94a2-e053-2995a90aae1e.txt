Drug Facts Benzalkonium chloride 0 . 1 % Antiseptic Uses For hand sanitizing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Recommended for repeated use .
For external use only When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops , or if condition persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek medical attention or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions Wet hands thoroughly with product .
Discard wipe in trash receptacle after use .
Do not flush .
Rub hands together briskly until dry .
Other information Store in a cool dry place below 104 F Water , C9 - 11 Pareth - 6 , Cocamidopropyl PG - dimonium chloride phosphate , Dihydroxyethyl cocamine oxide , Acetamidoethanol , Citric acid .
