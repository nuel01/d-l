Drug Facts Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 77 % ( v / v ) Purpose Antiseptic Use Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For external use only .
• Flammable , keep away from fire or flame .
• Contents under pressure .
Do not store at temperatures above 120 ° F ( 48 ° C ) , puncture or incinerate .
Do Not Use • On children less than 2 months of age • On open skin wounds When using this product keep out of eyes , ears and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
Other Information • Store between 59 - 86 ° F ( 15 - 30 ° C ) • Avoid freezing and excessive heat above 104 ° F ( 40 ° C ) Inactive Ingredients Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice , Ammonium Acryloyldimethyltaurate / VP Copolymer , Caprylic / Capric Triglyceride , Hydroxyethyl Urea , Tocopheryl Acetate , Water .
