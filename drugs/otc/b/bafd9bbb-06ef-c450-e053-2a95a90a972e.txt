Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride ( 0 . 13 % ) Purpose Antibacterial Warnings For external use only .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eyes contact , flush with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develops .
Keep out of reach of children if swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison control center right away .
Uses For hand washing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Directions • Pump into hands .
• Lather vigorously for at least 15 seconds .
• Rinse and dry thoroughly .
