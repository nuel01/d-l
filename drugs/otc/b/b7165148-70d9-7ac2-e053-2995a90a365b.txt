Drug Facts Benzalkonium chloride 0 . 1 % Antimicrobial Uses For hand sanitizing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Recommended for repeated use .
For external use only When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops , or if condition persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Pump a small amount of gel into pal of hand • Rub thoroughly over all surfaces of both hands • Rub hands together briskly until dry .
Water , carbomer , glycerin , lactic acid .
