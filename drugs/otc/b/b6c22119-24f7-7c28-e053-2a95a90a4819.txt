Drug Facts Active Ingredient Benzalkonium chloride 0 . 13 % w / v Purpose Antiseptic Uses Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • in children less than 2 months of age • on open skin wounds When using this product keep of eyes , ears and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor If irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions place enough product to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing Other Information • store between 88 - 77ºF ( 20 - 25ºC ) • may discolor certain fabrics and wood surfaces .
