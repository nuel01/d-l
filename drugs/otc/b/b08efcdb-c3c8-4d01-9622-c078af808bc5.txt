Drug Facts Witch Hazel 50 % Purpose : Hemorrhoidal Astringent Uses For temporary relief of local discomfort , burning and irritation associated with Hemorrhoids Directions When practical , clean the affected area with mild soap and warm water , and rinse thoroughly • Gently dry by patting or blotting with toilet tissue or a soft cloth before applying • Apply externally to the affected area up to 6 times daily or after each bowel movement • After application , discard pad Children under 12 years of age : consult a doctor Apply externally to the affected area up to 6 times daily or after each bowel movement • For External Use Only • Avoid contact with the eyes Consult a doctor • If condition worsens or does not improve within 7 days • In case of bleeding • Before exceeding the recommended dosage DO NOT put this product into the rectum using fingers or any mechanical device .
Other Ingredient Alcohol , Citric Acid , Deionized Water , DMDM Hydantoin , Glycerin , Sodium Citrate .
Store at room temperature 15 ° - 30 ° C ( 59 ° - 86 ° F ) If swallowed , contact the Poison Control Center immediately .
