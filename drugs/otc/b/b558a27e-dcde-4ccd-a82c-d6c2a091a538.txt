Benzoyl Peroxide 10 % Acne Treatment Treats and helps prevent acne pimples .
Foe external use only If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Cleanse skin thoroughly before applying medication .
Cover the entire affected area with a thin layer 1 to 3 times dally .
Because excessive drying of the skin may accur , start with 1 application daily , then gradually increase to 2 to 3 times daily If needed or as directed by a physician .
If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to once a day or every other other day .
Inactive Ingredients : Water , glycerin , propylene glycol , carbomer , diazolidinyl urea , methylparaben , propylparaben , aminomethyl propanol .
