Drug Facts Active Ingredients Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antimicrobial Uses • For hand sanitizing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
• Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use in eyes .
If contact occurs , flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours , consult a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Pump a small amount into palm of hand .
• Wet hands thoroughly with product and allow to dry without wiping .
• Rub hands together briskly until dry .
