Read and follow all directions and warnings on this carton .
• do not allow contact with the eyes • do not bandage or apply local heat ( such as heating pads ) to the area of use Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use .
