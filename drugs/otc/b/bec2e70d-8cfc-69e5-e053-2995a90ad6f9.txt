Active Ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 1 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses • For hand washing to help reduce bacteria on the skin • For use when soap is not available Warnings For external use only .
When using this product Avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops , or if condition persists for more than 72 hours Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Pump a small amount of foam into palm of hand • Rub thoroughly over all surfaces of both hands • Rub hands together briskly until dry .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
