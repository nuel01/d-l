Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antibacterial Use for hand washing to decrease bacteria on the skin Warnings For external use only When using this product avoid contact with the eyes .

Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develops Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
