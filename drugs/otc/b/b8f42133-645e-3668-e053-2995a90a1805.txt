Active ingredient Salicylic Acid 2 % Purpose Acne Treatment Use ■ For the treatment of acne Warnings For external use only Keep out of eyes ■ If contact with eyes occurs , immediately flush with water .
Using other topical acne drugs at the same time or right after use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one drug should be used unless directed by a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions ■ Use twice a day .
■ Wet face .
■ Apply to hands , add water and work into a lather .
■ Massage face gently , then rinse thoroughly .
