Patch - A drug delivery system that often contains an adhesive backing that is usually applied to an external site on the body .
Its ingredients either passively diffuse from , or are actively transported from , some portion of the patch .
Depending upon the patch , the ingredients are either delivered to the outer surface of the body or into the body .
A patch is sometimes synonymous with the terms ‘ extended release film ’ and ‘ system ’ .
