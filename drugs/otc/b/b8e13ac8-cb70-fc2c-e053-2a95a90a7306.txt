Drug Facts Active Ingredient Sodium Monofluorophosphate 0 . 76 % ( 0 . 122 % w / v of fluoride ion ) Purpose Anticavity Use Helps protect against cavities .
Warnings Keep out of reach of children under 6 years of age .
If you accidentally swallow more than used for brushing , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • Adults and children 2 years of age and older : Brush teeth thoroughly after meals or at least twice daily , or as directed by a dentist or physician .
• Children under 6 years of age : Use a pea - sized amount to minimize swallowing and supervise child ' s brushing until good habits are established .
• Children under 2 years of age : Ask a dentist or physician .
