Drug Facts Active ingredients Alcohol 70 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses • Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame Do not use • on children less than 2 months of age • on open skin wounds When using this product keep out of eyes , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
• Superivse children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
