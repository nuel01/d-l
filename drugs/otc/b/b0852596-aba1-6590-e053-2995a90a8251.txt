DRUG FACTS Active Ingredients Alcohol 80 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses • Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
• For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame Do not use • On children less than 2 months of age • On open skin wounds When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop using and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
Other information • Store between 15 - 30 ° C ( 59 - 86 ° F ) • Avoid freezing and excessive heat above 40 ° C ( 104 ° F ) Inactive Ingredients Water ( Aqua / Eau ) , Glycerin , Propylene Glycol , Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice ( Aloe Vera Gel from Aruba ) , Fragrance ( Parfum ) , Citric Acid .
