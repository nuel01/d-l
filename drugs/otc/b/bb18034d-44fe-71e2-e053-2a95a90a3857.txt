Active Ingredient ( s ) Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antiseptic , Hand Sanitizer Use Hand Sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame Do not use • in the eyes When using this product do not get into eyes .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develop .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours consult a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Wet hands thoroughly with product and allow to dry .
• Children under six years of age should be surprised when using this product .
Other information • Store in a cool , dry place .
