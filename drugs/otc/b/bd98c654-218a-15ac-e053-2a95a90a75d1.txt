Drug Facts Active Ingredient Cetylpyridinium chloride 0 . 07 % Purpose Antigingivitis Antiplaque Use helps control plaque that leads to gingivitis .
Warning Stop use and ask a dentist if • gingivitis , bleeding , or redness persists for more than 2 weeks • you have painful or swollen gums , pus from the gum line , loose teeth or increased spacing between the teeth .
These may be symptoms of periodontitis , a serious form of gum disease .
Keep out of reach of children under 6 years of age .
If more than used for rinsing is accidentally swallowed , get medical help .
