Drug Facts Active ingredient Benzoyl Peroxide 7 . 5 % Purpose Acne treatment Uses • For the treatment of acne .
• Reduces the number of acne blemishes .
• Helps prevents new blemishes from forming .
Warnings • For external use only .
• Using other topical acne medication at the same time or immediately following use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one medication should be used unless directed by a doctor .
• Do not use if you .
• have very sensitive skin .
• are sensitive to benzoyl peroxide .
• When using this product .
• avoide unnecessary sun exposure and use a sunscreen .
• avoid contact with dyed fabrics , which may be bleached by this product .
• skin irritation may occur , characterized by redness , burning , itching , peeling , or possibly swelling .
Irritation may be reduced by using the product less frequentl or in a lower concentration .
• Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation becomes severe .
• Keep out of reach of children .
• If swallowed , get medical help or contact poison control .
Directions • Cleanse the skin thoroughly before applying medication .
• Cover blemish and surrounding area with a thin layer of medication before bed .
• In the morning , wash and pat dry .
• Contnue using until blemish has disappeared • If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to every other day .
• If going outside , apply a broad - spectrum sunscreen after using this product .
• If irritation or sensitivity develops , stop use of product and ask a doctor .
