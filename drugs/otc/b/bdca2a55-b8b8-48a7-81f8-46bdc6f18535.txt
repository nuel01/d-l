If contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if rash occurs Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a poison control center right away .
Directions Adults and children 6 months and up .
Apply generously and evenly 15 minutes before sun exposure .
Reapply at least every 2 hours , after 80 minutes of swimming or sweating and immediately after towel drying .
