Drug Facts Active Ingredient ( s ) Benzoic Acid 0 . 1 % Purpose Antimicrobial Agent Use Remove oil , debris and desquamated skin from the eyelids .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use If you are allergic to any of the ingredients .
When using this product , avoid contact with eyes and mouth .
If product gets in the eyes , flush thoroughly with water .
Discontinue use and contact a doctor if irritation and redness develop and condition persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children unless under adult supervision .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Tear open packet and remove wipe .
Gently wipe away dirt and debris from eyelids .
Throw used wipe in trash .
Do not flush .
