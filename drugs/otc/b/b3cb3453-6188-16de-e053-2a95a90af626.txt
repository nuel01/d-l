Active Ingredient ( s ) Alcohol 70 % v / v Purpose Antiseptic Use ( s ) • Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap water are not available .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
Do Not Use * On children less then 2 months of age * On open skin wounds When using this product : Keep out of eyes , ears , a nd mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor : If irritation or rash appears and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children : If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions * Put enough product in your palm to cover hands and rub hands together until dry .
* Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
