Drug Facts Active ingredient ( diluted solution ) 0 . 05 % Sodium fluoride ( 0 . 02 % w / v fluoride ion ) Purpose Anticavity Use Aids in prevention of dental cavities Warnings Keep out of reach of children .
If more than used for rinsing is accidentally swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Do not use before mixing with water • Measure 2 . 5 ml .
Dilute with water to 4 x volume ( 10 ml ) .
Use immediately • Adults and children 6 years of age and older : • Use once a day after brushing your teeth with a toothpaste • Vigorously swish 10 ml of rinse between your teeth for 1 minute and then spit out • Do not swallow the rinse • Do not eat or drink for 30 minutes after rinsing • Supervise children as necessary until capable of using without supervision • Children under 6 years of age : consult a dentist or doctor Other information • Do not store the diluted product • Store concentrate at room temperature • Do not use if seal under cap is broken or missing .
