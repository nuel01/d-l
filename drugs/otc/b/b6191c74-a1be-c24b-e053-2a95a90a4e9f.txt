Disinfecting Wet Wipe Active Ingredient ( s ) Polyhexamethylene Biguanide 0 . 9 mg / cloth .
Purpose : Antiseptic Purpose Antiseptic , Disinfecting Wet Wipe Use Disinfecting Wet Wipe to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • in children less than 2 months of age When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
