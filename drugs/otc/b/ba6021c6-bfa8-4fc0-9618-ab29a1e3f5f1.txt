Active ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 70 % Purpose Antimicrobial Use Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin that could cause disease Warnings Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only .
When using this product , do not use in or near the eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash appears and lasts Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Place enough product in your palm to thoroughly cover your hands .
Rub hands together briskly until dry .
Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
