Drug Facts Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 62 % Purpose Antiseptic Use To help reduce bacteria and germs on the skin .
WARNING Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only • Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops and persists .
• Keep out of reach of children .
• In case of accidental ingestion , seek professional assistance or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • Place enough product in palm to cover hands and rub hands together briskly until dry .
• Children under 6 , use only under adult supervision .
• Not recommended for infants .
Other Information • Do not store above 100 ° F ( 38 ° C ) .
• May discolor some fabrics .
• Harmful to wood finishes and plastics .
Inactive Ingredients • Deionized Water , Glycerine , Carbomer , Vitamin E , Fragrance , Aloe Barbadensis Gel , Triethanolamine , Propylene Glycol .
