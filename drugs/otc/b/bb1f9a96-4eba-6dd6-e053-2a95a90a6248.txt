ACTIVE INGREDIENT Benzalkonium chloride 0 . 08 % PURPOSE Antiseptic USES • For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
• After changing diapers .
• After assisting ill persons .
• Before contact with a person under medical care or treatment .
• Recommended for repeat use .
WARNING For external use only .
Do not use in the eyes .
WHEN USING THIS PRODUCT When using this product do not get into eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water .
STOP USE AND ASK A DOCTOR IF Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develop .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours consult a doctor .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN Keep out of reach from children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
DIRECTIONS • Wet hands thoroughly with product and allow to dry .
• Children under six years of age should be supervised when using this product .
OTHER INFORMATION Store in a cool , dry place .
