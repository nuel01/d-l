Drug Facts Active ingredients Salicylic Acid 0 . 5 % Purpose Acne Medication Use For the treatment of acne Warnings For external use only .
When using this product • apply to affected areas only • using other topical acne medication at the same time may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If irritation occurs , use only one medication at a time .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • clean the skin thoroughly before applying • cover the entire affected area with a thin layer 1 to 3 times daily .
Avoid eye area .

Do not smoke when applying this product .
