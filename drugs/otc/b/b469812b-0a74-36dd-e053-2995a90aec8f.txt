Menthol 2 . 76 % Purpose Antitussive ( Cough Suppressant ) Uses Temporarily suppresses cough due to minor throat and bronchial irritation associated with a cold or inhaled irritants .
Controls the impulse to cough to help you get to sleep .
For external use only .
Do not take by mouth or place in nostrils Flammable : Keep away from fire or flame .
• if epileptic • with high blood pressure Ask a doctor before use if you have • persistent or chronic cough such as occurs with smoking , asthma , or emphysema • cough accompanied by excessive phlegm ( mucus ) When using this product , do not • heat • microwave • use near an open flame • add to hot water or any container where heating water .

Stop use and ask a doctor if cough persists for more than 1 week , tends to recur , or is accompanied by fever , rash , or persistent headache .
A persistent cough may be a sign of a serious condition .
If pregnant or breast - feeding , do not use this product .
Keep out of reach of children .
If product is swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
