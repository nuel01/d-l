uses helps loosen phlegm ( mucus ) and thin bronchial secretions to make coughs more productive .
warning Ask a doctor before use if you have • cough that occurs with too much phlegm ( mucus ) • cough that lasts or is chronic such as occurs with smoking , asthma , chronic bronchitis , or emphysema Stop use and ask a doctor if cough lasts for more than 7 days , comes back , or occurs with fever , rash , or persistent headache .
A persistent cough may be a sign of a serious condition .
