Active Ingredient Stannous fluoride , 0 . 63 % Purpose Anticavity / antigingivitis Uses • Aids in the prevention of dental decay • Helps prevent gingivitis • Helps interfere with harmful effects of plaque associated with gingivitis Warnings Keep out of reach of children under 6 years of age .
If more than used for rinsing is swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control center right away .
Do not swallow .
