Active Ingredients : Pyrithione Zinc 1 % Purpose : Antidandruff Use : controls flaking , itching and irritation associated with dandruff and seborrheic dermatitis Warnings : • For external use only .
When using this product Avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Consult your physician before using this product if condition covers a large area of the body .
Stop use and consult a physician if condition worsens or does not improve after regular use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • For best results use at least twice a week or as directed by a physician .
• Wet hair , lather and massage gently into the hair and scalp .
• Rinse thoroughly and repeat if necessary .
