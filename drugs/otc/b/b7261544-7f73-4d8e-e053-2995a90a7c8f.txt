Active Ingredient ( s ) Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antibacterial Use For hand washing to decrease bacteria on the skin Warnings For external use only When using this product do not get into eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develop , condition persists for more than 72 hours Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
