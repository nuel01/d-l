Drug Facts Active Ingredient Sulfur 3 % Purpose Acne treatment Uses For the management of acne .
Helps clear up acne blemishes .
Warnings For external use only • When using this product skin irritation and dryness is more likely to occur if used with another topical acne medication at the same time .
If irritation occurs , only use one topical acne medication at a time .
• Avoid contact with eyes .
If excessive skin irritation develops or increases , discontinue use and consult a doctor .
• Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Do not use on • Broken skin • Large areas of the skin When using this product • Apply only to areas with acne Directions Cleanse skin thoroughly and tone before applying .
Cover the affected area with a thin layer one to three times daily .
Because excessive drying of the skin may occur , start with one application daily , then gradually increase to two to three times daily if needed or as directed by a doctor .
If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to once a day or every other day .
