Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 1 % Purpose Antimicrobial Uses • • For hand sanitizing to decrease bacteria on the skin • • Recommended for repeated use • • Significantly reduces bacteria on hands between regular hand washing but not intended to replace regular hand washing .
Warnings For External use only .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes and nose .
In case of contact flush eyes with water .
Stop use and consult a doctor if irritation or redness develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Pump small amount of foam into palm of hand .
Rub thoroughly over all surfaces of both hands .
Rub hands together briskly until dry .
