Active Ingredient Ethyl alcohol 80 % v / v Purpose Antimicrobial Use Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin that could cause disease .
Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only - do not use on damaged or broken skin When using this product do not use in or near the eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughyl with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
