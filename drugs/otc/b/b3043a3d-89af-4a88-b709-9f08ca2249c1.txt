Active ingredient Purpose Menthol - 10 % , Methyl Salicylate - 15 % ............................................................ External Analgesic Uses • For the temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints Warnings For external use only .
Use only as directed .
Keep out of reach of children to avoid accidental poisoning .
Discontinue use if excessive irritation of the skin develops .
Do not bandage tightly , apply to wounds or damaged skin or use with a heating pad .
If condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear - up and occur again within a few days , discontinue use of this product and consult a doctor .
Avoid contact with eyes or mucous membranes .
Directions • Adults and children 2 years of age and older : Apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
• Children under 2 years of age : Consult a doctor .
