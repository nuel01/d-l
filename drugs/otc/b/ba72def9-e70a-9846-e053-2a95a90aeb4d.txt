Active Ingredient ( s ) Didecyl Dimethyl Ammonium Chloride 0 . 5 % Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 1 % Purpose Antibacterial Antibacterial Uses For hand sanitizing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Do not use • In the eyes • If you are allergic to any of the ingredients .
When using this product if eye contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develop and persist for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Storage .
Store at room temperature .
• Dispensing .
Peel resealable label back slowly .
Reseal after use to avoid wipes drying out .
• Use .
Apply wipe thoroughly to hands as desired .
Allow to dry without wiping .
To ensure best quality , use within 30 days of opening .
• Disposal .
Dispose of used wipes in trash receptacle .
Do not flush .
