Active ingredients Ethyl Alcohol 62 % Inactive Ingredients : Water , Carbomer , Triethanolamine , Glycerol and Aloe Vera Extract .
Uses ： Hand sanitizer to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Apply small amount and rub hands together until dry .
Warnings : Do not use in the eyes .
In case of contact , rise eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develop and last for more than 72 hours .
Get medical help immediately if swallowed .
Flammable ， keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only .
Keep out of reach of children .
