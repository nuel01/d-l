ACTIVE INGREDIENT ETHYL alcohol 65 % v / v .
Purpose : Antiseptic PURPOSE Antiseptic , Hand Sanitizer USES • Hand Sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
• For use when soap and water are not available .
WARNINGS For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
DO NOT USE near or in the eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes with water throughly .
STOP USE Stop use and ask a physician if irritation , rash , or redness develops .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN If swallowed get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
DIRECTIONS • Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
