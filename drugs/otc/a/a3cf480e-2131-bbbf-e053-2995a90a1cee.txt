Active Ingredient Alcohol 75 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses To help reduce bacteria on the skin .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For External Use only Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame Do not Use - On children less than 2 months of age - On open skin wounds - around eyes - in ears and mouth When using this product Keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
- avoid contact with broken skin .
- do not inhale or ingest Stop use and ask a doctor if Irritation or rash occurs Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away Directions - Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
- Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
