Drug Facts Active ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 62 % Purpose Antimicrobial Uses • help reduce bacteria on the skin that could cause disease • recommended for repeated use Warnings For exeternal use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
When using this product : do not use in or near the eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if : irritation and redness develops and lasts more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Place enough product in your palm to thoroughly cover your hands • Rub hands together briskly until dry • Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
