Active ingredient 75 % ethanol purpose antibacterial Uses Hand sanitizer helps reduce bacteria on the skin .
Warnings Flammable , away from fire or open flames , for external use only .
When using this product do not use it in or near your eyes . In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water . Discontinue use and ask your doctor if there is and continued irritation or rash . Keep out of reach of children . If swallowed , consult a doctor immediately or contact a poison control center .
Directions Put enough product in your palm to spread your hands thoroughly and rub into the skin until dry .
Children under 6 should be supervised when using this product .
