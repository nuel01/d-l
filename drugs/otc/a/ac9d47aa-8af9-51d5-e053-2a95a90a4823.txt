Active Ingredient White Petroleum ( 99 . 9 % ) Purpose Skin Protectant Uses : • Temporarily protects minor cuts , scrapes or burns .
• Temporarily protects and helps relieve chapped or cracked skin and lips .
• Helps protect from the drying effects of wind and cold weather .
Warning : For External Use Only .
Do not use on : • deep or puncture wounds • lacerations or infections • serious burns • animal bites Stop use and ask doctor • if condition does not improve or lasts more than 7 days .
Keep out of reach of children • if swallowed get medical help or contact Poison Control Center right away .
• if redness or swelling developes , consult a physician promptly .
Directions : Apply as often as necessary .
Inactive Ingredients : None .
