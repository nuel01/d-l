ACTIVE INGREDIENT Ethyl Alcohol ( 75 % ) PURPOSE Antimicrobial USES Helps decrease bacteria on skin . Recommend for repeated use .
WARNINGS For external use only . Keep away from fire or flame . Do not apply around eyes . When using this product , avoid contact with eyes . In case of contact , immediately flush eyes with water . Stop use and ask a doctor if imitaton or redness develops or condition persists for more than 72 hours . Keep out of reach of Children . Children must be supervised in use of this product .
DIRECTIONS Apply as needed into your palms and thoroughly spread on both hands .
OTHER INFORMATION Store at 20 ° C ( 68 ° to77 ° F ) cool place . Protect from light .
