Active Ingredient Ethanol 90 . 24 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses To help reduce bacteria on skin that may cause disease .
Warnings For external use only .
Avoid contact with face , eyes , and broken skin .
In case of eye contact , flush with plenty of water and seek medical attention .
DANGER : Flammable .
Contents Under Pressure .
Do not use near heat , sparks or flames .
Vapors may ignite explosively and cause flash fire .
Prevent build up of vapors .
Do not smoke turn of pilot lights , stove , heaters and other sources of ignition during use and until all vapors are gone .
Do not puncture of incinerate container .
Do not expose to heat or store at temperatures above 120 F .
Use with adequate ventilation .
Stop use and ask a doctor if : irritation and redness develops .
Keep out of reach of children and pets .
Children under 6 years of age should be supervised by an adult when using .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact your Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Spray hands thoroughly with product and rub into hands until dry .
Store at room temperature or in a cool place .
Inactive Ingredients Water , Panthenol ( Vitamin B5 ) , Glycerol , Tocopheryl Acetate ( Vitamin E ) , Aloe Barbadensis ( Aloe Vera ) Leaf Juice .
