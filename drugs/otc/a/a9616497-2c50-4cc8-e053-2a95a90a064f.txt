Drug Facts Active ingredient Ethyl alcohol 65 % Purpose Antiseptic Use For hand washing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable , keep away from fire or flame .
Do not use in the eyes .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • irritation and redness develops • condition persists for more than 72 hours Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Wet hands thoroughly with product and allow to dry without wiping .
