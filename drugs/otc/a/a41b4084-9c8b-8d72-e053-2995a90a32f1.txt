Active Ingredient ( s ) Ethyl alcohol 80 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on hands .
Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable - Keep away from fire or flame When using this product • Keep out of eyes , ear and mouth .
• In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develop or increase .
Keep out of reach of children if swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Apply a small amount onto hands and rub briskly until dry .
• Supervise children in the use of this product .
