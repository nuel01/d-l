Topical Analgesic Uses For temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with • arthritis • simple backache • bursitis • tendonitis • muscle strains • muscle sprains • bruises • cramps Warnings For external use only When using this product • use only as directed • do not bandage tightly or use with a heating pad • avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes • do not apply to wounds or damaged skin , broken or irritated skin Stop use and ask a doctor • condition worsens • redness is present • irritation develops • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a healthcare professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
