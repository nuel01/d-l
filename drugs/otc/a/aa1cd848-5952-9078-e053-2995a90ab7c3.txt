Active Ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 11 % Purpose Antimicrobial Uses • To decrease bacteria on skin when soap and water is not available .
• Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings • For external use only .
• When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
In case of contact with eyes , flush thoroughly with water .
• Stop use and ask a doctor if skin irritation or redness develops and lasts .
• Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Place enough product in your palm to thoroughly cover your hands .

Other information • Read the directions and warnings before use .
• Store in a cool , dry and well - ventilated place .
