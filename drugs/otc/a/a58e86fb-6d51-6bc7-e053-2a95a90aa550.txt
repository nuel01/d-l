Active Ingedients Alcohol 80 % v / v Purpose Antiseptic USE Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potencially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
WARNINGS For external use only .
Keep away from heat or flame .
DO NOT USE : * In children less than 2 months .
* On open skin wounds .
When using this product keep out of eyes , ears and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
DIRECTIONS OTHER INFORMATION Store between 15º - 30ºC ( 59 - 86 F ) .
Avoid freezing and excessive heat above 40ºC ( 104 F ) .
