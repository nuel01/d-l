Redness reliever Uses • relieves redness of the eye due to minor eye irritations • temporarily relieves burning and irritation due to dryness of the eye • protects against further irritation or dryness of the eye Warnings For external use only .
Do not use if solution changes color or becomes cloudy .
Ask a doctor before use if you have narrow angle glaucoma .
When using this product • remove contact lenses before using • to avoid contamination , do not touch tip to any surface • replace cap after using • overuse may cause increased redness of the eye • pupils may become enlarged temporarily Stop use and ask a doctor if • you feel eye pain • changes in vision occur • you have continued redness or irritation of the eye • condition worsens or persists for more than 72 hours Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) right away .
