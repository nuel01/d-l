Warnings For external use only .
Do not use • on children less than 2 months of age • on open skin wounds • on irritated skin When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • irritation or rash occurs .
• side effects occur .
You may report side effects to the FDA at 1 - 800 - FDA - 1088 Keep out of reach of children .
