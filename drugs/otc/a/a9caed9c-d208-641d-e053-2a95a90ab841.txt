Drug Facts Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride . 13 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses • For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush eyes with water .
Do not ingest .
Stop use and ask a doctor If irritation or redness develops and conditions persist .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact the Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Apply soap into hands , wet as needed • Lather vigorously for at least 15 seconds · Wash skin , rinse thoroughly and dry .
Inactive Ingredients Water , Cocamidopropyl Betaine , Glycerine , Caprylyl Glucoside , Tetrasodium EDTA , Benzisothiazol , Methylisothiazol , Fragrance , Citric Acid , Aloe Vera , Yellow # 5 , Red # 40 .
