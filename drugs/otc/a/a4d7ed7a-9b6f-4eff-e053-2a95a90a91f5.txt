Drug Facts Active ingredients Ethyl Alcohol ( 70 % v / v ) Purpose Antiseptic Uses • to help reduce bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
When using this product do not use on damaged or broken skin .
Do not leave near fire .
Wash with enough water , if it gets into eyes .
Do not drink .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • apply enough gel to wet hands and rub together until dry .
Use 1 to 3 times daily .
