Drug Facts Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 70 % v / v Purpose Antiseptic Use • Antibacterial hand sanitizing gel to help killing bacteria and germs as antiseptic on the skin .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only When using this product keep out of eyes and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , immediately rinse eyes thoroughly with water Stop use and ask doctor If irritation , rash or allergy occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed get medical help or contact a poison control center right away Directions • Apply .
Put enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together , until hands feel dry .
This should take around 20 seconds .
recommended for repeated use .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
