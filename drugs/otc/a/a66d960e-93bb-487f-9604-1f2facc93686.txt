Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses • helps eliminate bacteria on hands .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product • avoid contact with eyes • in case of contact , rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • use to refill a foaming pump bottle .
• from the pump bottle , pump onto dry hands .
• work into a rich foamy lather , rinse thoroughly and dry .
