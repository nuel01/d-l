Active Ingredient Alcohol 75 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses For hand washing to decrease bacteria on skin .
Warnings Flammable : Keep away from heat or flame .
For External Use Only Do not use in eyes .
In case of contact rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a poison control center immediately .
Directions Wet hands thoroughly with product and allow to dry without wiping .
Discard after single use .
Other Information Do not flush down toilet .
Keep away from sunlight .
