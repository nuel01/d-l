Active Ingredient Chloroxylenol 0 . 3 % .
Purpose Antibacterial USE For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warning For external use only : hands only When using this product avoid contact with the eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops - condition persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Directions Apply onto wet hands , lather for at least 30 seconds and rinse thoroughly Inactive ingredients WATER , SODIUM LAURETH SULFATE , COCAMIDOPROPYL BETAINE , GLYCERIN , COCAMIDE METHYL MEA , SODIUM CHLORIDE , PARFUM , CITRIC ACID , DISODIUM EDTA , METHYLCHLOROISOTHIAZOLINONE AND METHYLISOTHIAZOLINONE .
