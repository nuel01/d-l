DRUG FACTS Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 80 % Purpose Antiseptic Use [ s ] Health care personnel hand rub to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame Do not use • on open skin wounds • in children less than 2 months of age When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use & ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
Other Information • Store between 15 - 30 C ( 59 - 86 F ) • Avoid freezing and excessive heat above 40 C ( 104 F ) Inactive Ingredients Distilled water , glycerol , hydrogen peroxide , denatonium benzoate .
