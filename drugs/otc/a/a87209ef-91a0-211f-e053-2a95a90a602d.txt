CastivaCooling Drug Facts Active Ingredient Menthol 5 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Active Ingredient Methyl Salicylate 14 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Use For the temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with simple backache , arthritis , strains , bruises , sprains .
Warnings For external use only .
Allergy alert .
If prone to allergic reaction from aspirin or salicylate , consult a doctor before use .
Do not use otherwise than as directed , on damaged skin , on open wounds , with a heating pad .
Stop and ask a doctor if condition worsens , symptoms persist for more than 7 days , or clear up and occur again within a few days .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
Do not bandage tightly .
If pregnant or breast feeding , ask a health professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children to prevent accidental poisoning .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek professional assistance , or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • Adults and children 12 yrs .
of age and older .
Apply to the affected area no more than 3 to 4 times daily .
• Children under 12 years of age : Consult a doctor .
Other Information • Store at room temperature .
Inactive Ingredients Castor oil , diazolidinyl urea , glyceryl monostearate , isopropyl palmitate , lecithin , methylparaben , PEG - 100 stearate , poloxamer 407 , polyglyceryl 1 , 3 - disteareate , potato starch modified , propylene glycol , propylparaben , purified water , SD Alcohol 40 - B .
