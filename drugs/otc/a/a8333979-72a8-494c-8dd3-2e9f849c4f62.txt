Active ingredient Benzalkonium chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses for washing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop using this product and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • use only to refill a Liquid Hand Soap pump bottle .
• Pump onto dry hands , work into a rich foamy lather , rinse and dry thoroughly .
