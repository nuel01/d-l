Drug Facts Active Ingredients Capsaicin 0 . 034 % Methyl Salicylate 10 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Uses : For the temporary relief from minor aches & pains of sore muscles & joints associated with arthritis , backache , strains & sprains .
Warnings : For external use only .
Use only as directed .
Keep out of reach of children .
If accidentally ingested , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
This product is intended to & may cause a mild temporary skin irritation .
Discontinue use if excessive skin irritation develops .
When Using This Product : • Avoid contact with eyes or mucous membranes • Do not apply to wounds , broken skin or irritated skin • Do not use with other ointments , creams , sprays , liniments or other NSAIDs • Do not apply to irritated skin or if excessive irritation develops • Do not bandage • Wash hands with soap & cool water after using • Do not expose treated area to direct sunlight , heat or warm / hot water Stop Use and Ask a Doctor If : Condition worsens or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days , or clear up and reoccur Pregnant or breast - feeding : ask a health professional before use Directions : Adults / Children 12 years of age and older : Massage into affected area no more than 4 times daily .
• Children under 12 years of age : Consult a physician .
• If first time user , test in a small area .
Other Information : Store in a cool dry place with lid closed .
