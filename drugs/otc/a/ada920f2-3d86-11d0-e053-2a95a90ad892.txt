Active Ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antimicrobial Agent USE Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warning For external use only .
Do not use : In children less than 2 months of age .
On open skin wounds .
When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth , In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if rritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Apply to hands , allow to dry without wiping .
