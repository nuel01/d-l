Drug Facts Active ingredient Dibucaine 1 % Purpose Hemmorhoidal ointment Use • temporarily relieves pain and itching due to hemorrhoids and other anorectal disorders Warnings For external use only .
Allergy Alert • Certain persons can develop allergic reactions to ingredients in this product .
If the symptoms being treated does not subside or if redness , irritaion , swelling , bleeding or other symptoms develop or increase , discontinue use and ask a doctor .
Do not use • in or near the eyes • in children under 2 years of age When using this product • do not use more than directed unless directed by a doctor .
• do not put this product into the rectum by using fingers or any mechanical device or applicator Stop use and ask a doctor if • bleeding occurs • condition worsens or does not improve If pregnant or breast - feeding ask a health care professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a poison control center right away .
Directions • adults and children 12 years and over : If possible , clean the affected area with mild soap and warm water and rinse thoroughly .
Dry by patting or with toilet tissue or a soft cloth before applying .
