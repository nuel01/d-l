Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
Do not use on children less than 6 months of age , and on open skin wounds .
When using this product Keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Avoid contact with broken skin .
Do not inhale or ingest .
Stop use and ask doctor if irritation or rash occurs , persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Apply to skin as needed .
Discard after single use .
Always snap lid securely shut between uses to prevent moisture loss .
Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
Other information Protect from freezing and avoid excessive heat .
Store between 15 - 30 C ( 59 - 86 F ) .
Avoid freezing and excessive heat above 40 C ( 104 F ) .
