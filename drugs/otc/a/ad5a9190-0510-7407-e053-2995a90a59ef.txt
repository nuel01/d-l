Active Ingredient Ethyl alcohol 75 % Purpose It has killing effect on Escherichia coli , Staphylococcus aureus , Candida albicans and natural bacteria ( common bacteria in hospital infection ) .
Uses 1 .
Human skin disinfection : Dip a cotton swab or cotton ball ( or spray ) with an appropriate amount of disinfectant , and wipe the area to be treated 1 - 2 times for 1 . 5 minutes 2 .
Disinfect the surface of the object : use a cotton swab or cotton ball ( or spray ) to wipe the surface of the object with an appropriate amount of disinfectant for 1 . 5 minutes .
Warnings 1 .
This product is a disinfectant for external use and should not be taken orally .
2 .
Please place it out of reach of children .
3 .
This product is flammable and away from fire sources .
4 .
Use with caution in alcohol allergies .
