This spot solution cream is formulated to target current blemishes .
Directions : Apply directly to affected areas as needed .
Apply product sparingly to one or two small , affected areas during the first 3 days .
If no discomfort occurs , follow the directions stated above .
Warnings : Not to be used for children under 3 years of age .
Avoid direct contact with eyes .
If product gets into eyes , rinse thoroughly with water .
