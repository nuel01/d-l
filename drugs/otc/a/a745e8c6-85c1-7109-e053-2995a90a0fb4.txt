Severe liver damage may occur if you take • more than 6 packets in 24 hours , which is the maximum daily amount for this product • with other drugs containing acetaminophen • 3 or more alcoholic drinks every day while using this product Allergy alert : acetaminophen may cause severe skin reactions .
Symptoms may include : • skin reddening • blisters • rash If a skin reaction occurs , stop use and seek medical help right away .
Sore throat warning : if sore throat is severe , persists for more than 2 days , is accompanied or followed by fever , headache , rash , nausea , or vomiting , consult a doctor promptly .
