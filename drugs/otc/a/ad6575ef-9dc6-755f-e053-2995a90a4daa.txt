Drug Facts Active ingredient Isopropyl alcohol 70 % v / v Purpose Antiseptic Uses Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
Do not use • in children less than 2 months of age .
• on open skin wounds .
When using this product keep out of eyes , ears and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
Other information • Store between 15 - 30 C ( 59 - 86 F ) • Avoid freezing and excessive heat above 40 C ( 104 F ) .
Inactive ingredients Water / Aqua , Glycerin , Polysorbate 20 , Carbomer , Tocopheryl Acetate , Aminomethyl Propanol , Green 5 .
