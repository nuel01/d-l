Alcohol ( Ethyl Alcohol or Ethanol ) Ethyl Alcohol Hand Sanitizer OTC Antiseptic Drug Products Inactive Ingredients water , glycerin , hydrogen peroxide , fragrance Uses Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that can potentially cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact Poison Control Center right away .
Directions - Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
- Supervise children under six years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
- Do not use in children less than 2 months of age .
Do not use on open skin wounds .
- When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
- Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occur .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
