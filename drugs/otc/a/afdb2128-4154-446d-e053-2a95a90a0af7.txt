Active ingredient Cetylpyridinium chloride 0 . 05 % Purpose Antigingivitis , Antiplaque Use helps control plaque that leads to gingivitis Warnings Ask a dentist before use if - gingivitis , bleeding , or redness persists for more than 2 weeks - you have painful or swollen gums , pus from the gum line , loose teeth or increased spacing between the teeth .
These may be signs of symptoms of periodontitis , a serious form of gum disease .
Stop use and ask a dentist if • gingivitis , bleeding , or redness persists for more than 2 weeks • you have painful or swollen gums , pus from the gum line , loose teeth or increased spacing between the teeth .
These may be signs of symptoms of periodontitis , a serious form of gum disease .
Keep out of reach of children under 6 years of age If more than used for rinsing is accidentally swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
