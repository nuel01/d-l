Drug Facts Active Ingredient Undecylenic Acid 25 . 00 % Purpose Antifungal Uses : • For effective relief of itching , burning and cracking .
Warnings : • For external use only .
Do not use on children under 2 years of age except under the supervision of a doctor .
When using this product avoid contact with the eyes .
Stop use and ask a doctor if : - Irritation occurs .
- There is no improvement within 4 weeks .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
If pregnant or breast feeding , ask a health professional before use .

• Supervise , children in the use of this product .
