Drug Facts Active Ingredient Benzethonium Chloride @ 0 . 13 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses • For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use in the eyes .
Discontinue use if irritation and redness develops .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours consult a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Apply to hands and wet as needed .
• Lather vigorously for at least 15 seconds .
• Wash skin , rinse thoroughly and dry .
Other information - Protect from freezing .
Avoid excessive heat .
