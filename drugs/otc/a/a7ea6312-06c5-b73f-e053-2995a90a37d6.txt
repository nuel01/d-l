Purpose : Antiseptic Purpose Antiseptic , Hand Sanitizer Use Hand Sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
For external use only .
Do not use • in children less than 2 months of age • on open skin wounds When using this product do not use in or near the eyes .
Stop use and ask medical doctor if irritation or rash appears and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Place enough product in your palm to thoroughly spread on both hands and rub into the skin until dry .
• Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
Other information • Store below 106 F ( 41 F ) .
• May discolor certain fabrics or surfaces .
