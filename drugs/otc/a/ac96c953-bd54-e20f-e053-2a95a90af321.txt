Warning Flammable , Keep away from heat or flame . For external use only . Do not use in children less than two months of age or on open skin wounds .
Discontinue use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs . These may be signs of serious condition . Keep out of reach of children . Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing . Keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth . In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
