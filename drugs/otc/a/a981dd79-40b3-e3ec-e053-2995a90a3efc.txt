Active ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 70 % Purpose Antimicrobial Use For handwashing to decrease bacteria on skin without soap and water .
Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable , Keep away from fire or flame .
Do not use in eyes .
If this happens , rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and rash appears and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .

Children under 6 years of age should be supervised while using this product .
