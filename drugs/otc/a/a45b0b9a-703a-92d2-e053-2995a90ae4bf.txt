Drug Facts Active ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 75 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses • For hand sanitizing to decrease bacteria on the skin • Recommended for repeated use Warnings For external use only When using this product : avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if : irritation or redness develops , or if condition persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children : If swallowed get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions : Squeeze appropriate amount sanitizer on to hands and rub together until dry .
Use as needed .
For children under 6 , use with adult supervision .
Not recommended for infants .
Inactive ingredients : Water ( Aqua ) , Aloe Barbadensis leaf Extract .
