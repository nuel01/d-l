Drug Facts Active ingredient Benzocaine 6 % w / v Isopropyl alcohol 60 % w / v Purpose Antiseptic Uses First aid to help prevent infection nearly minor cuts , scrapes and burns .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use in the eyses . If contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop use if irritation , redness or other symptoms develop .
Consult a doctor if the condition persists or get worse .
CAUTION : Flammable .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Apply to affected area 3 - 4 times daily for adults and children 2 years of age and older .
Children under 2 years : consult physician .
Inactive ingredients Purified water .
