Drug Facts Active Ingredient [ s ] Alcohol 75 % v / v Purpose Antiseptic Use [ s ] Use sanitizer on hands or surfaces as a disinfecting agent to kill viruses and bacteria .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
Do not use • On children less than 2 months of age • On open skin wounds When using this product , keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a poison control center right away .
Directions • Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
• Generously rub into palms and all over hands until dry .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product to avoid swallowing .
Other information • Store between 15 - 30 C ( 59 - 86 F ) • Avoid freezing and excessive heat above 40 C ( 104 F ) Inactive ingredients Demineralized water , Carbomer 940 , Glyerin , Aloe Vera , Triethanolamine , Ethyl alcohol Package Labeling : [ MULTIMEDIA ] [ MULTIMEDIA ]
