ACTIVE INGREDIENT [ S ] Alcohol 70 % v / v PURPOSE Antiseptic USE [ S ] Hand Sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
WARNINGS For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame .
DO NOT USE • On children less than 2 months of age .
On open skin wounds .
WHEN USING Keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
STOP USE If irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN If swallowed , get medical help , or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
DIRECTIONS • Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age .
When using this product avoid swallowing .
OTHER INFORMATION • Store between 15 - 30 ° C ( 59 - 86 ° F ) Avoid freezing and excessive heat above 40 ° C ( 104 ° F ) INACTIVE INGREDIENTS Water ( Aqua ) , Propylene Glycol , Glycerin , Methyl Hydroxypropyl Cellulose , Melaleuca Alternifolia ( Tea Tree ) Leaf Oil .
