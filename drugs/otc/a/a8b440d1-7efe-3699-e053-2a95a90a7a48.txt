When using this product using other topical acne medication at the same time or immediately following the use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .

Warnings For external use only Directions Clean the skin thoroughly before applying .
Cover the entire affected area with a thin layer 1 to 3 times daily .
Avoid contact with eyes Because excessive drying of the skin may occur start with 1 application daily , then gradually increase to 2 or 3 times daily .
If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to once a day or every other day .
