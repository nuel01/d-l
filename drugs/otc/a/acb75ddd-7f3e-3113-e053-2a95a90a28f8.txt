Active Ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antiseptic USE For Hand Sanitizing Warning ■ For external use only ■ Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame When using this product ■ Do not use in or near the eyes .
In case of contact with eyes flush thoroughly with water ■ Use wipes only on unbroken , intact skin ■ If you have sensitive skin or certain allergies , check the list of ingredients on the label before using wipes ■ Do not ingest Stop use and ask a doctor ■ If skin irritation or redness develops Keep out of reach of children ■ Children under 6 years of age use only under adult supervision Directions ■ Wipe hands thoroughly , let skin dry and discard ■ Not recommended for infants Inactive ingredients Aqua , Propylene Glycol , Phenoxyethanol .
