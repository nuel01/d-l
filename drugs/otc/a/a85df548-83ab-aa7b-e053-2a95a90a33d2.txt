Drug Facts Active ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 70 % Purpose Antiseptic Use for hand washing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable , keep away from fire and flame .
Do not use in the eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if : • irritation and redness develop • condition persists for more than 72 hours Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Wet hands thoroughly with product and allow to dry without wiping .
Other information Store at no more than 86ºF ( 30ºC ) Inactive ingredients Aminomethyl propanol , carbomer , glycerin , water .
