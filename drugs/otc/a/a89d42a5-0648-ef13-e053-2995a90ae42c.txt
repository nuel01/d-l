Warnings For external use only .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
If in eyes , flush thoroughly with water .
In the event of an adverse reaction discontinue use and contact a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Open clip - lid and peel back fresh - seal label .
• Remove moist towels to clean skin as required .
• Responsibly discard towel once used .
Do not flush .
• Close lid when not in use to keep remaining towels moist .
• Store in a cool dry place and keep away from heat and direct sunlight .
