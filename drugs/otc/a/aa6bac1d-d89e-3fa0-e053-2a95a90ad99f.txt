Drug Facts Active ingredient Ethanol ( Alcohol ) 75 % Purpose Antiseptic Use • to reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease • recommended for repeated use Warnings For external use only : hands Do not use • in children less than 2 months of age .
• on open skin wounds When using this product keep out of eyes , ears , and mouth .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Place enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
• For children under 6 , use only under adult supervision .
