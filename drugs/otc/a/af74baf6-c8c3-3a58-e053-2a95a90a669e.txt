Acne Treatment Uses For the treatment of acne .
Warnings For External Use Only Keep out of Eyes When using this product skin irritation and dryness is more likely to occur if you use another topical acne medication at the same time .
If irritation occurs , only use one topical acne medication at a time .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Wet face .
Apply one to two pumps of foam onto wet skin .
Gently massage in a circular motion .
Rinse well .
• Because excessive drying of the skin may occur , start with one application daily , then gradually increase to two or three times daily if needed or as directed by a doctor .
• If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to once a day or every other day .
Other Information Store at room temperature .
