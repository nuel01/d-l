Warnings - Do not use • for longer than one week • if you are taking mineral oil • when abdominal pain , nausea , or vomiting are present Ask a doctor before use if you have a sudden change in bowel habits that lasts over two weeks Ask a doctor or pharmacist before use if you are taking any other drug .
Take this product two or more hours before or after other drugs .
Laxatives may affect how other drugs work .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • you experience rectal bleeding • you fail to have a bowel movement after using this product These may indicate a serious condition .
If pregnant or breastfeeding ask a health professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
