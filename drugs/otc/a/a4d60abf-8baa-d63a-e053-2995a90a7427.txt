Ethanol 70 % Purpose : Antiseptic Uses : - For hand washing to decrease bacteria on skin .
- Recommended for repeated use .
WARNINGS : - Flammable , keep away from children .
- For external use only .
- Do not use in eyes .
If contact occurs , flush thoroughly with water .
- Stop use and ask doctor if redness and irritation develop and persist for more than 72 hours .
- Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact the Poison Control Center right away .
Directions : Wet hands thoroughly with product and allow to dry without wiping .
- Do not use other than directed .
Store at room temperature 20 - 25 ° C ( 66 - 77 ° F ) Inactive Ingredients : Aqua , Glycerin , Citrus Aurantium Dulcis ( Orange ) Peel Oil , Thymus Vulgaris ( Red Thyme ) Oil , Tocopherol .
