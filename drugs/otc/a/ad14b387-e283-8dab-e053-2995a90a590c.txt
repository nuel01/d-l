Drug Facts Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 75 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses • Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin that could cause disease • Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only .
Do not use in the eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develops and persists .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Place enough product in your palm to thoroughly cover your hands Rub hands together briskly until dry • Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
Other information • Do not store above 110 ° F ( 43 ° C ) • May discolor certain fabrics or surfaces Inactive Ingredients Acrylic polymer , Triethanolamine , Water .
