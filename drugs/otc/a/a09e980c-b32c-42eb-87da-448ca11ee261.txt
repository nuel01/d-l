Drug Facts Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 70 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses • Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin that could cause disease • recommended for repeated use Warnings Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only .
When using this product do not use in or near the eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and rash appears and lasts .
Keep Out of reach of children .
If swallowed get medical help or contact poison control center ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) right away .
Directions • Place enough product in your palm to thoroughly cover your hands .
• Rub hands together briskly until dry .
• Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
