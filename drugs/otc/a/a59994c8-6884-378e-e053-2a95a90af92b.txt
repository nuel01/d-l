Drug Facts Active ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 70 % w / w Purpose Antimicrobial Use hand sanitizer to help reduce microbes on the skin Warnings Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame For external use only .
When using this product , do not use in or near the eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develops and persists for more than 72 hours and continues Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
