Active ingredient Phenol 1 . 4 % Purpose Oral Anesthetic / Analgesic Uses For the temporarily relief of occasional minor irritation , pain , sore mouth , and sore throat .
Warnings Sore throat warning : Severe or persistent sore throat accompanied by high fever , headache , nausea , and vomiting may be serious .
Consult a doctor promptly .
Do not use more than 2 days or give to children under 3 years of age .
When using this product , do not exceed recommended dosage .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • • sore mouth symptoms do not improve in 7 days • • irritation , pain or redness persists or worsens • • swelling , rash or fever develops If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a doctor before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
