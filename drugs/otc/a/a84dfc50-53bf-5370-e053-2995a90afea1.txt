Drug Facts Active Ingredient Ethyl alcohol 62 % .
Purpose Antiseptic For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings Flammable , keep away from fire or flame . For external use only .
When using this product Avoid contact with eyes .
In case of contact flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor If redness or irritation develop and persist for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Spread on both hands , rub in , let dry .
