Active ingredient Benzalkonium chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antiseptic Use ■ Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin .
For use when soap and water not available Warnings For external use only When using this product ■ Avoid getting into the eyes .
■ In case of eye contact immediately flush eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if ■ Irritation or redness develops ■ Conditions persist for more than 72 hours ■ Redness is present Keep out of reach of children In case of accidental ingestion contact a doctor or Poison Control Center immediately .
