Drug Facts Active ingredient Salicylic acid 2 % Purpose Acne treatment Uses • for the treatment of acne • dries up acne pimples • helps prevent new acne pimples Warnings For external use only When using this product • skin irritation and dryness is more likely to occur if you use another topical acne medication at the same time .
If irritation occurs , only use one topical acne medication at a time .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation becomes severe .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Cleanse the skin thoroughly before applying this product .
• Shake product well before use .
• Cover the entire affected area with a thin layer one to three times daily .
• Because excessive drying of the skin may occur , start with one application daily , then gradually increase to two or three times daily if needed or as directed by a doctor .
• If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to once a day or every other day .
Other information • store away from heat and direct sunlight • store at controlled room temperature : 15 ° – 25 ° C ( 59 ° – 77 ° F ) Inactive ingredients benzyl alcohol , chamomilla recutita ( matricaria ) flower extract , ethoxydiglycol , ext . violet 2 , fragrance , glycerin , menthol , menthoxypropanediol , menthyl lactate , SD alcohol 40 - B , water ( aqua ) Questions or comments ?
