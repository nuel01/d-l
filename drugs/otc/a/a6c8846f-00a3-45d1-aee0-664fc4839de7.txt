Active Ingredient Ethyl alcohol 62 % Purpose Antimicrobial Uses • for handwashing to reduce bacteria on the skin • recommended for repeated use Warnings For external use only Highly flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
Do not use in or near eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash appears and lasts Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
