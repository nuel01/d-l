Active ingredient Menthol 2 percentage Purpose External pain reliever Uses Temporary relief of minor pains of muscle and joints associated with simple back ache arthritis sprains , strains or bruises Warnings Do not use internally , with heating pads or devices , on wounds or damaged skin .
For external use only .
Ask a doctor before use if you have sensitive skin .
When using this product Use only as directed do not bandage tightly avoid contact with eyes and mucus membranes Stop use and ask a doctor if condition worsens symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days skin irritation develops If you are pregnant or nursing a baby , concult a physician before use .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN .
DO NOT APPLY TO CHILDREN UNDER 2 YEARS OF AGE .
In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact a Posion Control Center immediately .
Directions Adult and children 2 years of age and older : Clean skin thoroughly .
Apply liberally to affected area not more than 3 - 4 times daily .
No protective cover needed .
May be used with ice packs .
May be used with wet or dry bandages , but wrap loosely .
Children under 2 years of age , consult a doctor .
Other information store between 59 - 86 Fahrenheit ( 15 - 30 degrees Celsius ) keep tightly closed do not use , pour , spill or store near heat or open flame .
