Drug Facts Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 percent Purpose Antibacterial Uses for hand washing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush with clean water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develops .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
