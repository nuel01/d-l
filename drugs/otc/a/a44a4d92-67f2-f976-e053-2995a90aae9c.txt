• Glycerin • Aloe Vera Extract • Water • Carbomer • Aminomethyl Propanol Active Ingredient ( s ) Alcohol 70 % v / v .
Purpose : Antiseptic Purpose Antiseptic , Hand Sanitizer Use To help reduce bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame Do not use on damaged or broken skin .
Do not leave near fire .
Wash with enough water , if it gets into eyes .
Do not drink .
Stop use and ask a doctor if skin irritation or redness develops .
In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away Directions Apply enough gel to wet hands and rub together until dry .
Use 1 to 3 times a day .
