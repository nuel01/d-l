Active Ingredients ( s ) Ethyl Alcohol 75 % v / v Purpose Antiseptic Use ( s ) : Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially can cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings : For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from heat or flame Do not use • On children less than 2 months of age • On Open skin wounds When using this product : Do not use in or near the eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor : If irritation or rash occurs .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children : If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions : • Put enough product on hands to cover all surfaces .
Rub hands together until dry .
• Supervise children under 6 years of age when using this product .
