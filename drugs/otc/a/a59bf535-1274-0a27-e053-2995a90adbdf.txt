Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 75 % v / v Purpose Antiseptic Uses A hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
When using this product do not use in or near the eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash appears on the skin .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Place enough to cover both hands in the palm , and rub hands together until dry .
Children under 6 years of age should be supervised by adult when applying this product .
