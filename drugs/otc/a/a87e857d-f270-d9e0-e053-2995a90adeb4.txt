This is a hand sanitizer manufactured according to the institute of personal care science .
The hand sanitizer is manufactured using only the following United States Pharmacopoeia ( USP ) grade ingredients .
• Isopropyl Alcohol ( 70 % w / w ) in an aqueous solution denatured according to Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau regulations in 27 CFR part 20 .
