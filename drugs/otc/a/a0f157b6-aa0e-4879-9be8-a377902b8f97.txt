Active ingredients Purpose Benzalkonium 0 . 13 % First Aid Antiseptic Lidocaine HCl 2 . 5 % Pain Relieving Spray Uses First aid to help prevent bacterial contamination or skin infection , and for temporary relief of pain and itching associated with minor : cuts , scrapes , burns , sunburn , skin irritations Warnings For external use only Ask a doctor before use if you have • deep or puncture wounds • animal bites • serious burns When using this product • do not use in or near the eyes • do not apply over large area of the body or in large quantities • do not apply over raw surfaces or blistered areas Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days , or clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of reach of children If swallowed get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away Directions • Adults and children 2 years and older : clean the affected area .
Apply small amount on the area 1 - 3 times daily ; may be covered with a sterile bandage ( let dry first ) • Children Under 2 years : Consult a physician .
