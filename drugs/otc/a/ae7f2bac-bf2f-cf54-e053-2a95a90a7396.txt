Drug Facts Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 75 % Purpose Antiseptic Use for sanitizing hands to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings : Flammable , Keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only .
When using this product Keep out of eyes .
If contact with eyes occurs , rinse promptly and thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor If significant irritation , or sensitization develops .
Consult a physician if irritation persists for more than 5 days .
Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Peel back label at tab remove wipes as needed , reseal pouch by pressing label firmly back into place , wipe hands thoroughly with product , allow hands to air dry .
Discard after single use .
Other Information Do not store above 105 F Inactive Ingredients Aloe barbadensis leaf juice ( aloe vera ) , fragrance , glycerin , propylene glycol , water .
