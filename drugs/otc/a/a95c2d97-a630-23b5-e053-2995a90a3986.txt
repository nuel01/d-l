Active Ingredient ( s ) Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 1 % .
Purpose : Antiseptic Purpose Antiseptic , Wet Wipes Use For disinfection of hands , face , legs , skin , surfaces , and to reduce germs and bacteria on the skin if you cannot wash hands Warnings For external use only .
May cause irritation on sensitive skin , discontinue use if irritation or rash appears .
Avoid direct contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush with plenty of water .
In case of skin irritation , wash off skin immediately with plenty of water , using soap if available .
Seek medical attention immediately if irritation persists .
Keep out of the reach of children .
Directions Remove top , open bag , lift pop top , feed wipe through opening , and gently pull to remove wipe .
Gently wipe hands and / or face to remove 99 . 9 % of germs and bacteria .
After use , close top to keep wipes moist .
Do not flush , please discard wipe in trash after use .
Please recycle canister .
Other information Shelf life 2 years if unopened sealed container .
