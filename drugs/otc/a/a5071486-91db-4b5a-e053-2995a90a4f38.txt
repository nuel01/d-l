Active Ingredient Alcohol 75 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses : Hand Sanitizer to help reduce bacteria that potentially cause disease .
For use when soap and water are not available . Use as part of your daily cleansing routine .
Warnings : Flammable .
Keep away from fire . For external use only .
When using this product avoid contact with or near the eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash appears and lasts .
These may be signs of a serious condition .
Keep out of reach of children , if swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
