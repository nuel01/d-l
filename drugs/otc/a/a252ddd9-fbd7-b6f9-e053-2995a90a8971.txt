Active ingredient Sodium hypochlorite Purpose Antiseptic Uses Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin recommended for repeated use .
Warnings • Flammable • Keep away from fire or flame • For external use only When using this product • avoid contact with eyes in case of eye contact immediately flush eyes with water , call a doctor • avoid contact with broken skin • Discontinue use if irritation or redness develops .
If condition persists from more than 72 hours , consult a doctor Keep out of reach of children .
Children should only use this product unde adult supervision .
Do not drink .
Not edible .
In case of accidental ingestion , seek professional assistance or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Other information • Do not store above 150 F .
