Ethyl Alcohol ( Ethanol ) 70 % v / v Purpose : Antiseptic Uses : Hand sanitizer to help reduce bacteria on the skin .
WARNINGS : Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only .
When using this product do not use in or near the eyes .
In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or rash lasts .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact the Poison Control Center right away .
Directions : • Place enough product in your palm to thoroughly spread on both hands and rub into the skin until dry .
• Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
• Do not store above 104 F • May discolor some fabrics .
Harmful to wood finishes and plastics .
Inactive Ingredients : Water ( Aqua ) , Carthamus Tinctorius ( Safflower ) Oleosomes , Glycerin , Polyacrylate Crosspolymer - 6 , Hydroxyethylcellulose , Sodium Hydroxide [ MULTIMEDIA ] [ MULTIMEDIA ]
