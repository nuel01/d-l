Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 75 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses Hand sanitizer to reduce bacteria on the skin .
Warnings Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only .
When using this product do not use in or near the eyes . In case of contact , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develops and persists .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact the Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Place enough product on hand to cover fully • Rub hands together until dry • Children under 6 years of age should be supervised when using this product .
