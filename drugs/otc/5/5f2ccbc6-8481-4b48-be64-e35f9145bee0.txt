Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 1 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses to help decrease bacteria on the skin .
For use when soap and water are not available .
Warnings For external use only .
When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse thoroughly with water .
Do not use • on children less than 2 months of age • on open wounds Stop using this product and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops and lasts more than 7 days .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • pump enough product to your palm to thoroughly cover your hands , rub together for at least 30 seconds , until dry .
• children under 6 years should be supervised when using this product .
Other information store at room temperature .
