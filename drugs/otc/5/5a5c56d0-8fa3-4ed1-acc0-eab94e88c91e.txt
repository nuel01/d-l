Drug Facts Active ingredient Salicylic Acid 1 . 85 % Purpose Anti - Dandruff Uses • helps prevent reoccurance of flaking and itching associated with dandruff .
Warnings For external use only .
Avoid contact with the eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
If condition worsens , or does not improve after regular use of this product as directed , stop use of this product and consult a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Gently massage into scalp and work through hair to the ends .
Leave on 30 seconds for normal hair and 2 - 3 minutes for damaged and / or processed hair .
Rinse with warm water , dry and style as usual .
For best results use at least twice a week or as directed by a doctor .
Store at room temperature .
