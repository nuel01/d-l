External Analgesic Purpose External Analgesic Uses For the temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with : • simple backache • arthrits • strains • sprains • For external use only .
Do not use otherwise than as directed .
Do not use • with a bandage • on wounds or damage skin • with a heating pad • on a child under 12 years of age • over extensive areas of the body • on irritated skin Ask a doctor before use if you have redness over the affected area When using this product • avoid contact with eyes or mucuos membranes • do not bandage Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsen or symptoms persists for more than 7 days • symptoms clear up and occur again within a few days • excessive skin irritation occurs if pregnant or breast - feeding ask a health professional before use Keep out of the reach of children to avoid accidental poisoning .
If swallowed , induce vomiting and call a physician or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions use only as directed • adults and children 12 years of age and older : apply to affected area no more than 3 to 4 times daily • children under 12 years of age : ask a doctor Other information • store at 15 ° to 25 ° C ( 59 ° to 77 ° F ) Inactive Ingredients cajeput oil , clove oil , essential oil of peppermint , eucalyptus oil , juniper , menthol , white mineral oil .
