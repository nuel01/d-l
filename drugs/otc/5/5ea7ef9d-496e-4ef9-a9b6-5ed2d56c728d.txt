Drug Facts : Active Ingredients Salicylic acid 1 . 5 % Purpose Acne treatment Uses for the management of acne Warnings For external use only When using this product • Skin irritation and dryness are more likely to occur if you use another topical acne medication at the same time .
If irritation occurs , only use one topical acne medication at a time .
• Avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor • If irritation becomes severe .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Wet face , then squeeze out a dime or quarter size amount and apply to face .
• Use exfoliating brush in a gentle , circular motion around face and other areas with acne , avoiding eye area .
• Rinse thoroughly with warm water .
