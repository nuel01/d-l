Active Ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 1 % Purpose Antibacterial Use To decrease bacteria on the skin that could cause disease Directions • Rub a dime sized drop into hands .
• For children under 6 use under adult supervision .
Warnings • For external use only - hands .
• Keep out of eyes .
Avoid contact with broken skin .
• Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops .
• Do not inhale or ingest .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a poison control center right away .
