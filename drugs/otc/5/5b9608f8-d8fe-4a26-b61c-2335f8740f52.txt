Active ingredient Pyrithione zinc 1 % Purpose Antidandruff Use helps eliminate recurrence of scalp flaking , scaling , and itching associated with dandruff Warnings For external use only When using this product do not get into eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and consult a physician if condition worsens or does not improve after regular use Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
