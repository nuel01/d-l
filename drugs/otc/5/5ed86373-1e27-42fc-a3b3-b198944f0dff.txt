INDICATIONS AND USAGE P32 chromic phosphate is indicated for intracavitary instillation for the treatment of peritoneal or pleural effusions caused by metastatic disease , and may be injected interstitially for the treatment of cancer .
It is also used in hemophilia patients for intraarticular injections .
Mechanism of Action Radioactive phosphorous works by local irradiation by beta emission .
CONTRAINDICATIONS P32 sodium phosphate should not be used in the presence of ulcerative tumors .
WARNINGS Not for intravascular injection .
DOSAGE AND ADMINISTRATION Shake the vial well before injection .
For intraperitoneal instillation , administer 10 - 20 mCi For intrapleural instillation , administer 6 to 12 mCi Doses for interstitial use should be based on estimated gram weight of tumor , about 0 . 1 to 0 . 5 mCi / gm .
