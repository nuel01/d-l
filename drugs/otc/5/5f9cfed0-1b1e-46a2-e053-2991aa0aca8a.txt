When using this product • use only as directed • avoid contact with eyes , mucous membranes or rashes Stop use and ask a doctor if • rash , itching or excessive skin irritation develops • conditions worsen • symptoms persist for more than 7 days • symptoms clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
