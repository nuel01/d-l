Walgreens Lubricant Eye Drops Drug Facts Active ingredient Carboxymethylcellulose Sodium 0 . 5 % Purpose Eye Lubricant Uses • for the temporary relief of burning , irritation and discomfort due to dryness of the eye or exposure to wind or sun • may be used as a protectant against further irritation .
Warnings For use in the eyes only .
• Retain outer carton for full product drug facts .
Do not use • if solution changes color or becomes cloudy .
When using this product • avoid contamination , do not touch tip of container to any surface .
• replace cap after use .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • you experience eye pain , changes in vision , continued redness or irritation of the eye .
• the condition worsens or persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep this and all drugs out of the reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) .
Directions • Shake well before use .
• Instill 1 or 2 drops in the affected eye ( s ) as needed .
