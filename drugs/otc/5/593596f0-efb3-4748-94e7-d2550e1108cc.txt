Active ingredient ( per tablet ) Calcium Carbonate USP 500 mg Purpose Antacid Uses relieves • heartburn • acid indigestion • sour stomach • upset stomach associated with these symptoms Warnings Ask a doctor or pharmacist before use if you are presently taking a prescription drug .
Antacids may interact with certain prescription drugs .
When using this product • • do not take more than 15 tablets in 24 hours • • if pregnant do not take more than 10 tablets in 24 hours • • do not use the maximum dosage for more than 2 weeks except under the advice and supervision of a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
Directions • • adults and children 12 years of age and over : chew 2 - 4 tablets completely as symptoms occur , or as directed by a doctor .
Do not swallow tablets whole .
• • do not take for symptoms that persist for more than 2 weeks unless advised by a doctor Other information • • each tablet contains : elemental calcium 200 mg • • store at room temperature .
Keep the container tightly closed .
