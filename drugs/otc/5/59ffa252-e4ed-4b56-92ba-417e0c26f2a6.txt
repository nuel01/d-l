For general hand washing and germ killing .
Alert Open refillable reservoir dispensers are susceptible to microbial contamination even when using antimicrobial soaps .
Ideally , liquid products should be dispensed from closed , SANITARY SEALEDTM refills .
Open refillable reservoir dispensers should be washed and sanitized before every refilling .
Soaps should not be added to a partially empty dispenser as this practice of " topping off " may lead to bacterial contamination of soaps and compromise the beneficial effects of hand cleaning .
