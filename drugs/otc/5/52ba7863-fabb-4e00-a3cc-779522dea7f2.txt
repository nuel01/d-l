Drug Facts Active Ingredient ( by volume ) Isopropyl alcohol ( 70 % conc . )
Purpose First aid antiseptic Uses • First aid to help prevent the risk of infection in minor cuts , scrapes , and burns .
WARNINGS • For external use only • If taken internally , serious gastric disturbances will result .
CHILDREN UNDER 12 YEARS SHOULD BE SUPERVISED IN THE USE OF THIS PRODUCT .
CHILDREN UNDER 2 YEARS OF AGE : CONSULT A DOCTOR .
When using this product : Do not get into eyes • if eye contact occurs flush thoroughly with water .
• do not apply over large areas of the body • do not use longer than one week unless directed by a doctor .
Ask a doctor before use if you have deep puncture wounds , animal bites or serious burns .
Stop use and ask doctor if Condition persists or gets worse .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) right away .
Directions • For use as a first aid antiseptic : • clean affected area • if bandaged , let dry first • apply small amount of this product on affected area 1 - 3 times a day • may be covered with a sterile bandage .
Other Information • Keep cap tightly closed , storage at room temperature .
• Do not shake bottle .
Hold away from face when opening • Does not contain , nor is intended as a substitute for grain or ethyl alcohol .
Inactive Ingredient : Purified water .
