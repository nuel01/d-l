Drug Facts Active Ingredients Camphor 11 % , Menthol 10 % Purpose Topical analgesics Uses For temporary relief of minor aches and pains of neck and shoulder muscles and joints Warnings For external use only When using this product : • Use only as directed • Avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes • Do not apply to wounds , damaged or irritated skin • Do not bandage or cover with wrap or use heating pad • Do not use 1 hour prior to bathing or 30 minutes after bathing .
Stop use and ask a doctor if : • Condition worsens • Severe skin irritation occurs • Pain persists for more than 7 days • Pain clears up and then recurs a few days later .
If pregnant or breastfeeding , or if you have sensitive skin , ask a healthcare professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • For adults and children over 12 years , rub well on the affected area • Repeat 3 to 4 times daily • For children 12 years of age or younger , consult a doctor before use .
Other information This product may cause allergic reaction in some individuals .
Test on small area before use .
