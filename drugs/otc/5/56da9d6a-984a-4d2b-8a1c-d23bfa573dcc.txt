Drug Facts Active Ingredient White Petrolatum 95 % Purpose A Skin protectant Uses For the temporary protection of minor cuts , scrapes , burns .
Protects chafed skin Warnings FOR EXTERNAL USE ONLY .
When using this product : Avoid contact with eyes .
DO NOT apply over deep or punctured wounds , infections or lacerations IF CONDITION WORSENS OR DOES NOT IMPROVE WITHIN 7 DAYS STOP USE AND CONSULT A PHYSICIAN .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN .
In the event of accidental ingestion contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Cleanse area with mild soap and water .
Apply liberally as often as necessary .
Storage • Store at Controlled Room Temperature 15 ° - 30 ° C ( 59 ° - 86 ° F ) .
