uses Temporarily relieves these symptoms due to hay fever or other upper respiratory allergies and common cold , sneezing , runny nose , itchy , watery eyes , itchy throat and nose Ask a doctor before use if you have glaucoma , a breathing problem such as emphysema or chronic bronchitis , trouble urinating due to an enlarged prostate gland .
directions Adults and children 12 years and over : take 25 to 50 mg ( 1 to 2 tablet ) every 4 to 6 hours ; not more than 12 tablets in 24 hours .
Children 6 years to 12 years of age : take 25 mg ( 1 tablet ) every 4 to 6 hours ; not more than 6 tablets in 24 hours .
