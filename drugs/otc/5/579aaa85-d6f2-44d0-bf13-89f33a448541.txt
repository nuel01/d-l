Active Ingredient Salicylic Acid 12 % Purpose Wart remover Uses • For the removal of common warts .
The common wart is easily recognized by the rough “ cauliflower - like ” appearance of the surface .
• For the removal of plantar warts on the bottom of the foot .
The plantar wart is recognized by its location only on the bottom of the foot , its tenderness , and the interruption of the footprint pattern .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use this product on irritated skin , on any area that is infected or reddened , if you are a diabetic , or if you have poor blood circulation .
Do not use on moles , birthmarks , warts with hair growing from them , warts on the face or mucous membranes .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , call poison control or seek medical help .
Stop use and see your doctor if discomfort or condition persists .
Directions Wash affected area .
If desired , soak in warm water up to 5 minutes .
Dry area thoroughly .
Apply a thick layer of Terrasil to wart .
Cover affected area with a clean bandage .
Repeat procedure 1 - 2 times daily until wart is removed ( for up to 12 weeks ) .
Other Information Store at room temperature .
Inactive Ingredients bentonite clay , cera alba ( organic beeswax ) , magnesium oxide , peppermint oil , silver oxide , simmondsia chinensis ( jojoba seed oil ) , zinc oxide .
