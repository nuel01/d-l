Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 70 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only .
Flammable : Keep away from fire or flame .
When using this product Do not use in eyes .
In case of contact with eyes , rinse with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develop and persist .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help promptly .
Directions Wet hands thoroughly with product and allow to dry without wiping .
Other information Store under 105 ° F Inactive Ingredients Acrylates / Vinyl Isodecanoate Crosspolymer , Aloe Barbadensis Leaf , PEG - 6 , PEG - 12 Dimethicone , Water .
May contain fragrance .
