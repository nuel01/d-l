Luden ’ s BlueRaspberry Throat Drops Drug Facts Active ingredient ( in each drop ) Pectin 2 . 8 mg Purpose Oral demulcent Uses For temporary relief of minor discomfort and protection of irritated areas in sore mouth and sore throat .
Warnings Sore throat warning : if sore throat is severe , persists for more than 2 days , is accompanied or followed by fever , headache , rash , swelling , nausea or vomiting , consult a doctor promptly .
These may be serious .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • sore mouth does not improve in 7 days • irritation , pain or redness persists or worsens .
Keep out of reach of children Directions • Adults and children 3 years of age and older : allow one drop to dissolve slowly in mouth .
• may be repeated as needed or as directed by a doctor .
• children under 3 years of age : ask a doctor Other information • store at 20º - 25ºC ( 68º - 77ºF ) in a dry place Questions ?
