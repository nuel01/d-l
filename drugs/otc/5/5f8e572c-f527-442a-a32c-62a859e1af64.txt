DRUG FACTS ACTIVE INGREDIENT Sodium Monofluorophosphate - 0 . 76 % ( 0 . 1 % w / v fluoride ion ) PURPOSE Anticavity toothpaste USE Helps protect against cavities .
WARNINGS Keep out of the reach of children under 6 years of age .
If more than used for brushing is accidentally swallowed , get medical help of contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
DIRECTIONS Adults & Children 6 years of age & older : Brush teeth thoroughly , preferably after each meal or at least twice a day , or as directed by a dentist or doctor .
Children 2 to 6 years : Use only a pea sized amount and supervise child ' s brushing and rinsing ( to minimize swallowing ) .
Children under 2 years : Ask a dentist or physician .
INACTIVE INGREDIENTS Calcium Carbonate , Purified Water , Sorbitol , Precipitated Silica , Sodium Lauryl Sulphate , Glycerine , Carboxy Methyl Cellulose , Flavor , Sodium Saccharin , Sodium Benzoate .
