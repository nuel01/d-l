Active Ingredients Royal Jelly ( 0 . 5 % ) Purpose Skin protectant Uses Moisturizes skin .
Helps to reduce the wrinkles .
Warnings For external use only Do not use when skin is red , inflamed , irritated , or painful When using this product - do not apply on other parts of the body - avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water - do not apply directly to wound or open cut Stop use and ask a doctor if - Rash or irritation on skin develops and lasts Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) right away .
Directions After cleansing face , apply the adequate amount on skin .
