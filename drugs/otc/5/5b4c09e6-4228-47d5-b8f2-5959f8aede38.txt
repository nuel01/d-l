Active ingredient Methyl Salicylate 14 . 0 % Menthol 3 . 0 % Keep Out of Reach of Children Uses For the temporary relief of minor ach3es and pains of muscles and joints associated with arthritis and rheumatism .
Also good for simple backache , strains and sprains .
Warnings Use only as directed .
If pain persists more than 10 days , or redness is present , or in conditions affecting children under 12 years of age , consult your physician immediately .
Discontinue use if excessive irritation of the skin develops .
Avoid getting into the eyes or on mucous membranes .
For external use only .
Use only as directed .
Do not apply to wounds , broken or irritated skin .
Do not use with a heating pad .
Do not bandage tightly .
If swallowed , induce vomiting and contact a physician or poison control center immediately .
Directions Apply fast vanishing lotion with gentle massage to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily .
