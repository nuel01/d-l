Purpose Antiseptic Use [ s ] • to decrease bacteria on the skin that could cause disease • recommended for repeated use Warnings For external use only - hands .
Flammable , keep away from heat or flame .
Do not use • in children less than 2 months of age • on open skin wounds When using this product • keep out of eyes , in case of contact with eyes , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
• avoid contact with broken skin .
• do not inhale or ingest .
Stop use and ask a doctor if skin irritation develops .
Keep out of reach of children .

Directions • Wet hand thoroughly and allow to dry without wiping .
• For children under 6 , use only under adult supervision .
• Not recommended for infants .
