Frequent or prolonged use may cause nasal congestion to come back or get worse .
• • use only as directed • • temporary discomfort such as burning , stinging , sneezing or an increase in nasal discharge may occur • • use of this container by more than one person may spread infection Stop use and ask a doctor if symptoms persist If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
