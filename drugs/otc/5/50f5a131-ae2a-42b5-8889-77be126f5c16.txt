Active Ingredient Capsicum extract 0 . 025 % as Capsaicin Purpose Topical Analgesic Uses Temporarily relieves minor pain associated with : • arthritis • simple backache • strains • sprains • bruises Warnings • For external use only .
• Discontinue use at least 1 hour before a bath or shower and do not use immediately after a bath or shower .
• If pregnant or breastfeeding consult a healthcare professional before use .
• Allergy Alert : if you are allergic to any ingredients of this product or chili peppers contact a doctor before use .
Do not use • on wounds or damage skin • with , or at the same time as , other external analgesic products When using this product • use only as directed • do not bandage tightly or use with a heating pad • avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days • rash , itching or excessive skin irritation develops Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
