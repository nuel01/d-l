Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 1 % Purpose Antiseptic Uses to help decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings For external use only When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
If contact occurs , rinse eyes thoroughly with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops and lasts .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions • Pump enough product on your palm to thoroughly cover your hands , rub together until dry .
• Children under 6 years should be supervised when using this product .
