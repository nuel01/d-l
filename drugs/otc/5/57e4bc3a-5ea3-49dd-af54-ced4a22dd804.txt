ACTIVE INGREDIENT BENZALKONIUM CHLORIDE 0 . 1 % PURPOSE ANTIMICROBIAL USES For hand sanitizing to reduce bacteria on the skin .
Recommended for repeated use .
WARNINGS For external use only .
Avoid direct contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , please flush the eyes with water .
Contact a doctor if irritation or redness persists .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help and / or contact a poison control center immediately .
DIRECTIONS Spray the hands and rub together briskly to cover all surfaces .
