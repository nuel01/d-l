antiseptic antimicrobial ALCOHOL FOAMING HAND SANITIZER This product is an alcohol - based hand sanitizer designed to produce a light foam and spread evenly .
Bac - San meets the US Center for Disease Controls recommendations for broad - spectrum sanitizers for use on hands .
Bac - San sanitizes hands and leaves a fresh aloe scent .
Directions 1 .
Apply liberally into palm of hand 2 .
Rub hands together to spread foam evenly 3 .
Allow hands to air dry FIRST AID : IF IN EYES : Rinse cautiously with water for several minutes .
Remove contact lenses , if present and easy to do .
Continue rinsing .
IF ON SKIN : Wash with plenty of water .
IF INHALED : Remove victim to fresh air and keep at rest in a posltion comfortable for breathing .
IF SWALLOWED : Rinse mouth .
Do NOT induce vomiting .
Keep out of reach of children .
