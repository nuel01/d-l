Taking this product with or after meals may reduce stomach upset .
• Your urine will become reddish - orange in color .
This is not harmful , but care should be taken to avoid staining clothing or other items .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • Your symptoms last for more than 2 days • You suspect you are having an adverse reaction to the medication If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health care professional before use .

Directions • Adults and children 12 years of age and over : take 2 tablets 3 times daily with a full glass of water , with or after meals as needed .
• Children under 12 years of age : consult a doctor .
