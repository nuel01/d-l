Drug Facts Active ingredient Menthol 2 . 0 % Purpose Topical Analgesic Uses • ♦ temporarily relieves minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with • ♦ simple backache • ♦ arthritis • ♦ strains • ♦ bruises • ♦ sprains Warnings For external use only When using this product • ♦ do not use with other topical pain relievers • ♦ do not use with heating pads or other heating devices • ♦ avoid contact with the eyes • ♦ do not bandage tightly • ♦ do not apply to wounds or damaged skin Stop use and ask a doctor if • ♦ condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days .
If pregnant or breastfeeding , ask a health professional before use Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately at 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 .
Directions • ♦ Clean affected area before applying product • ♦ adults and children 2 years of age and older : apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily • ♦ children under 2 years of age : do not use , consult a doctor .
