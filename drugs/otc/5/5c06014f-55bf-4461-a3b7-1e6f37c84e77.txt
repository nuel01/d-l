Drug Facts Active Ingredients Salicylic Acid 2 . 0 % Purpose Acne treatment Uses • penetrates pores to clear most acne blemishes .
Helps keep skin clear of pimples .
Warnings For external use only .
Using other acne medication at the same time or immediately following the use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one medication should be used unless directed by a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Cleanse the skin thoroughly before applying medication .
• Shake well before use .
• Apply a thin layer directly to affected areas one to three times daily .
