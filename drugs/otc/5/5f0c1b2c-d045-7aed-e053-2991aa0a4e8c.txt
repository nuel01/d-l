Active Ingredients Isopropyl alcohol , 95 % Purpose Ear Drying aid in an Anhydrous glycerin base , 5 % Uses Dries water in the ears and relieves water - clogged ears after : • swimming • showering • bathing • washing the hair Warnings Flammable : Keep away from fire or flame .
Do not use in the eyes .
Ask a doctor before use if you have • ear drainage or dischage • pain , irritiaon , or rash in ear • had ear surgery • dizziness Stop use and aks a doctor if irritation ( too much burning ) or pain occurs Keep our of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions Apply 4 to 5 drops in each affected ear .
