Drug Facts Active ingredient Salicylic Acid ( 0 . 55 % ) Purpose Acne treatment Use • For the treatment of acne .
• Penetrates pores to eliminate most acne blemishes .
Warnings • For external use only .
Using other topical acne medication at the same time or immediately following the use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one medication should be used unless directed by a doctor .
• Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Cleanse the skin thoroughly before applying medication .
• Cover the entire affected area with a thin layer once daily .
• If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to every other day .
• Use at night following toner .
• Dispense about a dime size and apply lightly in an upward , outward motion to face and neck .
