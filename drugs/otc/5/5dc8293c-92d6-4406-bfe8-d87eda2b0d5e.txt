Active ingredients Avobenzone 1 . 5 % Octocrylene 7 % Terephthalylidene Dicamphor Sulfonic Acid 0 . 5 % Titanium dioxide 3 . 3 % Warnings For external use only .
Do not use on damaged or broken skin .
If rash occurs , discontinue use and consult a health care practitioner .
Keep out of eyes .
Rinse with water to remove .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Avoid contact with textiles .
Directions Apply generously and evenly 15 minutes before sun exposure .
Reapply at least every 2 hours .
Use a water resistant sunscreen if swimming or sweating .
For use on children less than 6 months of age , consult a health care practitioner .
