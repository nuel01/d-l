If you do not know if your child ’ s prescription drug contains an MAOI , ask a doctor or pharmacist before giving this product .
Ask a doctor before use if the child has • Cough that occurs with too much phlegm ( mucus ) • Persistent or chronic cough such as occurs with asthma Stop use and ask doctor if cough lasts more than 7 days , comes back , or occurs with fever , rash , or persistent headache .
These could be signs of a serious illness .
Keep this and all drugs out of the reach of children .
In case of overdose , seek professional assistance or contact a Poison control center right away .
