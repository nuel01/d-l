ASPIRIN Purpose Pain Reliever Inactive Ingredients In case of over dose get medical help and contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
For temporary relief of minor aches and pains or as recommended by your Doctor .
Because of this products delayed release action , this product will not provide fast relief of headaches or symptoms needing immediate relief .
Do not use if you have ever had an allergic reaction to any other pain relievers / fever reducers .
Children or teenagers who are recovering from the Chicken Pox or flu like symptoms should not use this product .
When using this product if changes in behavior occur with nausea and vomiting occur , consult a Doctor because this could be a sign of Reye ' s Syndrome , a rare but serious illness .
Directions - drink a full glass of water with each dose .
Adults and children 12 years of age and over : take 4 to 8 tablets every 4 hours not to exceed48 tablets in 24 hours unless directed by a doctor .
