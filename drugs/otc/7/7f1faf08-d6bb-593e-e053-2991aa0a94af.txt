Drug Facts Active Ingredient Benzalkonium chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antiseptic Hand Sanitizer Uses Hand Sanitizer to help decrease bacteria on the skin .
• Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings For external use only .
Do not use in ears or mouth .
When using this product avoid contact with the eyes .
• In case of contact flush eyes with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if redness or irritation develops and persists for more than 72 hours .
Keep out of reach of children .
Children should be supervised when using this product .
Do not freeze .
Directions Apply a small amount into palms of hands and spread on both hands .
