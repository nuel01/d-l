Drug Facts Active Ingredient Salicylic Acid ( 2 % ) Purpose Acne medication Use For the treatment of acne .
Warnings • For external use only .
Using other topical acne medication at the same time or immediately following the use of this product may increase dryness or irritation of the skin .
If this occurs , only one medication should be used unless directed by a doctor .
• Keep out of the reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Cleanse skin thoroughly before applying .
• Cover the affected area with a thin layer one to three times a day .
• Because excessive drying of the skin may occur , start with one application daily , then gradually increase to two or three times daily if needed or as directed by a doctor .
• If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application to once a day or every other day .
