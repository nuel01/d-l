Active Ingredient Glycerin 3 g Purpose Glycerin Hyperosmotic Laxative Uses For relief of occasional constipation Warnings For rectal use only Do not use when rectal irritation , abdominal pain , nausea , fever , or vomiting are present unless directed by a doctor .
Ask a doctor before using any laxative if you have • a sudden change in bowel habits lasting more than 2 weeks • already used a laxative for more than 1 week Stop using and ask a doctor if you have • rectal discomfort • burning sensation • rectal bleeding • no bowel movement within 1 hour of using this product Keep out of reach of children If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .

Discard unit after use .
No left fluid should be re - used .
Other Information Store below 77 ° F ( 25 ° C ) in a cool dry place and protected from sunlight .
