WARNINGS • For external use only .
Use only as directed .
Avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes .
• Do not cover with bandage .
• Do not use on wounds or damaged skin .
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN Consult physician for children under 12 .
DIRECTIONS Apply product directly to affected area .
Product may be used as necessary , but should not be used more than four times per day .
