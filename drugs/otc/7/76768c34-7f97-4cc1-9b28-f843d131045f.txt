Active Ingredient Triclosan 0 . 1 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses For handwashing to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Recommended for repeated use .
Warnings For external use only When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact immediately flush eyes with water , call a doctor .
Avoid contact with broken skin .
Discontinue use if irritation or redness develops .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours , consult a doctor .
Keep out of reach of children Children should only use this product with adult supervision .
Do not drink .
Not edible .
In case of accidental ingestion seek professional assistance or contact Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions Wet hands .
Apply palmful to hands .
Scrub thoroughly .
Rinse .
