Active Ingredient : White Petroleum USP ( 76 % w / w ) Purpose : Skin Protectant Warnings : For external use only Stop use and ask a doctor if condition worsens , symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again in a few days .
Keep out of the reach of children .
If swallowed get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions for Diaper Rash : • Change wet and soiled diapers promptly .
• Cleanse the diaper area and allow to dry .
• Apply as needed .
Inactive Ingredients : Mineral oil , Vitamin A , Vitamin D Other Information : • Store at room temperature 59 - 86 F ( 15 - 30 C ) • Avoid excessive heat .
Uses : • Helps treat and prevent diaper rash • Helps seal out wetness • Temporarily protects minor cuts , scrapes , burns • Temporarily helps protect and relieve chapped and cracked skin or lips • Helps prevent and protect from the drying effects of wind and cold weather • Helps prevent and protect chafed skin or minor skin irritations associated with diaper rash • With each diaper change , especially at bedtime when exposure to wet diapers may be prolonged When using this product , do not get into eyes .
Do not use on • deep puncture wounds • animals bites • serious burns [ MULTIMEDIA ] [ MULTIMEDIA ]
