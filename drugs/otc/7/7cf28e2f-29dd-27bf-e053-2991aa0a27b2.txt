Directions • apply liberally 15 minutes before sun exposure • use a water resistant sunscreen if swimming or sweating • reapply at least every 2 hours • Sun Protection Measures .
Spending time in the sun increases your risk of skin cancer and early skin aging .
To decrease this risk , regularly use a sunscreen with a broad spectrum SPF of 15 or higher and other sun protection measures including : • limit time in the sun , especially from 10 a . m . – 2 p . m . • wear long - sleeve shirts , pants , hats , and sunglasses • children under 6 months : Ask a doctor Inactive Ingredients WATER • CYCLOPENTASILOXANE • GLYCERIN • BUTYLENE GLYCOL • CYCLOMETHICONE • DIPHENYLSILOXY PHENYL TRIMETHICONE • SYNTHETIC FLUORPHLOGOPITE • SD ALCOHOL 40 - B • DIMETHICONE / VINYL DIMETHICONE CROSSPOLYMER • SODIUM GLUTAMATE • LAURYL PEG - 9 POLYDIMETHYLSILOXYETHYL DIMETHICONE • PEG - 10 DIMETHICONE • SORBITAN SESQUIISOSTEARATE • ISOPROPYL MYRISTATE • BARIUM SULFATE • BIS - BUTYLDIMETHICONE POLYGLYCERYL - 3 • ARGANIA SPINOSA KERNEL OIL • POLYQUATERNIUM - 51 • NYLON - 12 • THYMUS SERPYLLUM EXTRACT • SODIUM ACETYLATED HYALURONATE • VACCINIUM MACROCARPON ( CRANBERRY ) SEED OIL • CITRUS JUNOS SEED EXTRACT • DISTEARDIMONIUM HECTORITE • ALUMINUM HYDROXIDE • STEARIC ACID • ALUMINA • TRIETHOXYSILYLETHYL POLYDIMETHYLSILOXYETHYL DIMETHICONE • SODIUM CITRATE • TRIETHOXYCAPRYLYLSILANE • POLYSILICONE - 2 • TRISODIUM EDTA • ALCOHOL • DIMETHICONE • TOCOPHEROL • CITRIC ACID • HYDROGEN DIMETHICONE ( ONLY IN : GOLDEN 5 • NEUTRAL 4 , 5 • ROSE 4 , 5 ) • BHT • SODIUM METABISULFITE • HYDRATED SILICA • DIMETHICONE / PEG - 10 / 15 CROSSPOLYMER • TIN OXIDE • POLYMETHYLSILSESQUIOXANE • DIPROPYLENE GLYCOL • DIISOSTEARYL MALATE • GLYCERYL DIISOSTEARATE • HELIANTHUS ANNUUS ( SUNFLOWER ) SEED OIL • ROSMARINUS OFFICINALIS ( ROSEMARY ) LEAF EXTRACT • SODIUM DEHYDROACETATE • METHYLPARABEN • ETHYLPARABEN • PHENOXYETHANOL • FRAGRANCE • TITANIUM DIOXIDE • IRON OXIDES • MICA • RED 27 • Other information • protect this product in this container from excessive heat and direct sun .
