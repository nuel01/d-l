Warnings For external use only Do not use on : Broken skin , large areas of the skin .
Stop use and and ask a doctor : if irritation occurs or gets worse .

Directions Cleanse the Skin thoroughly before applying this product .
Cover the entire affected area with a thin layer and let dry 15 minutes then rinse well .
Use twice a week .
Because excessive drying of the skin may occur , start with one application , then gradually increase to two times a week if needed or as directed by a doctor .
If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs , reduce application or discontinue use .
