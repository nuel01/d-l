Active ingredient Benzalkonium Chloride 0 . 13 % Purpose Antibacterial Uses Helps eliminate bacteria on hands Warnnigs For external use only .
When using this product Avoid contact with eyes .
In case of contact , rinse thoroughly with water .
Stop using this product and ask doctor if irritation and redness develop and last .
Keep out of reach of children .
In case of accidental ingestion , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Directions Pump onto dry hands .
Work into a rich foamy lather , rinse thoroughly and dry .
