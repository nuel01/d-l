Drug Facts Active Ingredients Lidocaine 4 % Purpose Topical Anesthetic Uses : For the temporary relief of discomfort and pain associated with • minor burns and skin irritations • minor cuts and scrapes • itching .
Directions : Adults and children 12 years of age and older : Apply to affected area not more than 3 - 4 times daily .
Warnings : For external use only .
Avoid contact with the eyes .
Do not use in large quantities , particularly over raw or blistered areas .
Stop use and ask a doctor if • skin becomes irritated • condition worsens or sysmptoms last than 7 days • symptoms clear up and recur within a few days .
