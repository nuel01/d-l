Active ingredient Povidone iodine 10 % ( equivalent to 1 % titrateable iodine ) Purpose First aid antiseptic Uses • first aid antiseptic to help prevent infection in minor cuts , scrapes and burns Warnings For external use only Ask a doctor before use if you have • deep or puncture wounds • animal bites • serious burns Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens or persists for more than 72 hours • irritation and redness develops Keep out of reach of children .
