Drug Facts Active ingredients Menthol 10 % Methyl salicylate 15 % Purpose Topical analgesic Uses temporarily relieves minor pain associated with : • arthritis • simple backache • muscle strains • sprains • bruises • cramps Warnings For external use only Allergy alert : If prone to allergic reaction from aspirin or salicylates , consult a doctor before use .
When using this product • use only as directed • do not bandage tightly or use with a heating pad • avoid contact with eyes or mucous membranes • do not apply to wounds or damaged , broken or irritated skin Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition worsens • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days • redness is present • irritation develops If pregnant or breast - feeding , ask a health care professional before use .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
