Keep this and all drugs out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately .
Uses • In an emergency , to help flush loose foreign material or chemicals from the eye • To help relieve eye irritation , burning , itching , and stinging by removing air pollutants ( smog or pollen ) or chlorinated water Warnings For external use only When using this product • Remove contact lenses before use • Avoid contamination , do not touch tip of container to any surface • Do not reuse • Overuse may cause more eye redness Stop use and ask a doctor if • You experience eye pain • Changes in vision occur • Continued redness or irritation of the eye • Condition worsens or persists for more than 72 hours • You experience any open wounds in or near the eye Do not use • If solution changes color or becomes cloudy Directions • Flush the affected eye as needed , controlling the rate of flow of solution by applying pressure on the bottle Other Information • Store at 20 ° - 25 ° C ( 68 ° - 77 ° F ) • Do not use if imprinted seal on cap is torn , broken or missing .
Inactive Ingredients Boric acid , sodium borate , sodium chloride .
