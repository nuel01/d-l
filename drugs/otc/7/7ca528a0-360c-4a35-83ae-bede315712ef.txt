Pain & Muscle Relief Uses : For the temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints .
For External Use only .
For use by adults only .
Do not use otherwise than as directed .
Do not use on wounds or broken skin .
Do not use if known sensitivity to NeuroMAX exists , or if allergic to salicylate or any ingredients of this product If pregnant or breastfeeding , ask a healthcare provider before using this product When using this product avoid contact with the eyes or mucous membranes .
Do not bandage tightly .
Stop use and ask a doctor if skin irritation occurs or gets worse , symptoms persist for more than 7 days , or symptoms clear up and occur within a few days .
Keep out of reach of children .
