Active ingredient Calcium Carbonate USP 750 mg Purpose Antacid Uses relieves : • heartburn • sour stomach • acid indigestion • upset stomach associated with these symptoms Warnings Ask a doctor or pharmacist before use if you are taking a prescription drug .

When using this product • do not take more than 10 tablets in 24 hours • if pregnant do not take more than 6 tablets in 24 hours • do not use the maximum dosage for more than 2 weeks except under the advice and supervision of a doctor .
