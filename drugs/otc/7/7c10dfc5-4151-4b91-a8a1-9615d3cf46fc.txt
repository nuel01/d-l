When using this product • marked drowsiness may occur • sedatives and tranquilizers may increase drowsiness • excitability may occur , especially in children Keep out of reach of children .
In case of overdose , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center ( 1 - 800 - 222 - 1222 ) right away .
