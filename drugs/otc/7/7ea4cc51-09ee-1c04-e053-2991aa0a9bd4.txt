Active ingredient Alcohol 67 . 6 % Purpose Skin Antiseptic Uses For hands to decrease bacteria on the skin .
Warnings Flammable .
Keep away from fire or flame .
For external use only .
When using this product Do not use in the eyes .
In case of contact , immediately flush with water .
Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation and redness develop .
If condition persists for more than 72 hours , consult a doctor .
Keep out of the reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Use in well ventilated area .
Directions Wet hands thoroughly with spray , covering entire surface of hands , including fingertips , cuticles , wrist and forearms .
Rub vigorously into skin until dry .
No rinsing or toweling required .
