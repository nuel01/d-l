Drug Facts Active Ingredient Ethyl Alcohol 70 % Purpose Antimicrobial Use • For hand sanitizing to decrease bacteria on the skin • Recommended for repeated use Warnings • For external use only .
• When using this product avoid contact with eyes .
In case of eye contact , flush eyes with water .
• Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation or redness develops , or if condition persists for more than 72 hours .
• Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • Pump a small amount of liquid into palm of hand .
• Rub thoroughly over all surfaces of both hands .
• Rub hands together briskly until dry .
