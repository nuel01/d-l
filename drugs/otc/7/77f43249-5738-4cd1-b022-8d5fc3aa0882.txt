Active ingredients Diphenhydramine HCl 2 % Zinc Acetate 0 . 1 % Purpose External analgesic Skin protectant Uses ■ for the temporary relief of pain and itching associated with : ■ insect bites ■ minor skin irritations ■ sunburn ■ minor burns ■ minor cuts ■ scrapes ■ rashes due to poison : ■ ivy ■ oak ■ sumac ■ dries the oozing and weeping of poison : ■ ivy ■ oak ■ sumac Warnings For external use only Flammable .
Keep away from fire of flame .
Do not use • on large areas of the body • with any other product containing diphenhydramine , even one taken by mouth Ask a doctor before use on chicken pox or measles When using this product do not get into eyes Stop use and ask a doctor if condition worsens or symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
