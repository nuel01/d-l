PURPOSE : nasal decongestant Uses : Temporarily relieves nasal congestion due to the common cold , hay fever or other upper respiratory allergies and nasal congestion associated with sinusitis Temporarily relieves sinus congestion and pressure DIRECTIONS : Take every 4 hours ; Do not take more than 6 doses in 24 hours ; Adults and children 12 years of age and over ; 1 tablet Children under 12 years of age ; ask a doctor WARNINGS ; Do not use if you are taking a prescription monoamine oxidase inhibitor ( MAOI ) ( certain drugs for depression , psychiatric or emotional conditions , or Parkinson ' s disease ) or for 2 weeks after stopping the MAOI drug .
If you do not know if your prescription drug contains an MAOI , ask a doctor or pharmacist before taking this product .
