Drug Facts Active ingredient ( each gram contains ) Bacitracin Zinc 500 units Purpose First aid antibiotic Uses first aid to help prevent infection in • minor cuts • scrapes • burns Warnings For external use only Do not use • in the eyes • over large areas of the body • if you are allergic to any of the ingredients • longer than 1 week unless directed by a doctor Ask a doctor before use • on deep or puncture wounds , animal bites , or serious burns Stop use and ask a doctor if • condition persists or gets worse • a rash or other allergic reaction develops Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions • clean the affected area and dry thoroughly • apply a small amount of this product ( an amount equal to the surface area of the tip of a finger ) on the area 1 to 3 times daily • may be covered with a sterile bandage Other information • To open : unscrew cap , pull tab to remove foil seal • Store at 20 ° - 25 ° C ( 68 ° - 77 ° F ) • see carton or tube crimp for lot number and expiration date Inactive ingredients mineral oil , white petrolatum Questions ?
