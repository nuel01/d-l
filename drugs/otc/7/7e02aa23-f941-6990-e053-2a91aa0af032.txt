Active Ingredient Povidone Iodine USP , 10 % w / v ( equivalent to 1 % titratable iodine ) Purpose Antiseptic Uses • Health - care antiseptic for preparation of the skin prior to surgery • First aid antiseptic to help prevent the risk of skin infection in minor cuts , scrapes and burns Warnings For external use only .
Do not use or apply in the eyes • on individuals who are allergic or sensitive to iodine • over large areas of the body • as a first aid antiseptic longer than 1 week unless directed by a doctor • Discontinue use if irritation and redness develop .
Stop use and consult a doctor if the condition persists or gets worse .
Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed , get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away .
Directions For preparation of the skin prior to surgery clean the affected area , remove swab by the stick , apply to the operative site prior to surgery .
For use as a first aid antiseptic clean the affected area , apply a small amount of this product on the area 1 - 3 times daily .
May be covered with a sterile bandage .
If bandaged , let dry first .
