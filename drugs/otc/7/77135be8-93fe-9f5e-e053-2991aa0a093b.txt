Drug Facts Active Ingredient Menthol 1 percent Purpose Pain relieving gel Uses : For the temporary relief of minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with simple backache , arthritis , strains , bruises , sprains Warnings For external use only .
When using this product • avoid contact with the eyes • do not handage tightly • do not apply to wounds or damaged skin • do not use with heating pads or other heating devices Stop use and ask doctor if condition worsens , or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again with a few days .
( see other side panel ) Keep out of reach of children .
If swallowed get medical help or contact a Posion Control Center immediately .
Directions adults and children 2 years of age and older : apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily children under 2 years of age : do not use , consult a doctor Other Information Do Not Freeze Inactive Ingredients carbomer , isopropyl alcohol , nonyl phenyl polyoxyethylene ether , camphor , kathon CG , FD & C blue no . 1 , triethanolamine , water [ MULTIMEDIA ] [ MULTIMEDIA ]
